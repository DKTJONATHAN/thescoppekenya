## Galaxy UNPACKED 2026 | Samsung Africa Gears Up for Major Reveal

Samsung Africa is set to host its highly anticipated Galaxy UNPACKED 2026 event on February 25, 2026, at 8 PM CAT, livestreaming on samsung.com. The event promises to unveil the next generation of Galaxy S Series smartphones and new Galaxy Buds, marking a significant moment for mobile technology enthusiasts across the continent.

### What to Expect from Galaxy UNPACKED 2026

The upcoming Samsung Galaxy UNPACKED 2026 event will serve as the global stage for the introduction of the new Galaxy S Series smartphones, anticipated to be the Galaxy S26 lineup, alongside a new generation of Galaxy Buds, likely the Buds 4 series. Samsung has confirmed that these devices will feature hardware innovation and optimised software, aiming to be the most impressive smartphones yet. The official launch date for South Africa, including pre-order windows and retail availability, will be announced during the event, with pre-orders commencing immediately after the livestream concludes.

Viewers in Africa can tune in to the livestream via Samsung.com, Samsung Newsroom, or the company's official YouTube channel to witness these advancements firsthand. Participants who register for the event also stand a chance to win a Galaxy Watch8, Buds3 Pro, or a SUPERSIZE 98" Crystal UHD TV. It's noteworthy that the Galaxy Watch8 was officially announced on July 9, 2025, and the Galaxy Buds3 Pro were released on July 24, 2024, indicating these are established products offered as part of the promotional activities.

### The Power of Galaxy AI and One UI 7.0 in Africa

A central theme for the new Galaxy S Series will be the enhanced integration of Galaxy AI and One UI 7.0. Users in Africa can anticipate personalised recommendations, AI-powered multitasking, smart photo editing, and seamless device integration, all designed to enhance productivity and ensure an intuitive interface across Galaxy smartphones, tablets, and wearables. Galaxy AI, initially launched with the Galaxy S24 series in January 2024, is powered by Google's Gemini and Imagen 2 technologies. The Galaxy UNPACKED 2026 event is expected to showcase improved on-device generative AI capabilities, faster text-to-image processing, and smarter battery optimisation. Samsung is also expanding Galaxy AI with a new multi-agent approach, integrating Perplexity as an additional AI agent to improve contextual assistance across devices.

The One UI 7.0 interface, which saw its official release on February 7, 2025, with the Galaxy S25 series and rolled out to other flagships from April 7, 2025, promises a bold new design for greater personalisation. This update, based on Android 15, brings a simplified home screen, redesigned widgets, and advanced lock screen customisation. These software advancements are crucial as the digital economy continues to evolve, making the user experience more fluid and responsive.
