## Scoop Ni Gani?
Mabeshte, kimeumana kwa box office huku filamu ya "Wuthering Heights" ikiweka rekodi mpya za mapenzi, ikiongoza chati na kuacha zingine zikishika mkia. Movie hiyo, steamy romantic drama na Margot Robbie na Jacob Elordi, imetengeneza balaa kubwa sana, haswa over Valentine’s Day weekend. Imepiga $34.8 million hapa North America na inatabiriwa ifike $40 million hadi President’s Day. Lakini ngori ni kwamba, internationally imefanya poa zaidi na $42 million, ikileta total global ya $82 million. Budget ya kutengeneza ilikuwa $80 million, so inategemea audiences za nje kuilipia.

Emerald Fennell, yule dame alifanya "Promising Young Woman" na "Saltburn," ndiye ameandika na ku-direct hii. Netflix walikuwa wanataka kulipia $150 million, lakini yeye na producers, including Robbie, walichagua Warner Bros ili ipate theatrical release na marketing campaign safi. Na hiyo strategy ime-pay off, Warner Bros wamepiga No. 1 mara tisa mfululizo.
Lakini si zote zimeenda shule. "Crime 101" na Chris Hemsworth, Mark Ruffalo na Barry Keoghan, imejitukana vibaya sana. Imefika No. 3 tu, na imefanya vibaya sana compared na budget yake.

## Evidence Ni?
"Wuthering Heights" ilikuwa de facto choice kwa madem wengi around Valentine's Day, more than 75% ya walioenda kuona walikuwa wanawake. Walipatia "B" grade kwa CinemaScore, so bado hatujui kama word-of-mouth itaisaidia kudunda. Ilichukua kiti ikiwa juu ya zingine kama animated sports adventure "GOAT" na hiyo "Crime 101" iliyoshika mkia.

"GOAT" ilifungua second na $26 million, na inatabiriwa ifike $32 million. Globally imepiga $47.6 million. Sony Pictures Animation walitengeneza hii na $80 million. Audis walipatia "A" grade kwa CinemaScore, na hii inamaanisha inaweza kudumu kwa sinema.

Sasa tuchukue "Crime 101." Ilifungua third na $15.1 million over the weekend, na wanapredict ifike $17.7 million. Internationally imekusanya $12 million. Hii ingekuwa poa kwa R-rated film, lakini Amazon MGM walitumia $90 million kutengeneza! Theater owners wanachukua karibu half ya ticket sales, so "Crime 101" inahitaji kukaa kwa sinema long after opening weekend ndio iweze kufika bei. Reviews zilikuwa poa, lakini moviegoers walikuwa mixed, walipatia "B" CinemaScore.
Amazon MGM wanasema ati "Crime 101" haihitaji kupiga juggernaut kwa box office ndio iwe success. Eti they just want to earn back marketing na distribution spend na wanataka theatrical release i-elevate launch kwa Prime Video. Watu wengi kwa industry wanaona hii kama explanation ya kujificha, haswa na budget kama hii.

"Send Help," horror film, ilishuka fourth na $9 million. Disney imepiga $1 billion mark global box office 2026. "Solo Mio" ilikuwa fifth na $6.2 million.

## Verdict Yetu Ni
Rada ni kwamba "Wuthering Heights" imetengeneza kitu! Inaonyesha bado kuna nguvu kwa classic stories zikipatiwa touch mpya na wasanii wako rada. Warner Bros wamecheza smart kukataa pesa ya Netflix na kuenda theatrical, na imewalipia. Hii inatupa kichwa kwamba sinema bado ina flow, na watu wanataka ku-experience movie kwa big screen.

Lakini "Crime 101" ni lesson kwa industry. Hata kama una majina makubwa na budget kubwa, kama rada haiko sawa, unaweza kukula hasara. Ile story ya Amazon MGM ati hawatafuti box office ni ka wanataka kujificha nyuma ya vidole, lakini mtaa hauko hivi. Pesa nyingi imetumika, na results hazionyeshi value. Ni kama wamecheza chini ya maji, na hiyo haipendi. Itabidi wafanye math safi next time.
