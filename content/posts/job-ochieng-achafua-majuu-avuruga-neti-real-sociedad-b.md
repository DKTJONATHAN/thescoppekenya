**The Scoop**
Tumenusa hii story na hatuwezi nyamaza. Harambee Stars wetu, Job Ochieng, ameweka Spain kwa rada yake vibaya sana! Maboys wanasema ameachia Real Sociedad B goli ya kunyandua, na hiyo si mchezo. Hadi tunajiuliza, huyu msee ana form gani?

**The Evidence**
Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, Monday jioni, Ochieng alichafua kwa game ya Real Sociedad B dhidi ya Malaga. Walikuwa wametoka sare ya 1-1, alafu huyu msee wetu akaingia, akapiga kichwa ikazama neti! 68th minute, ball imeingia na deflections kadhaa, na hapo ndio Sociedad B walipata win ya 2-1. Hii si mara ya kwanza msee wetu anachafua, amekuwa akitoka bench na kuweka form. Sources wanasema ameshakwisha piga magoli tatu na assists mbili kwa mechi 18 za Sociedad B this season. Hii form ndio imemfanya akue promoted hadi senior team na hata ameshacheza La Liga na Copa del Rey. Yani, msee ameshika ball hadi Benni McCarthy na Nacho Monreal wamemchafua. **The Verdict**
So rada ni kwamba, Job Ochieng hana mchezo. Ameweka bendera ya Kenya juu huko Spain na bado anapeleka ball mbele. Hii ni kumaanisha, huyu msee ni yule mmoja wetu, ananyandua majuu. Ata kama alikuwa bench kwa game ya senior team dhidi ya Real Madrid, akirudi kwa second string, bado anachafua. Form yake ni fire, na tunasema tu aendelee hivyo huko!
