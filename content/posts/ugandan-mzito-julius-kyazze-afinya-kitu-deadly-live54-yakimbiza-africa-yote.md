## Scoop Ni Gani?
Wasee, tumenusa hii story kali saa hii inawasha mtaa! Inspecta wetu wameconfirm mzinga mpya imedungwa na mogul mmoja kutoka Uganda, anaitwa Julius Kyazze. Huyu msee ni kama **Elchapo** wa creative game, amefinya kitu inaitwa Live54+, ati ni Pan-African creative holding structure itacoordinate media, entertainment na marketing businesses across six African markets. Imagine, ni kama wanataka kushika kila kitu kuanzia content creation, experiential marketing, talent management hadi radio na digital marketing. Fom ni kule Nairobi ndio itakuwa hub yao kubwa, lakini operations ziko Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Ghana, na Burundi.

## Verdict Yetu Ni
Hii kitu ya Live54+ inaleta economies of scale kwa procurement, production, na distribution, na itasaidia sana kwa governance na reporting frameworks, vitu ambazo hapo awali zilikuwa zinatorosha **mkuchu** (pesa) za wawekezaji. Nairobi inazidi kujitenga kama business center ya region, huku Kampala ikiendelea kushika kwa music na events. Hivyo basi, Kyazze anataka ku-link hizi ecosystems zote, Live54+ itashika clients wanaotaka regional execution bila kutegemea global agency networks. Analysts wanasema creative economy ya Africa inaprojectiwa kutoa mamia ya billions of dollars kwa miaka ijayo, na **wasee wataomoka** sana. Lakini, changamoto ni capital constraints, fragmented markets, na limited cross-border coordination. Live54+ inataka kuziba hizi gaps kupitia integration na shared infrastructure. Kama watafanikiwa ku-attract investments na ku-inspire consolidation ingine kwa East African Community, hiyo itategemea jinsi wata-execute na transparency yao. Lakini, ujio wao ni sign kubwa ya kusema African-owned groups zinazidi kulenga continental scale. Hii si mchezo, wasee, hapa kazi tu!
