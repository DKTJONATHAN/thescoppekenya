## Tragic Collision on Valley Road Claims One Life A city shuttle bus lost control and collided with multiple vehicles on Valley Road this Monday morning, March 9, 2026, resulting in one fatality and three injuries. The incident, near the Pan-Afric Hotel, caused significant traffic disruption as emergency services responded to the scene.

Incident Details The bus reportedly experienced brake failure, veering off the road and striking oncoming traffic. Images and videos obtained by Kenyans.co.ke show extensive damage to the left front side of the bus. Witnesses confirmed that three vehicles were hit, sustaining severe damage, and a motorbike rider was dragged a considerable distance. One person was pronounced deceased, while three others are receiving medical attention for their injuries.

Emergency Response and Ongoing Investigation Police and emergency responders quickly arrived on-site to conduct rescue operations and manage the resulting traffic congestion. The involved bus has since been towed from the scene to facilitate a thorough investigation into the precise cause of the accident. Authorities are working to determine all contributing factors.

Prior Road Incident in Kisumu This incident follows another serious road accident reported on Sunday, March 8, 2026, in the Otonglo area of Kisumu County. That crash resulted in three deaths and 19 injuries. The Ministry of Health confirmed the casualties, denying earlier allegations that a vehicle from the Health Cabinet Secretary's motorcade was directly involved. The Ministry clarified that Health CS Aden Duale encountered the accident while traveling from Rarieda to Kisumu International Airport and immediately mobilized an emergency medical response to aid the victims at the scene.
