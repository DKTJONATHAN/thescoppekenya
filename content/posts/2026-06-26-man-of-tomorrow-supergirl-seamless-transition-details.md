Alcock’s portrayal of Kara Zor-El anchors much of this unfolding drama. ### Supergirl's Cinematic Arc

This personal moment is key to understanding their bond and how it will inform their actions in the battles ahead. It's a character-driven approach that ensures the epic scale of the universe never overshadows the human, or rather, Kryptonian, element.

Alcock will join a robust ensemble cast in *Man of Tomorrow*. David Corenswet returns as Superman, alongside Rachel Brosnahan as the intrepid Lois Lane. The star-studded lineup also includes Lars Eidinger, Aaron Pierre, Frank Grillo, Skyler Gisondo, Sara Sampaio, Isabela Merced, Nathan Fillion, Edi Gathegi, Adria Arjona, Matthew Lillard, Sinqua Walls, and even Howard Stern.
