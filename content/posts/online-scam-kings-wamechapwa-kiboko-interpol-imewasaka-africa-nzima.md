## Scoop Ni Gani?
Yo wadau! The Nairobi Street Insider tumenusa hii story noma imetoka directly kwa ma-dreadi wa global, Interpol. Apparently, wame-run operation noma inaitwa Red Card 2.0, na imekuwa kichapo kwa ma-scammer wa online across Africa. Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, wazito 651 wame-kamata, na pesa ngumu $4.3 million zime-patikana! Hii ni noma, inamaanisha ma-hustler feki wame-pata kichapo cha mbwa.

## Evidence Ni?
Receipts ziko clear, wadau. Hii operation ilikuwa eight weeks, kuanzia December 8, 2025 hadi January 30, 2026. Ilikuwa inachapa ma-scam za high-yield investment, mobile money fraud, na zile loan app za kunyofoa. Imagine, wame-expose deal za losses za *over* $45 million na wame-identify 1,247 victims, wengi wao ni wa hapa Africa na wengine kutoka global. Gadgets 2,341 zime-shikwa, na IPs, domains na servers 1,442 za uhuni zime-zimishwa. Neal Jetton, Director wa Cybercrime kwa Interpol, amesema ma-syndicate hawa wanachapa watu financially na psychologically na madeni zao za ukora.

Kwa Naija, ma-spona walichapa plans za network ya 419 people through fake investment schemes. Pia, wame-delete more than 1000 social media accounts za ukora na kupata building ilikuwa base yao ya uhalifu. Kuna case ingine kwa Naija, ma-dreadi walikamata six members wa syndicate walikuwa wame-penetrate systems za telco na msaada wa worker aliyekuwa ame-compromise, wakaiba airtime na data ku-uza illegally.

Hapa Ke, ma-law enforcement agents walikamata more than 20 suspects walikuwa linked na fraud schemes. Hawa walikuwa wanatumia WhatsApp, IG, TikTok na testimony za uongo ku-lure victims ku-invest kwa makampuni noma za global na madeni ya returns noma. Inspecta wetu wame-verify, wame-shika gadgets kibao walikuwa wanatumia ku-run hii deal haramu. Hadi Cote d'Ivoire, zaidi ya 50 suspects walikamata, na simu 240, laptops 25 na SIM cards zaidi ya 300 zime-shikwa kwa operation dhidi ya mobile loan fraud. Hawa walikuwa wanatoa loan za haraka, alafu wanapiga watu fees, wanatumia debt-collection practices chafu na ku-iba data sensitive za kibinafsi na pesa.

Hii story inakumbusha vile Erico alishtuka baada ya safari ya Mombasa. Ilikuwa "Mwili Ilishtuka Baada ya Safari ya Mombasa, Sisi Kwa Sisi Groundbreaking Ni True!" just like vile ma-victims wa hizi scams wanapatana na kichapo. ## Verdict Yetu Ni
Kwetu sisi kama The Nairobi Street Insider, hii ni warning kali kwa ma-scammer wote wa online. Rada ya Interpol iko on point, na wame-onyesha hawacheki na online fraud. Hadi wazito wetu wa hapa Ke wame-kamata watu, so usifikirie uta-escape. Kama unapanga kumangulia watu online, ujue siku zako zimehesabika. Na kwa wazito wote, be vigilant! Hawa watu wana-come na strategies noma, so usikubali ku-lala. Pesa za hard earned money usikubali ku-cheza na ma-fraudsters.
