## Apprentice Reboot: Donald Trump Jr. Eyed as Host, Dad Gives a 'Soft' Nod

Whispers are swirling about Amazon considering a revival of The Apprentice, with Donald Trump Jr. potentially stepping into his father's iconic boardroom shoes. While the former President offered a rather lukewarm endorsement for his eldest son's hosting prospects, Amazon itself remains coy, labeling all such talks "purely speculative" for now.

### The Trump Dynasty's Reality TV Return?

The prospect of Donald Trump Jr. hosting a new iteration of The Apprentice has certainly piqued interest. Donald Trump Sr., who helmed the hit NBC show for a staggering 14 seasons from 2004 to 2015, acknowledged the rumors. "He's a good guy, he'd probably be good," President Trump remarked about his son, adding, "He's got a little charisma going. You need a little charisma for that sucker." This "soft endorsement" comes after Trump Jr. himself was a fixture on the original series, serving as a "board member" and helping his father decide who would hear the infamous "You're fired!"

The original series saw contestants compete for a job under Trump Sr., becoming a pop culture phenomenon. Following Trump Sr.'s controversial comments about immigrants during his 2015 presidential campaign, NBC severed ties, declaring they would not put him back on the network. Arnold Schwarzenegger briefly took over for the show's final season. Despite the past network drama, the Mark Burnett-created franchise continues to generate substantial income, with Trump Sr.'s company, Trump Productions, reportedly receiving royalties between $100,000 and $1,000,000 in 2024. Amazon shares ownership of the franchise with Trump's company.

### Amazon's Apprentice Ambitions - Or Lack Thereof?

While a report from The Wall Street Journal ignited the reboot chatter, an Amazon spokesperson quickly moved to temper expectations. Speaking to Entertainment Weekly, the spokesperson confirmed "preliminary internal discussions about what's next for The Apprentice as a property" since their acquisition of MGM. However, they firmly stated that a reboot is not in "active development" and any reports on specific details or potential hosts are "purely speculative."

Despite the official caution, Amazon's existing relationship with the Trump family adds another layer to this developing story. The company reportedly paid $40 million for a recent documentary centered on former first lady Melania Trump. This existing partnership could certainly pave a smoother path if Amazon ever decides to move beyond "preliminary discussions" and actively revive The Apprentice with a Trump in the lead. For now, we'll keep an eye on this space, ready for the next boardroom announcement.
