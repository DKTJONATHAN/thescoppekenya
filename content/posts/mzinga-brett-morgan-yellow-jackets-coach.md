## Scoop Ni Gani?
Kumbe zile story zilitrend wiki jana kuhusu John Carr kuhepa kwenda Cleveland Browns zilikua true! Sasa, Yellow Jacket Football ya Starkville Oktibbeha Consolidated School District imepata mzinga mpya. Wameingia box na Brett Morgan, yule mcoach kinyama alikua West Point High School. Hii ni mbichwa kweli, maana Morgan amekuwa West Point for 17 years, akipiga kazi kama assistant, offensive coordinator, alafu akakuwa head coach for two seasons. Si mchezo! Hii move inaleta maswali mob mtaani, ju ni kama Harambee Stars captain alipotea Europe akapatikana Kilimani. Fom ni gani sasa, ama ni kutafuta rada ingine? Anyway, this one ni official, Morgan ameanza kazi.

## Evidence Ni?
Huyu morio, Brett Morgan, si mgeni kwa game. Amepiga coaching for 20 years huko Mississippi. Before kuja Starkville, alikuwa graduate assistant Mississippi State University for three years alafu akaenda West Point 17 years. Cheki rekodi yake ni heavy: akiwa West Point, mbogi yake ilichukua 11 district championships na eight 5A state titles, na walicheza 12 state championship title games. Akiwa head coach, alikuwa na 24-4 record, na 2025 team yake ilimaliza 13-1, walibant tu kwa championship game. Jay Hopson, yule buda mkubwa wa Jacket Athletics, amesema walitaka msee wa hapo area, anajua fom ya Starkville, na Morgan ndiye Elchapo. Anasema Morgan ataleta ‘physical style of play’ na ‘discipline’ kwa Yellow Jackets. Morgan mwenyewe alisema alikuwa "blessed" na West Point lakini "excited" kuhusu Starkville. Anasema program yao itaanza na Christ na watafanya maboys wakuwa mabwana. Hii ni rada mzito.

## Verdict Yetu Ni
Kutoka vile tumedukua dukuduku, inaonekana Starkville wamepata elchapo wa kazi. Morgan ana pedigree, ame-win state championships, na ana drive ya kufanya maboys wa field wakuwa wazee wa maana. Huyu si mcoach wa kawaida, ni msee ana imani na vision. Siku zombo atakuwa anapray na team yake, akijenga culture ya team first na ubingwa mtaani. Expect Yellow Jackets kupiga kichapo kinyama under this new leadership. Mambo ni set, na tunaona hii ni mwanzo wa erra mpya ya ushindi kwa Jacket Athletics.
