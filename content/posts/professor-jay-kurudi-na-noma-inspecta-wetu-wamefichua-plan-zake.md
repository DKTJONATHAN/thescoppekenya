## Scoop Ni Gani?
Wasee wa ngoma, tumenusa hii story ya kimeumana from Tanzania! Veteran wa Bongo Flava, Joseph Haule, anajulikana kama Professor Jay, ameconfrim kurudi stage na anasema yuko ready kureclaim kiti chake kwa industry ya muziki Tanzania. This man is set to headline the fourth season launch ya Bongo Flava Honours on May 1. Hii event, managed na Deiwaka World, itakuwa ndio shoo yake ya kwanza after kukaa nje ya rada for a while.

## Evidence Ni?
Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, receipts ziko hapa! Professor Jay, akiongea Tuesday, February 17, 2026, during the official announcement ya new season, alireveal alikuwa amestep away from the spotlight juu ya challenges mob, ikiwemo health concerns. Watu wanasema alikuwa ICU 127 days akipambana na kidney na liver complications, si mchezo! Lakini sasa amerudi na nguvu mpya na anaprepare one of the biggest performances za career yake, designed kuremark his return na kuconnect na mafans wake. Alisema, “Professor Jay wa ‘Mitulinga’ is now fit. I am taking fans from where I started in Bongo Flava to where I stopped. Many artistes I have worked with will be present to show love. That day will be more than just a show.” Hii Bongo Flava Honours imekuwa ikiheshimu ma-legend wa Bongo Flava kwa miaka kadhaa, na wasanii kama Dully Sykes na Mr. Blue wameshapewa heshima zao hapo. Hata Joseph Mbilinyi 'Sugu', pia legend wa Hip Hop, ndiye mastermind wa hii kitu. ## Verdict Yetu Ni
Mtaani tunasema, hii si tu show, ni statement. Professor Jay, yule former Mikumi MP na pioneer wa Bongo Flava, kurudi kwake kuta-redefine legacy yake na ku-reassert presence yake kwa music scene ya Tanzania. Mafans wametii vibaya sana na wanasubiri kuona legend akirudi na mikito mpya. Kama amepiga pasi challenges kama hizo na amerudi fiti, basi hii comeback itakuwa ya kinyama. The Nairobi Street Insider anasema, wacha tungoje May 1st, tuone kama Professor Jay atapindua meza kweli ama ni mambo ya makelele tu!
