Mugithi sensation DJ Fatxo recently shared a deeply personal moment with fans, appealing for prayers for his ailing mother. On Thursday, March 19, 2026, he posted a heartfelt video on Facebook, presenting his frail mother with flowers as she battles illness, sparking an outpouring of support from well-wishers across Kenya.

DJ Fatxo's Emotional Appeal for His Mother

A Son's Love and Silent Battles The video captured his mother, initially appearing frail, her face gradually brightening into a gentle smile as she accepted the bouquet. She offered soft reassurances to her son, a moment that visibly uplifted DJ Fatxo's spirits despite the difficult circumstances. Reflecting on the profound experience, the singer spoke about the often-unseen struggles many individuals endure, specifically calling upon God to grant healing to his mother. "The battles we fight silently- May the good Lord heal you, mum," he wrote, echoing the sentiments of many facing similar family health challenges.

Finding Solace Amidst Public Scrutiny In a separate, candid revelation, DJ Fatxo openly addressed the emotional toll of incessant online criticism. This led him to seek spiritual comfort at powerful sermons delivered by gospel minister Sarah K at Nairobi Cinema. The artiste recounted attending the service alone, a rare departure from his usual routine and an experience he had not undertaken in years. His visit also marked his return to Nairobi's central business district after a considerable absence, where he initially admitted struggling to find his way around. Upon arrival, he expressed surprise at the massive turnout, noting the extensive queues of worshippers eager to enter the venue-a sight he remarked he had not witnessed in the city centre for years. Despite not possessing a ticket, a compassionate security guard and fellow attendees who recognized him graciously facilitated his entry, allowing him to join the uplifting service. The artiste described the entire experience as both unexpected and deeply uplifting, affirming his ongoing pursuit of strength through faith while navigating personal challenges.
