## The Decline of Parliamentary Standards

### Vulgar Rhetoric in Government
The controversy stems from the use of crude double entendres intended to describe political positioning within the administration. By utilizing phrases that imply sexual favors in exchange for political influence, these legislators have faced accusations of debasing the sanctity of the National Assembly. Critics contend that such language is fundamentally inappropriate for public officials and serves to undermine the authority of the legislative branch.

### Prioritizing Spectacle Over Policy
A significant critique of these lawmakers is their apparent abandonment of a serious policy agenda. Observers note that instead of addressing pressing economic concerns or legislative reforms, these representatives have shifted their focus to inflammatory and base rhetoric. This trend suggests a troubling environment where political relevance is measured by an individual's ability to engage in provocative discourse rather than their capacity to draft meaningful legislation or provide effective constituency representation.

### Models of Professionalism
In contrast to those utilizing offensive language, other female leaders are being highlighted for their sober and objective approach to governance. Legislators such as Njeri Maina, Faith Odhiambo, and Crystal Asige serve as examples of professionalism, maintaining a focus on substantive public service rather than theatrics. Their conduct underscores a growing divide in the legislature between those committed to institutional integrity and those contributing to the degradation of political discourse.

### Impact on Future Generations
Beyond the immediate political fallout, there is deep concern regarding the influence of this behavior on the next generation of women. By normalizing vulgarity within the halls of government, these leaders are criticized for providing poor role models for young girls. Many argue that this conduct suggests that influence is a product of manipulation and lewdness rather than intellect or hard work, ultimately jeopardizing the morale and aspirations of the nation's youth.
