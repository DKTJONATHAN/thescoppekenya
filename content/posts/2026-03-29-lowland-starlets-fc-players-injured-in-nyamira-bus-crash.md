Players from Nyamira's Lowland Starlets FC were involved in a road accident on the Chepilat-Chabera Highway in Nyamira County this morning, March 29, 2026. At least ten players sustained injuries and are receiving emergency treatment at Kaplong Hospital, following the incident as the team travelled to Bunyore for a league fixture.

Accident Details Emerge

Medical Response at Kaplong Hospital Emergency medical personnel at Kaplong Hospital are currently treating ten Lowland Starlets FC players. The athletes were quickly transported to the facility after their team bus crashed while en route to a scheduled league fixture in Bunyore. Medical staff are providing urgent care for their injuries.

Scene of the Crash Images obtained by Za Ndani depict the immediate aftermath of the accident. A school bus was observed overturned on a foggy road, with personal belongings scattered nearby as onlookers approached cautiously. Further visuals show the damaged vehicle upright by the roadside, where responders and local residents were inspecting the scene. A tractor was also present, assisting in the misty conditions.

Investigation Initiated The exact cause of the accident, which occurred on the morning of March 29, 2026, along the Chepilat-Chabera Highway, remains under investigation. Authorities have not yet released details regarding the factors that led to the incident.

Nationwide Support for Lowland Starlets Following news of the crash, football teams within Lowland Starlets FC's league and individuals across the country have conveyed heartfelt messages of support to the injured players and the club.
