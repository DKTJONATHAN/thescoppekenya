## Dorcas Rigathi Electrifies Interview, Dismissing "Namkalia" Rumours Pastor Dorcas Rigathi recently set the record straight on persistent whispers that she dominates her husband, former Deputy President Rigathi Gachagua. Speaking candidly on Oga Obinna TV this past Sunday, the former Second Lady clarified the true nature of their partnership, describing the widespread claims as exaggerated and a complete misunderstanding of their shared journey.

Unpacking the "Namkalia" Narrative The buzz surrounding Dorcas Rigathi's prominent presence alongside her husband during his political and official duties has been undeniable, with many speculating she holds undue influence. However, she firmly rejected the "namkalia" narrative, stating her appearances are purely at her husband’s request and a demonstration of unwavering support. "Sometimes you see me there, it’s because he asked me to be there. I support my husband fully," she explained, cutting through the gossip surrounding their dynamic.

A Foundation of Shared Vision and Wealth Beyond addressing public curiosity about their present relationship, Dorcas offered insight into the foundations of their achievements and wealth. She revealed that their life's successes are the culmination of decades of joint planning and strategic effort. Emphasizing her active role, she underscored that she was a full participant in establishing their estate from the very beginning, not merely a passive observer. This deep involvement, she suggested, explains her clear understanding of their journey together. Independent Contributions and Unwavering Advocacy While her visibility certainly soared during Rigathi Gachagua’s tenure as Deputy President from 2022 until his impeachment in late 2024, Dorcas was quick to remind everyone of her distinct contributions. As Second Lady, she championed critical causes, including the "boy child" initiative, disability inclusion, and the welfare of widows and orphans-work she passionately continues through her ministry in Mathira. These efforts, she clarified, reflect her inherent values and shared commitments with her husband, rather than any internal power struggle.

Despite the ongoing chatter, her recent interview firmly reiterates her consistent stance: their relationship is built on mutual support and a shared history, not on one partner dominating the other.
