Wasee, listen up! The streets are buzzing, na Lindsey Vonn ameendelea kutushangaza kwa hizi 2026 Winter Olympics kule Milan-Cortina. Jana, alishuka mlima na ACL yake imerupture, na leo, Feb 7, 2026, amefanya tena, na si mchezo!

Huyu mama wa miaka 41, despite having a 100% *gone* ACL kwa goti yake ya kushoto- si eti 80% ama 50%, no, *kabisa*- amepiga training run yake ya pili ya downhill na kuweka time ya top-3! Imagine! Ameshave zaidi ya sekunde mbili kutoka time yake ya Ijumaa, akitupatia 1:38.12. Ametrail tu Kira Weidle-Winkelmann wa Germany na teammate wake Breezy Johnson, ambaye ndiye alikuwa fastest.

Kocha wake, Aksel Lund Svindal, alikuwa anasema ati Lindsey alikuwa "very calm" baada ya run yake na hakusema chochote kuhusu goti. Hiyo inamaanisha amejichunga tu, anajua anachofanya. Lakini pia amesema Vonn atahitaji kushika kasi zaidi kesho, coz it's the real deal- the Olympic downhill medal event.

Remember, huyu si mgeni kwa hizi vitu. Alirudi kwa ski racing last season baada ya sita years za retirement na hata akapata partial titanium replacement kwa goti yake ya kulia 2024. Alikana kabisa kukunja mkia baada ya hiyo crash ya Jan 30 iliyoacha ACL yake *total damage*, akisema hataleft hii chance impitie.

Mambo ya weather imekuwa tricky na snow na fog, mpaka start gate ilibidi ishushwe leo. Lakini Vonn amefanya vile anafanya, na sasa tunasubiri Sunday kwa women's downhill medal event. Tutashuhudia kama huyu legend ataendelea kutupatia *serere* na kuchukua medal na ACL *kabisa*!
