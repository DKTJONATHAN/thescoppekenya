## Scoop Ni Gani?
Kumekuwa na mbio mingi kwa streets za Hollywood, na "Wuthering Heights" imeshika headlines baada ya ku-dominate global box office na $82 million. Hii ni kama top worldwide debut ya mwaka, kitu imefanya ma-producer wengi wabaki wame-stun. Lakini, kama kawa, sisi huchimbua deep, hatubaki tu na story za juu juu. So, tumego through hizi numbers kujua kama ni kweli filamu imefanya kweli ama ni mchezo tu wa kutupigia macho na stats za nje ya nchi.

## Evidence Ni?
Sasa, tukianza na 'Wuthering Heights', story ni hivi: Imepull $82 million globally. Hii pesa, $42 million imetoka nje ya nchi, from territories 76, na $40 million ni ya hapa kwao over four days za President's Day holiday. Lakini ma-rival studios wanasema North American tally inaweza kuwa $35 million tu. Warner Bros. ndio wako behind hii production ya $80 million. Filamu ni ya Emerald Fennell, na ina Margot Robbie na Jacob Elordi wakicheza Catherine Earnshaw na Heathcliff. Maoni ya mtaa na critics yamekuwa mix-mix, na domestic ticket sales zimekuwa softer kidogo, so ni kama wanategemea audience ya nje kufidia budget. Top markets za nje zilikuwa UK na $10.3 million, Italy $4.4 million na Australia $4.3 million. Bado hawaja-release data za Asia, na China na Japan bado hawajafungua.

Then, kuna "GOAT," ya Sony, animated sports comedy, imeshika number two na $47.6 million debut. Hii imepata $15.6 million from 42 international markets. Hii filamu ya watoto, about roarball champion, bado haijatoka 40% ya markets za nje, kama China, Australia, Germany na South Korea. UK ilibeba $4.8 million, Mexico $1.8 million na Spain $1.2 million. "GOAT" pia imecost $80 million, so Sony wanatumai itafanya kama "Migration" ya 2023 ama "The Wild Robot" ya 2024. Maneno haya si mageni jijini, ma-studio hu-push hivi ndio filamu zi-gain traction. Watu wamekuwa wamesota new family movie since "Zootopia 2" so "GOAT" ina advantage kabla ya "Hoppers" ya Pixar ifike March.

Tukimalizia, "Crime 101," crime thriller na Chris Hemsworth na Mark Ruffalo, imeshika number tatu na $12 million from 60 markets. Hii filamu ya Amazon MGM ilistruggle hapa kwao na $15 million over traditional weekend na estimated $17.7 million mpaka Monday, ikileta global tally ya $29.7 million over four days. Top territories ni UK na $2 million, Australia $1.4 million na Saudi Arabia $790,000. Hii budget yake ni $90 million, so itahitaji long theatrical life ndio i-justify hiyo mukuchu. Amazon MGM wanatumai big screen inasaidia ku-elevate profile ya filamu kabla ziende streaming. ## Verdict Yetu Ni
So, "Wuthering Heights" imefanya noma, lakini tusi-kufinish kumalo. Bado kuna maswali kama domestic performance itatosha kufidia budget bila kurely sana kwa international market. Pesa ni mingi, but pia budget ni high. "GOAT" ina potential, especially na hio gap ya family movies, na Sony wako na matumaini makubwa. Kama "Crime 101" nayo, imebant kidogo, na budget yake ni noma. Ni kama Amazon MGM wako tu in it for the long game ya streaming. Bottom line ni kwamba, box office ni kama soko, sometimes unaji-flex, sometimes una-anguka nayo. Tuendelee kuchimba!
