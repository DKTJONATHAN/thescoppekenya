**The Scoop: Dosh ya Ma-Youth Iko Shwari ama Inaenda Ku-Anguka Nayo?**

Rada imechafuka kwa Nyota program, ile mradi ya ma-youth ilishikwa mkono na World Bank. Tumenusa hii story na imesema hivi: around Sh1.06 billion, ile dosh ilipeanwa kwa startups, iko karibu kupotea! Serikali yenyewe inasema kuna chance kubwa, kama 20 percent, ya hizo biashara 120,000 za ma-youth zitafeli, no cap. Hii ni rada mbaya sana, cousin, hasa vile small businesses huku Kenya hu struggle kufikisha hata miaka tano.

Lakini sasa projections zinasema 24,000 enterprises zinaweza kufyeka. Hii imeweka Hustler Fund kwa spotlight tena, ju nayo pia imekuwa na high default rates.

Kuna kitu ingine imefanya hii story iwe sus zaidi. Tumenusa vile last year, serikali ilish splash Sh1 billion under Nyota program kwa consultancies na administrative expenses pekee yake, for the year ending June 2025. World Bank walitoa Sh1.223 billion, na karibu yote, Sh1.032 billion, ikaenda kwa MSEA for administrative activities and consultancy services. Hii ni ku-anguka nayo for real!

Hii Nyota progie, yenye budget ya Sh33 billion na World Bank wakifadhili Sh29.5 billion, inataka kusaidia 820,000 ma-youth na grants, skilling, na digital training. Hadi sahi, Sh3 billion imetolewa kwa 120,000 ma-youth. Kila beneficiary hupata Sh50,000 kwa tranches, na Sh6,000 huenda direct kwa NSSF kama savings, no cap. Kwa ile first tranche, wanapata Sh22,000 kwa Pochi la Biashara na Sh3,000 kwa NSSF.

Lakini, kama vile tuliona kwa ile stori ya [AI Ni Ndogo, Context Ndiyo Mdosi wa Ukweli!](/posts/ai-ndogo-context-ndiyo-mdosi-ukweli), projections ni kitu moja, lakini ground-zero ni ingine. Hata Mang'eni alishasema at least nusu ya hizo investments zinaweza sail through. Mbeleni, kuna progie ingine World Bank ilifadhili, the Kenya Youth Employment and Opportunities Project (KYEOP), yenye iliisha 2024. Audit yake ilionyesha half of the beneficiaries walikuwa unreachable, ama biashara zao zilikufa kabisa. Ndio maana World Bank wameanza kutumia blockchain technology ku track dosh ya Nyota.

Na hivi majuzi, leo kabisa, Deputy President Kithure Kindiki amesema wata-expand Nyota program kusupport two million ma-youth, from 820,000 ya kwanza. Hii ni fiti, lakini bado kuna maswali mengi. PS Mang'eni mwenyewe alishataka ku-caution dem zetu waki-jituma wasiombe ma-boy dosh ya fare from Nyota funds, wakisema kazi ni kujituma na wajenge biashara zao. Hii inaonyesha kuna pressure kwa hizi funds, no cap.

**The Verdict: Wito kwa Uangalifu na Uwazi**

Wasee, hii rada ya Nyota progie ni complex. Kuna hope kubwa ya kusaidia ma-youth, especially na expansion plan ya ku reach 2 million beneficiaries. Lakini, na hiyo Sh1 billion ilipotea kwa consultancies, na ile 20 percent ya biashara zinazotarajiwa kufyeka, tunajiuliza kwani dosh inasimamiwa aje? Hii si soft life hata kidogo kwa ma-youth wana struggle kuanzisha biashara. Serikali inahitaji kuwa transparent, na kuhakikisha dosh inafika kwa walengwa na si kwa mifuko zingine.
