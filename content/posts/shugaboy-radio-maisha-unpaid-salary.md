He revealed a history of inconsistent payments and financial difficulties plaguing the media house during his tenure. Shugaboy’s claims shed a harsh light on the often-glamorous facade of the media industry, exposing a bitter reality behind the microphones. After nearly a decade of service, contributing to talk shows, news, and entertainment programming, Shugaboy's decision to leave was reportedly prompted by "months of inconsistent salary payments". He detailed how initial monthly salaries devolved into irregular disbursements, with some months passing without any pay, leading to a substantial build-up of arrears he could no longer bear. This isn't just a personal grievance; it's a stark reminder that even prominent voices in the media are allegedly not immune to the precarious financial state of their employers.
