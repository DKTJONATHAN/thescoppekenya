## *Supernatural* Stars Invade *The Boys* Final Season! Jared Padalecki and Misha Collins are officially joining the highly anticipated final season of *The Boys*, reuniting with former *Supernatural* co-star Jensen Ackles. Set to debut in April 2026, the duo will play "douchebag" characters, promising a delightfully chaotic and messy reunion for fans.

A Diabolical Reunion on Screen The anticipation for *The Boys* season 5 is skyrocketing, especially with the news of Jared Padalecki and Misha Collins joining the cast. The former *Supernatural* brethren are set to appear in episode 5 of the final season, bringing their unique brand of "douchebag" energy to the supe-filled universe. Showrunner Eric Kripke, the mastermind behind both series, expressed his delight, noting that the characters are "just really not morally upstanding dudes." This aligns perfectly with the gritty, morally ambiguous world of *The Boys*. Kripke has a history of bringing his *Supernatural* family into *The Boys*, with actors like Nathan Mitchell, Jim Beaver, Alvina August, and Jeffrey Dean Morgan having already made appearances. With season 5 marking the series' grand finale, it feels like a full-circle moment to have the main gents back together, albeit in vastly different roles.

Ackles on the Supernatural Crossover Jensen Ackles, who returns as the cryogenically frozen Soldier Boy and father of Homelander, shared his initial thoughts on the crossover. He insisted that the integration "make sense" for the show, showcasing his commitment to *The Boys*' integrity. "Kripke figured out a way to weave them in there and make it outrageous and make it something special," Ackles revealed. He emphasized that their appearance will propel both the story and the existing characters forward, promising more than just a fleeting cameo. Trailers have already offered glimpses of the mayhem, hinting at scenes involving "light choking and at least one massive, blood-splattering explosion." Ackles teased the intensity, stating, "I did not expect some of the things that we filmed in that sequence to go the way they did. It's a messy one."

The Stakes for the Final Battle The final season of *The Boys* picks up with Homelander having seized complete control of the U.S. government. Dissidents, including Hughie, Frenchie, and Mother's Milk, find themselves in "Freedom Camps," while Starlight valiantly attempts to rally a rebellion. Homelander’s obsession with immortality and declaring himself a god sets a dangerous precedent. Meanwhile, Butcher, now superpowered himself, aims to use a chemically engineered supe-killing virus to eliminate Homelander and every other supe for good. As the stakes reach an all-time high, Homelander awakens his slumbering father, Soldier Boy, for a critical mission that inevitably puts them on a collision course with Padalecki and Collins' new characters. *The Boys* season 5 premieres on April 8, 2026, on Prime Video. Even beyond the main series, Ackles will continue his role as Soldier Boy, headlining the prequel spinoff *Vought Rising* alongside Aya Cash's Stormfront, a series that has already wrapped filming.
