A Safarilink light aircraft carrying 17 passengers and two crew members crashed shortly after takeoff from the Angama Airstrip in the Maasai Mara on the morning of April 20, 2024. Remarkably, all 19 individuals on board survived the incident with only minor injuries.

## A Miraculous Escape in the Maasai Mara

### A Brush with Destiny
The skies over the Maasai Mara are typically associated with tranquil wildlife tours, but the morning of April 20 brought a harrowing aviation emergency. The light aircraft plummeted to the ground shortly after departing from the Angama Airstrip. Witnesses on the ground described the scene as shocking, noting that the wreckage of the small plane lay crumpled near the runway. Despite the terrifying nature of the impact, the fact that every person on board survived with only minor injuries is being described as a miraculous outcome.
