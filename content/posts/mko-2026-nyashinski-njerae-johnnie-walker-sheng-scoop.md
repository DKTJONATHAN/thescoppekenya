## Scoop Ni Gani?
Wasee wa Nairobi, Inspecta wetu wameconfirm! Hii Magical Kenya Open (MKO) 2026, si mchezo tu ya golf. Johnnie Walker wameamua ku-finyilia entertainment side vibaya sana na wameweka wasanii wakali kushika show. Nyashinski na Njerae ndio ma-headliner kwa Johnnie Walker Entertainment Village pale Karen Country Club. Hii event yote inaendelea kuanzia Feb 19 hadi Feb 22, 2026. Hii inamaanisha, hata kama wewe sio fan wa golf, bado kuna form ingine noma ya kushika!
