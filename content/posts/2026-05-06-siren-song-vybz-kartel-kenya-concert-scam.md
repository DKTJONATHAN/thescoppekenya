<h3>The Lure of Unseen Riches and the South Sea Company's False Dawn</h3>

He repurchased at inflated prices, a costly re-entry that would later wring from him a lament: "I can calculate the movement of the stars, but not the madness of men." The collective belief in boundless prosperity, nurtured by vague promises of exotic trade, had eclipsed all reason.
