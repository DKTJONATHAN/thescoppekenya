**THE SCOOP**

Mtaani kumechemka! Wasee wa Nairobi wameanza kuwaza sana baada ya billboard moja ya ajabu kuibuka Ngong Road, ikiwa na picha ya President William Ruto. The Nairobi Street Insider, tumenusa hii story na hatujui ni nini inajiendeleza. Hii billboard imeanza kuzua maswali mob, na wengi wanajiuliza, "Nani anazusha hii upuzi na ni nini inafanywa?"

Picha ya Daktari Ruto akiwa amevaa Kaunda suit ya kijivu, akishika karatasi, imejaza billboard yote. Juu ya hayo, kuna sifa kama "Bold," "Brave," na "Innovative," na phrase "A Cut Above the Rest." Lakini sasa, kitu imezua ngori zaidi ni ile namba ya simu imewekwa hapo wazi kabisa! Watu wameanza kubonga X (Twitter ya kitambo) wakijiuliza, hii ni advertising ya nini? Ama ni campaign ya 2027 imeanza kufinyilia mapema?

**THE RECEIPTS**

The Nairobi Street Insider tumepiga msako, na Receipts ziko. Hii billboard haikuja tu hivi hivi. Inaonyesha President Ruto na accolades zake, ikiwemo CGH (Chief of the Order of the Golden Heart) title na pia kusema ni PhD holder. Hizi details zote zimefanya story ishikane moto kwa mitandao.

X user AbdiHakim Keinan ndiye alishare hii picha ya kwanza, akiandika, "I saw this along Ngong Road this morning and couldn’t understand it up to now. What exactly is the advert for?" Inspecta wetu wameconfirm kuwa confusion ni national. Hata mzee wa media Eric Latiff aliingilia kati, akibonga na kuuliza, "Who is behind this billboard? What’s the message? And why is their telephone number so prominent? What do they want us to do with it?" Hii inamaanisha hata ma-guru wa habari wamepigwa na butwaa.

Wengine wameanza kuweka connection. Kibet Bull alilinganisha hii na campaigns za Museveni wa Uganda, akisema, "It shouts this, by 2027 his campaign posters will be at JKIA." Mwingine, Mbiyu, aliharn sote dhidi ya personality politics, akisema, "When we resort to individual worship and not the agenda or ideology, we have lost it as a country." Kwa kifupi, kila mtu ana form yake kuhusu hii billboard. Na ile namba ya simu imewekwa, ndio imefanya wasee wengi waanze kuhoji motives. Ni ya nini haswa?

**THE VERDICT**

The Nairobi Street Insider tunaona hii kitu ni kama game imeanza. Na mimi kama The Nairobi Street Insider, naona hii ni kama mchezo wa kucheza na akili za Wakenya. Kenya yetu ina shida za kiuchumi, mambo imekuwa ngumu, doshi inakosa, alafu tunapelekewa billboards za ku-hype mtu? Hii ni kama kuongeza chumvi kwa kidonda.

Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, hii fom ina harufu ya 2027. Inaonekana kuna watu wametaka kuanza kuweka foundation mapema. Lakini sasa, ni nani hao wanaweka doshi mingi kwa hizi billboards, na wanataka Wakenya tufanye nini na hiyo namba ya simu? Ni kama wanataka tuwapigie simu tuwasifu ama? Kwa The Nairobi Street Insider, tunasema hii ni utata! Wajinga waliisha Kenya, hatuwezi kubembelezwa na picha na sifa ilhali mambo ya mtaani bado ni ngori. Ngoja tuone vile hii story itaisha.
