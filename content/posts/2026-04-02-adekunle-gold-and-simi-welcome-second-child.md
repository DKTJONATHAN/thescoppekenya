Nigerian music power couple Adekunle Gold and Simi have welcomed their second child. Simi announced the baby's arrival on her Instagram on Wednesday, April 1st, 2026, confirming the birth occurred on March 10th, 2026. This heartfelt revelation follows months of public anticipation.

## Adekunle Gold and Simi Welcome Second Child

### A Heartfelt Announcement

The acclaimed singer shared a series of intimate maternity photographs, including a striking black and white silhouette and a matching denim ensemble with Adekunle Gold, to mark the special occasion. Simi's caption, "+1, 3.10.26, God bless my little family #mommyof2," conveyed the joy without disclosing the baby's gender or name. The announcement swiftly put an end to online whispers that had circulated for months, including earlier rumours in January of 2026 suggesting the couple had welcomed twins. Simi's post definitively confirmed the arrival of a single child.

### A Legacy of Love and Music

The pregnancy itself was unveiled with the couple's signature understated style. Adekunle Gold subtly showcased the baby bump within the visuals for 'My Love Is The Same,' a track from his recent album, Fuji, allowing the music to deliver the news. The announcement garnered an immediate outpouring of well wishes from prominent figures across the entertainment industry. Celebrities such as Temi Otedola, Ladipoe, Broda Shaggi, Kiekie, Mercy Aigbe, and media personality Ebuka all shared their congratulations, celebrating the expansion of the beloved family.

Adekunle Gold and Simi, who have been together since approximately 2014, cemented their union in a private ceremony in January 2019. This intimate affair only became public knowledge after the event. Their first daughter, Adejare, born in May 2020, has charmed fans over the years with glimpses of her vibrant personality shared by her parents online. The couple celebrated their seventh wedding anniversary in January 2026, continuing to exemplify one of Nigerian entertainment's most stable and admired relationships.
