**The Scoop**
Rada ni moto! Tumenusa hii story chap chap na tuka confirm: Kenya Pipeline Company (KPC) IPO imepigwa extension ya siku tatu za kazi. Hii kitu ilikuwa ifunge leo Thursday, but sasa imesukumwa mpaka February 24, 2026. Yaani, wasee wamepewa time extra ya kujipanga.

**The Evidence**
Sources wanasema Capital Markets Authority (CMA) ndio walikubali hii extension, na sio bure. Kumbe, kwa zile public participation forums na stakeholder engagement, ma-retail investors walikuwa wamejipiga kifua wakiomba masaa zaidi ya kuseti investment zao. Acting MD wa Privatisation Authority, Dr. Janerose Omondi, amesema hii move ni ya ku-ensure wasee wengi wanaparticipate na pia ku-expand domestic share ownership. Eti, serikali inataka kila Mkenya ajiskie yuko ndani ya hii mzinga. Target ni kublaze Sh106 billion kutoka kwa mauzo ya hizi 11.8 billion KPC shares, kila share ikienda Sh9.

Hii fom ingine ni kwamba, allocation results zitatoka March 4, alafu shares zikreditiwe kwa CDS accounts na refunds zifanyike by March 6. Listing na trading kwa Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE) itaanza March 9. Wasee wananong'ona eti, hii extension pia ni juu IPO ilikuwa inastruggle kufikisha target ya mauzo. Si mchezo!

Interpol Imewasaka Africa Nzima!](https://zandani.co.ke/posts/online-scam-kings-wamechapwa-kiboko-interpol-imewasaka-africa-nzima), kuna mabazenga wengi huku nje wanachapa deals za kizungu. Hii KPC inakaa legit, msisahau kudig deep.

**The Verdict**
So, kama ulikuwa umekaa ukisema utaingia box lakini masaa yakakuishia, sasa una chance ingine. Ama kama ulikuwa bado unajipanga ku-invest, the window is still open mpaka February 24. Hii move inaonyesha serikali inajaribu ku-make sure kila mtu ana-feel inclusive kwa hizi deals za kitaifa, especially zile za kukuza uchumi. Kazi ni kwako!
