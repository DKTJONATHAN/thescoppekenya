## Diana Kemunto Ignites Iyanii Relationship Firestorm Social media sensation Diana Kemunto is pushing back against singer Iyanii's public disavowals, boldly declaring their romance is far from over. She claims to have unilaterally renewed their "contract," asserting her control over their narrative despite the "Donjo Maber" hitmaker's denials. It appears this celebrity love story has more chapters yet to unfold.

The Unilateral Renewal In a recent interview with Ankali Ray, Kemunto was unequivocal, dismissing Iyanii's attempts to distance himself. She dramatically withdrew previous statements regarding a breakup, emphatically stating, "What contract ended? Who ended it? I withdraw what I said before. The contract is not over; I have renewed it by fire, by force. He might not have renewed it, but I have." This bold declaration sets a new tone for their public saga.

Two Sides, One Story? Despite her confident stance, Kemunto admitted a stark difference in perception. "I am with him, but I don’t know if he is with me," she confessed, highlighting the curious disconnect. This admission adds a layer of intrigue to her resolute claim, leaving fans wondering about the true state of their union. While many Kenyans are keenly [navigating Kenya's economic future](https://zandani.co.ke/article/just-money-podcast-just-ivy-africa-kenyan-wallstreet) or pondering financial investments, Diana seems to be making a significant emotional investment of her own.

Love's Melody and Rivals Beware Kemunto’s motivations are clear: a deep affection for Iyanii and the undeniable pleasure of being serenaded. "I love him. Do you know what it means to have someone sing for you? I enjoy being sung to," she explained. Unfazed by the possibility of other romantic interests, she issued a playful yet firm caution to any potential rivals, warning them to "be careful" around her man. She insists the relationship's end will solely be her decision.

Iyanii's Counter-Narrative Kemunto's determined "renewal" stands in direct opposition to Iyanii's recent public efforts. Following earlier rumors of a reconciliation, the singer took to social media to shut down the whispers, declaring, "I am not aware of it or involved in it. RUMORS DON’T SPREAD!" While Iyanii seeks to quell speculation, Kemunto appears intent on crafting her own romantic script, keeping their story very much in the public eye.
