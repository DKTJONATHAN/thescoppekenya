Social media personality and businesswoman Esther Musila and gospel singer Guardian Angel recently marked six years of their high-profile relationship. Musila shared a heartfelt Instagram post on Friday, March 13, 2026, reflecting on their journey and offering a nostalgic look back at the origins of their widely followed romance.

## Esther Musila and Guardian Angel's Enduring Partnership

### Acknowledging Their Support System

In her celebratory message, Musila underscored the vital role of the support system that has been with the couple since the very beginning. She shared a throwback photograph featuring media veteran Maina Kageni, whom she credited as a foundational part of their narrative. Musila noted that both she and Guardian Angel maintain distinct friendships with Kageni, and they both hold him in high regard, promising to continue "giving him his flowers" for the significant role he has played in their lives.

### An Unexpected Path to Lasting Love

Reflecting on how their bond has evolved, Musila openly admitted that their current life together was not necessarily what they had initially envisioned. She described their journey with a profound sense of spiritual gratitude, questioning "Who is God?" as she looked back on how their relationship has remarkably surpassed their original plans. She expressed deep appreciation for her husband, thanking him for entering her life and helping "give birth" to a love story that only grows more beautiful with time. This sentiment of celebrating and appreciating loved ones resonates with other public figures, such as Yhemo Lee, who recently gifted his mother a fourth car for Women's Day.

### Advocating for Genuine Connection

Since they first made their relationship public, Musila and Guardian Angel have consistently garnered national attention, often navigating conversations about their age difference with grace and unwavering focus on their shared faith. By openly celebrating their six-year milestone, the couple continues to champion the power of genuine connection and commitment. Musila emphasized that the friendships they have cultivated over the years have served as an anchor for their marriage, proving that a strong community is essential to a lasting partnership. As they embark on their seventh year together, the couple remains a prominent example of unconventional love within the Kenyan entertainment industry, continually encouraging their followers to pursue happiness steadfastly.
