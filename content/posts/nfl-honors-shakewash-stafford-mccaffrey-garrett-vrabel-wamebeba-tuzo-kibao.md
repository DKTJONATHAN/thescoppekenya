Sasa basi, kama hujasikia, NFL Honors ya 2026 imekuwa moto kama pasi! Wasee wamekuwa wakisubiri matokeo, na wacha niwaambie, kumekuwa na shangwe na mshangao kibao.

Kwanza kabisa, Matthew Stafford, yule QB wa Rams, amechukua taji kubwa la AP NFL Most Valuable Player (MVP) kwa msimu wa 2025. Huyu jamaa amewashangaza wengi na return yake ya nguvu, akichukua tuzo hii adimu.

Lakini sasa, tukienda kwa wale wametajwa hapo awali, Christian McCaffrey wa San Francisco 49ers amebeba tuzo mbili, si mchezo! Amepigwa jina kama AP NFL Comeback Player of the Year na pia amechukua the USAA Salute to Service Award. Haishii hapo, McCaffrey pia ameshinda Bart Starr Award ya 2026, inayotambua uongozi na tabia njema ndani na nje ya uwanja. Huyu mtu ni legend!

Joe Thuney, guard kutoka Chicago Bears, naye hajabaki nyuma. Amekuwa wa kwanza kabisa kushinda tuzo mpya ya Protector of the Year. Hii ni kuonyesha vile amechunga sana line yake. Msee ako sawa!

Myles Garrett wa Cleveland Browns amethibitisha yeye ni D-line hatari kwa kuchukua tuzo ya AP NFL Defensive Player of the Year. Huyu jamaa amekuwa akishika sack rekodi kibao, so hii haijashangaza mtu.

Na kwa upande wa makochi, Mike Vrabel wa New England Patriots amepigwa jina la AP NFL Coach of the Year. Hii inaonyesha vile amepindua meza kwa Patriots na kuwaleta juu.

Awards zingine zilizotolewa ni pamoja na Jaxon Smith-Njigba wa Seattle Seahawks akishinda AP NFL Offensive Player of the Year, Tetairoa McMillan wa Carolina Panthers akichukua AP NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year, na Carson Schwesinger wa Cleveland Browns akipata AP NFL Defensive Rookie of the Year.

Hii sherehe ya NFL Honors imemalizika na washindi wengi wakiwa wamebebana na tuzo zao. Wacha tuone kama Kendrick Bourne na yellow suit yake alifanya statement, lakini hawa washindi wengine wamefanya statement kubwa sana kwa uwanja!
