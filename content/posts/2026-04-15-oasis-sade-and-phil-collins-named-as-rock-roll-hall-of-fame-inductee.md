## Rock & Roll Hall of Fame Welcomes Oasis, Sade, and Phil Collins to Legendary Ranks Get ready for musical fireworks! The Rock & Roll Hall of Fame just unveiled its 2026 class of inductees, a star-studded lineup spanning genres from Britpop to R&B. Oasis, Sade, and Phil Collins lead a diverse group set to be celebrated in Los Angeles this November, promising an unforgettable night of music history.

The Headliners: A Mix of Icons This year's Rock & Roll Hall of Fame inductees are nothing short of iconic. From the alternative rock anthems of British band Oasis to the smooth, soulful sounds of Sade, the committee clearly aimed for a wide appeal. Genesis frontman turned solo superstar Phil Collins also makes the cut, bringing his legendary drumming and crooning to the prestigious hall. Joining them are punk rock firebrand Billy Idol, heavy metal titans Iron Maiden, the influential post-punk sounds of Joy Division/New Order, the velvet voice of Luther Vandross, and the groundbreaking hip-hop collective Wu-Tang Clan. American Idol's Ryan Seacrest and Lionel Richie even had the honor of announcing these musical greats.

Beyond the Performers: Special Recognition The Hall of Fame isn't just about the main acts; other luminaries are also receiving well-deserved accolades. The Early Influence Award will honour trailblazers like salsa queen Celia Cruz, Afrobeat pioneer Fela Kuti, hip-hop royalty Queen Latifah and MC Lyte, and country-rock icon Gram Parsons. The Musical Excellence Award recognizes the profound contributions of Linda Creed, Arif Mardin, Jimmy Miller, and Rick Rubin. And for his unparalleled impact on bringing music to the masses, legendary TV host Ed Sullivan will posthumously receive the Ahmet Ertegun Award.

Who Missed the Cut? While celebrations are in order for the chosen few, some beloved artists will have to wait for another chance. The Black Crowes, Mariah Carey, Melissa Etheridge, New Edition, Pink, Shakira, INXS, Lauryn Hill, and Jeff Buckley were all nominated but didn't make it into this year's class. The good news is, history shows that persistence pays off; Billy Idol, Oasis, and New Order were all nominated last year before securing their spots this time around. The grand induction ceremony is set for November 14, 2026, at the Peacock Theater in Los Angeles. Fans eager to witness the tributes and acceptance speeches can tune in to ABC and Disney+ in December 2026. Get ready for a night of musical heritage and star-studded moments!
