**The Scoop: Calabar Amewasha Moto Nakuru!**

Maboys na madem wa mtaa, tumegonga rada ya nguvu, na hii si story ya jaba! David "Calabar" Owino, yule gathee wetu alikua anapiga tizi Harambee Stars na Gor Mahia, alafu akawaka Zesco United kule Zambia, amerudi kwao Nakuru. Na si kama mgeni, hapana! Amekam kama volunteer coach wa St Joseph Youth FC, na hapa mtaani, watu wamechangamka mbaya. Wengi wanasema hii ni move ya maana ku-revive football ya Nakuru, yenye imekuwa ikifinywa na ngori kibao lately. Lakini Inspecta wetu wamechimbua zaidi, hii yote ni nini haswa?

**The Evidence: Unveiling na Story Za Ndani**

Tumenusa hii story vizuri, na sources zetu zimeconfirm. Calabar alikuwa unveiled kwa Afraha Stadium annex, na mbali na yeye, St Joseph Youth FC pia walionyesha signings nane mpya. Hii team inacheza FKF Division One (third tier), na mission yao ni kufika Premier League within two seasons. Calabar mwenyewe amesema, "Nakuru ndio nilianzia," na anataka kutoa experience yake kwa maboys wadogo. Timu manager amemsifu sana, akisema ni rare kuona player wa caliber yake akifanya kazi for free. Hii move ya Calabar imewasha moto Nakuru, na wengi wanajiuliza kama ni plan ya kufanya football ya huko irudi rada, ama ni story tu za kuboost ego ya team. Sasa basi, Calabar kurudi home ni kitu poa sana. Inaweza kuwa catalyst ya kuamsha hio 'sleeping giant' ya Nakuru football. Na vile amekam kama volunteer, ni kama anapigia debe wengine, especially ma-veterans, wa-invest time na resources kwa grassroots. Macho ziko hapa kwa St Joseph Youth FC na Calabar.
