Haya sasa, watu wangu wa mchongoano! Jana usiku kulikuwa na mambo mazito pale Beverly Hilton, California, wakati Directors Guild of America (DGA) Awards za 2026 zilipigwa sherehe. Na kama kawaida, sisi huona yote!

Mzee Paul Thomas Anderson ndiye alikuwa anachukua taji la "Outstanding Directorial Achievement in Theatrical Feature Film" kwa filamu yake matata, 'One Battle After Another'. Manze, hii si mchezo mdogo! Ni kama amechukua kiti cha enzi, na wadau wanasema huyu jamaa anaweza kuenda kuchukua Oscar yake ya kwanza mwaka huu. Kwani DGA Awards mara nyingi huonyesha nani ataibuka mshindi wa Best Director kule Oscars.

Kuna vile Leonardo DiCaprio, ambaye yuko kwa 'One Battle After Another', alimkabidhi Anderson hako kamedallion ka DGA, na jumba zima likasimama kumshangilia! Hapo sasa, Anderson alitoa speech ya hisia, akimkumbuka yule first assistant director wake marehemu, Adam Somner, ambaye alitutoka mwaka 2024. Alisema, "Hii ni heshima kubwa sana," na akasema wataichukua kwa upendo na shukrani.

Lakini si Paul Thomas Anderson pekee alikuwa anachoma jukwaa. Kumekuwa na directors wengine waliochukua tuzo zao. Amanda Marsalis alishinda kwa "The Pitt" (Dramatic Series), na Seth Rogen na Evan Goldberg walinyakua yao kwa "The Studio" (Comedy Series). Eti, Seth Rogen na Evan Goldberg walimshukuru Catherine O'Hara marehemu kwa ushindi wao wa "The Studio". Shannon Murphy pia alichukua tuzo kwa "Dying for Sex" (Limited & Anthology Series), na Mstyslav Chernov alinyakua heshima kwa Documentary Film yake, "2000 Meters to Andriivka".

Kwa wale wanajiuliza, 'One Battle After Another' imepata nominations 13 za Oscars, ikifuata nyuma ya "Sinners" ya Ryan Coogler yenye nominations 16. So, kama huyu Anderson amechukua DGA, basi tutegemee makubwa!
