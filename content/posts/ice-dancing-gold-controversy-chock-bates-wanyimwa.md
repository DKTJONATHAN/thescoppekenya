**The Scoop: Walikuliwa Gold kwa Macho Wazi?**

Wasee wa Kanairo, Inspecta wetu wamekuwa rada sana kwa 2026 Winter Olympics kule Milan Cortina, Italy. Na tumenusa mzinga kubwa sana! French team, Laurence Fournier Beaudry na Guillaume Cizeron, ndio walishika gold kwa njia yenye imechachisha wengi, na hata wenyewe Chock na Bates wanasema walidhani wameshika gold!

**The Evidence: Rieng ya Ma-Judge Ilikuwa Wazi!**

Sasa, hii ndio ngori yenyewe. Chock na Bates, ambao wamekuwa wakiskate pamoja miaka 15 na walikuwa mabingwa wa dunia mara tatu, walipiga performance fiti sana. Walisema ilikuwa performance yao kali kuliko zote kwa career yao! Lakini, hawa ma-judge, haswa yule wa French, Jézabel Dabouis, walikuwa na rieng zao.

Cheki hii evidence: French team ilifanya makosa, Guillaume Cizeron alifinya kwa element inaitwa twizzle, na hiyo haikupaswa kupuuzwa. Lakini, ma-judge hawakuwa rada sana. Scores zao zilikuwa 225.82, Chock na Bates wakiwa 224.39 - difference ndogo tu ya 1.43 points!

Hapo ndio the real mbwakni inaanza: Judge wa French, Dabouis, alipea team yake mark ya 137.45 kwa free dance, lakini Chock na Bates akawapa 129.74. Hiyo ni almost eight points difference! Hii score yake ilikuwa the lowest kwa panel yote ya ma-judge tisa, na ilikuwa 5.20 points chini ya average ya ma-judge wengine! Inspecta wetu wameconfirm kuwa five out of nine judges walikuwa wamepea Chock na Bates points mingi kuliko French team, lakini huyo judge mmoja wa French ndiye alibadilisha all the calculations. Hapo sasa, ni kama alifanya decision kwa niaba ya panel yote!

Plus, hii French team yenyewe haiko fiti. Guillaume Cizeron amewahi shutumiwa na partner wake wa zamani, Gabriella Papadakis, kwa "controlling" na "demanding" behaviour. Na partner wa sasa wa Laurence Fournier Beaudry, Nikolaj Sørensen, alipigwa suspension kwa allegations za sexual assault, ingawa ilikuja overturniwa. Hii yote inaweka ukungu kwa ushindi wao.
