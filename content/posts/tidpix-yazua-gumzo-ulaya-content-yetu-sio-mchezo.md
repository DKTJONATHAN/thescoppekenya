## Scoop Ni Gani?
Tumenusa hii story inatrend mbaya kwa streets za digital! TidPix - Authentically African, wazito wa kutoa content safi kutoka Africa, wamechapisha mzinga wa expansion. Sai unaweza pata show zao kwa LG Channels kote Ulaya! Hii ni kama wamefungua njia kwa content yetu kushika place global, kutoka UK hadi Finland na Denmark. Hii si mchezo, madem na maboy wa Europe sasa watafinyanga remote waone story zetu 24/7 free, na ni ad-supported.

## Evidence Ni?
Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, Rushlake Media, wenye wako nyuma ya TidPix, wameingia partnership na LG Channels kupeleka African films, series, na reality TV kwa mamilioni ya households. Philipp Hoffmann, yule founder wa Rushlake Media, amebonga ukweli akisema hii move ina-cater demand ya African storytelling kwa mainstream global distribution. Matthew Broughton wa LGEEH Europe WebOS Content na Services pia amepiga tano, akisema wamefurahia ku-welcome TidPix na kupea LG TV owners access kwa hizo films na series za African.

Content zenyewe sio mchezo, ziko na mambo kama 'Nairobi Half Life' (Kenya), 'Supa Modo' (Kenya), 'Abujaa' (Nigeria), 'Coming to Africa: Welcome to Ghana' (Ghana), na 'Young and Rich' (Kenya). Na si hao tu, eti kuna ile teen drama ya Kenya-Uganda, 'Prefects', ilikuwa nominated kwa 2025 International Emmy Award! Receipts ziko wazi, 'Prefects' ilikuwa nominated kwa Kids: Live-Action category lakini haikushinda, mshindi alikuwa 'Fallen' kutoka UK. Hii mambo ya TidPix ku-expand ina-confirm African content sio tu za kubaki hapa kwetu. Sai tuna-represent global, si ati ni zile story tu za mtaa kama vile Georgina Njenga aliamwaga unga kwa Baha, ati story yao iliisha na ikazikwa. Hiyo ni kama mambo ya jana!

## Verdict Yetu Ni
Kama The Nairobi Street Insider, ninaweza sema TidPix wamepiga dimbwi kubwa. Hii ni fiti sana kwa African creators na story zetu kupata visibility kwa premium global platforms. Hawabaki tu kwa AVOD na FAST channels, wanasonga mbele na connected TV platforms. Hii inaonyesha jinsi African narratives zinaweza kushika place kote duniani. Kwa hivyo, kukaa rada ni muhimu, maana content yetu inakuja na kasi!
