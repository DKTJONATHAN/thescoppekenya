## Scoop Ni Gani?
Wasee, tumenusa hii story ya mmoja wetu, Alma Bronxi, akimeza game ya creative economy kote East Africa. Msupa amekuwa akipiga mangumi kwa entertainment industry for over 13 years, na si ati anapiga tu, ana-engineer mpaka structure. Anaselect wasanii, anawashika mkono, anawapeleka mbali. Hii si story ya kawaida, huyu ni kimbelembele amechukua skills za economics kutoka Ardhi University, akazitupa kwa showbiz, na sahii anapiga mark vibaya sana. Inspecta wetu wameconfirm huyu dem anajua kudrive!

## Evidence Ni?
Receipts ziko hapa mbele yetu, na hazidanganyi. Alma Bronxi anabonga vile entertainment haikuwa departure kwake, ilikuwa application ya economics. Aliona talent ni mob East Africa but structure ilikuwa zero. Akaamua kuingia mbele, ku-build system za kugeuza creativity into sustainable business. Na si ati alikuwa na chwani mingi, la! Aliretrenched kutoka banking sector 2017, akajirudisha kwa entertainment bila capital, bila safety net, just belief na relationships.

Hapo ndio Bronxi Entertainment ilizaliwa, design ya ku-professionalize artiste management na kuconnect creatives wetu na global platforms. Msupa amesupport, manage, na promote wasanii zaidi ya 30! Anabonga talent pekee haitoshi; unahitaji discipline, consistency na willingness to learn. Kama kawa, showbiz inakuanga na drama mingi, si ati hatujaona.
