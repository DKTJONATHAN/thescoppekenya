## Scoop Ni Gani?
Wazito wa Johnnie Walker wameseti ile mzinga ya Magical Kenya Open 2026, na si tu golf itapigwa. Entertainment nayo imepewa kipaumbele kinyama! Tumenusa hii story na inspecta wetu wameconfirm, Nyashinski na Njerae ndio wamechaguliwa kama main acts kwa Johnnie Walker Entertainment Village pale Karen Country Club. Hii si mchezo, manze. Hii ni fusion ya golf ya kimataifa na burudani ya hali ya juu!

## Evidence Ni?
Johnnie Walker, wakiwa official alcohol beverage na entertainment partner, wamemwaga pesa kibao, KES 65 million! Hii pesa ni ya ku-enhance fan experience kwa hii tournament kubwa ya golf Kenya. Tournament Village itafunguliwa rasmi Ijumaa, February 20, baada ya round ya pili ya golf na cut.
Ijumaa, washika wata-enjoy ngoma kali kutoka kwa DJs Nijo, Redbone, Vera na Festa, huku DJ Trixy akiwa resident DJ na Kibunja akipiga umcee.
Jumamosi, golf ikiendelea, the Village itabuild-up mpaka kwa headline performance ya Nyashinski, yule msee wa "Mungu Pekee" na Johnnie Walker brand ambassador. Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, Nyashinski anatarget kutoa album mpya April inaitwa "The Showman Residency." Huu msee anajulikana kwa lyrics zake deep na performance moto. Line-up ya Jumamosi pia itakuwa na DJ Mista C, DJ E, DJ Lisney na Daq Child, pamoja na resident DJ Benitez na emcee Gogo.
Tournament itaisha Jumapili, February 22, na baada ya final round, Njerae ndiye atafunga show na performance yake ya soulful. Njerae amepata following kubwa sana kwa ngoma zake za mapenzi, perfect kwa Valentine's weekend vibes. DJs watakuwa DJ MGM, DJ Ivy, DJ Grauchi na DJ Pierra, na DJ Payne akiwa resident DJ na Kwambox kama emcee.
Maureen Osoro, EABL Marketing Manager wa Spirits, alituambia wanataka ku-create meaningful experiences kwa fans. Anasema sponsorship yao si tu ya ku-support golf, but pia ku-elevate vile fans wanapata experience kwa tournament. Hii si story tu ya golf, manze. Hii ni experience mzima! Johnnie Walker wamemwaga pesa kinyama, KES 65 million specifically kwa logistics na kudesign fan experience. Hii pesa si mchezo, na inatuonyesha vile sponsors wameanza kuona sports events kama platform kubwa ya ku-engage na fans. Magical Kenya Open sasa ni 57th edition, event ya DP World Tour, na imevutia wachezaji 144 kutoka more than 25 countries.

Hii mzinga si ya kukosa, wanangu. Johnnie Walker wamepiga luku event kinyama, wakileta Nyashinski na Njerae kuwasha moto. Hii si tu kwa mafans wa golf pekee, hapana.
