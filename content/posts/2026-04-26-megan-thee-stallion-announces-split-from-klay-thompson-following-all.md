Grammy-winning rapper Megan Thee Stallion confirmed her split from NBA star Klay Thompson on Saturday, April 25, 2026, amid accusations of infidelity. The artist revealed the end of their high-profile romance on Instagram, citing a lack of trust, fidelity, and respect as core reasons for her decision to walk away.

## Megan Thee Stallion Ends Relationship with Klay Thompson

Megan Thee Stallion officially announced the end of her relationship with NBA star Klay Thompson, stating her decision to walk away after her core values were no longer being upheld. The "Mamushi" hitmaker shared the news on Saturday, April 25, 2026, emphasising that trust, fidelity, and respect are non-negotiable in her relationships. Her statement followed a series of emotional Instagram posts where she hinted at Thompson's alleged infidelity, though without naming him directly. Fans quickly connected the remarks to the Dallas Mavericks guard.

### Allegations of Compromised Values

In her Instagram posts, Megan Thee Stallion alluded to being "played" and dealing with "HORRIBLE mood swings and treatment" during Thompson's basketball season. She questioned his commitment to monogamy, expressing a need for a "REAL break" from the situation. Her public declaration underlines a firm stance on personal integrity within her romantic partnerships, resonating with many across the continent who value clear communication and respect in relationships. The news quickly spread across social media and entertainment platforms throughout Africa, demonstrating the continent's keen interest in global celebrity developments, similar to the widespread discussions that arise when figures like Larry Madowo criticise Nigeria over visa fees for Kenyan citizens.

### A Whirlwind Romance

The couple had only recently made their relationship public a few months prior to the breakup, showcasing their affection through shared photos, lavish gifts, and joint trips. In March 2026, Megan had posted a mirror selfie with the NBA star, and earlier in the year, Thompson had gifted her a powder-blue Bentley for her birthday, followed by a tropical getaway celebration. Megan Thee Stallion had previously expressed her comfort and contentment in the relationship, describing it as unexpected and Thompson as the "nicest person I've ever met in my life" at her Pete & Thomas Foundation Gala in New York City on July 16, 2025, where they made their red-carpet debut.

### Thompson Remains Silent

As of Sunday, April 26, 2026, Klay Thompson has not publicly responded to Megan Thee Stallion's breakup announcement or the allegations of infidelity. His silence leaves fans and observers awaiting his perspective on the highly publicised split. Prior to her relationship with Thompson, Megan Thee Stallion had been romantically linked to other public figures, including Torrey Craig, Romelu Lukaku, and rapper Pardison Fontaine.
