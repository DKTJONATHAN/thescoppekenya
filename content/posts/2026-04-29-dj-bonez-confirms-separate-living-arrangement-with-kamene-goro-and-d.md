## DJ Bonez Clarifies Marital Situation

DJ Bonez recently addressed the public's concerns in a phone interview with Radio 47's Alex Mwakideu, stating that Kamene Goro moved back to her parents' home after falling ill and has remained there during her recovery. Despite living apart, Bonez firmly maintains that they are still a couple, dismissing widespread speculation of a permanent split. "She is at their home. For me, I know that we are together unless you people have news that I have not yet received," Bonez remarked.

Similarly, the entertainment scene has recently seen other public figures facing health challenges, as was the case with Comedian Sammy Kioko, who was hospitalized following an alleged assault in a debt dispute.

He also mentioned that they continue to meet occasionally, with Kamene sometimes visiting their marital home. While Bonez previously hinted at a more complex story on social media, his latest statements appear to be an attempt to downplay the perception of a crumbling marriage.
