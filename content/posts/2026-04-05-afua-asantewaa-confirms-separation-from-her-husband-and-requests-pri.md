Ghanaian media personality and two-time Guinness World Record contender Afua Asantewaa Aduonum confirmed in early April 2026 her separation from her husband amidst increasing public speculation. She has appealed for privacy and sensitivity during this personal transition, following discussions surrounding her marital status.

## Afua Asantewaa Confirms Marital Separation

Afua Asantewaa Aduonum, a prominent figure in Ghanaian media, formally announced the end of her marital relationship in a public statement. The decision to separate was reached privately after careful consideration, according to the statement. Asantewaa emphasized her request for the public and media to treat this sensitive period with respect and understanding. She urged everyone to refrain from intrusive speculation as she navigates her personal life.

### Addressing Misinformation and Public Perceptions

Her confirmation comes amid a surge of claims and speculation regarding her personal circumstances. Asantewaa firmly stated that many circulating assertions are inaccurate and do not reflect the truth of the situation. She also directly addressed the perception that she has been ungrateful to individuals and institutions that supported her journey. Asantewaa unequivocally rejected such characterizations, affirming her deep appreciation for every contribution to her path.

### Unwavering Commitment to Professional Endeavors

Despite the personal challenges, Afua Asantewaa has reaffirmed her steadfast commitment to her professional and advocacy work. She stated that her focus remains on her goals and her determination to continue making meaningful contributions to society. The statement concluded with an apology to anyone who may have misunderstood her comments or felt concerned by recent developments, while expressing gratitude for the ongoing support from her community and partners.
