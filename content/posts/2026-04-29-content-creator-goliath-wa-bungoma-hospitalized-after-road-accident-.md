Popular content creator Goliath wa Bungoma is currently receiving medical treatment in a Nairobi hospital after a serious road accident in Ruaka on Sunday, April 26, 2026. Car Soko, his brand ambassador partner, confirmed his condition has stabilized following significant injury to his right leg when his vehicle was struck by a driver allegedly on the wrong side of the road.

## Goliath wa Bungoma Hospitalized After Ruaka Accident

Car Soko officially confirmed on Monday, April 27, 2026, that their brand ambassador, Goliath wa Bungoma, is undergoing medical treatment. The influencer sustained injuries after a road accident in Ruaka. According to the company's statement, while Goliath's right leg was significantly injured, his condition has since stabilized. Management expressed optimism for a full recovery and requested public support and respect for his privacy during this challenging time.

### Rising Concerns Over Reckless Driving in Nairobi

Police reports indicate Goliath wa Bungoma was hit by a vehicle reportedly driving against traffic, a dangerous trend that has increasingly worried city residents and authorities alike. This incident underscores growing concerns about reckless driving habits on Nairobi's roads. The unfortunate accident involving Goliath follows closely on the heels of another tragedy in the entertainment sector, the recent passing of Gengetone artist Shalkido (Kevin Mburu Kinyanjui). Shalkido tragically lost his life after a crash along Thika Road, between Githurai and Roysambu. Despite being rushed to the Intensive Care Unit at Kenyatta University Teaching, Referral and Research Hospital (KUTRRH), the artist was later declared brain-dead, highlighting the persistent dangers of early-morning road travel in the city. Discussions around road safety continue to be a vital topic for Kenyans.
