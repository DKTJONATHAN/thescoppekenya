Nigerian businessman Olakunle Churchill has vehemently denied widespread 2026 reports claiming he is divorced from actress Rosy Meurer, branding them false and misleading. Churchill’s clarification has sparked fresh conversations about celebrity unions across the continent.

## Churchill Addresses Meurer Divorce Claims

The entrepreneur took to Instagram to counter circulating rumours that he and Nollywood actress Rosy Meurer had quietly ended their marriage. Churchill insisted that the notion of a divorce is baseless, highlighting that no church or court wedding ever took place, only an introduction. He also questioned the authenticity and origin of any alleged court documents, asserting that he was never formally notified of any legal proceedings, a requirement for any legitimate process in Nigeria.

### The Nuance of Their Union

Despite the public perception of a formal marriage, fueled by the couple welcoming two children since 2021, Churchill maintains that a legal marital bond was never established. This distinction has added a new layer to the ongoing public discussion about their relationship. Meurer, for her part, has also dismissed various rumours, including claims she unfollowed Churchill on Instagram or had a prior close friendship with Tonto Dikeh.

### Reconciliation with Tonto Dikeh

Churchill also revealed a reconciliation with his ex-wife, actress Tonto Dikeh, with whom he shares a son, King Andre. He stated that they have forgiven each other and moved past their previously very public and contentious split. Their improved relationship was recently noted when they were spotted spending time together with their son, further fueling public interest in their evolving dynamic.
