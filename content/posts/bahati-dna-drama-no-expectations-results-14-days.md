**The Scoop**
Mambo yamenukia vibaya kwa Bahati, yule msee wa 'Mama' hit, after tumenusa amefanya DNA test na mwanamke anasema ndiye mama yake mzazi! Hii story imeshika neti vibaya sana, na kama reporter wenu mtaalamu wa mtaa, tumechimbua deep kuleta exclusive. Bahati, mwenyewe alikulia home ya watoto, amefungua roho kuhusu hii situation inamchanganya akili.

**The Evidence**
Sources wetu kutoka Nairobi hospital wameconfirm Bahati aliturn up Jumanne, February 17, 2026, kudesa hii mambo scientifically. Amekaa ngumu, akisema hana hype yoyote kwa results. "At the moment I have no expectations about the DNA test. I am expecting the results to be anything,” Bahati alisemanga huko nje.

Ukiangalia, huyu dem anasema yeye ndiye mama yake, na stori yake ilimuingia akili ndio akaamua kufanya due diligence. Hakuwa anataka kukatia mambo bila proof. "If I had certain expectations, I would not have come here, but because I listened to her and her story is genuine, and she has a strong belief that I am her son, I did not want to refute that or write it off before doing my due diligence with something scientific,” alisema. Mama wa watoto wake, Diana, amekaa kimya kama panya kinywani kwa hili swala. Bahati anasema ni kwa sababu ni sensitive na hata yeye hajui form ni gani. "Diana has not commented on this issue because she is also wondering whether the woman is my mum, and it’s a sensitive matter. It’s not something she could easily comment on," aliexplain. Wasee, anasema results zitakuwa out after 14 days, so tunasubiri na hamu. Hii story ya huyu mama imekuwa ikifinyika for five years, but Bahati alikuwa anaikatia hadi YouTuber mmoja akaipost ndio akaitake serious.
