## Scoop Ni Gani?
Watu wangu, mambo ni ngori Westside huko Iowa! Parker Sutherland, yule fresha balla wa UNI (University of Northern Iowa) tena tight end number 89, amerudi kwa mola. Huyu msee alikuwa na miaka kumi na nane pekee na angechapa 19 tarehe 24 Feb. Shule imetangaza amededi Saturday, Feb. 14, na imewacha genge la Panther Athletics na familia yake wamechapa vibaya. Hii ni story mbaya sana manze, yaani kijana mdogo hivi!

Yaani, msee amezimia tu, hakuna trauma, ama vipi? Sports Director Megan Franklin amesema "ni heartbreaking day" na wamevunjika roho.
