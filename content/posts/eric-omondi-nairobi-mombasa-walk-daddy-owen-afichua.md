**The Scoop**
Mtaa yote imekuwa ikipiga mchapa na kuhoji Eric Omondi, yule kaimoko amekuwa activist, vile alifinyanga safari yake ya Nairobi-Mombasa charity walk kwa four days pekee! Cheki, huyu msee alianua journey yake ya 486-kilometre kutoka Nairobi hadi Mombasa Monday, February 10, 2026, akitaka kuchanga dooh za Sisi Kwa Sisi rescue centre. Hii initiative ilishika attention ya nchi nzima, na Wakenya wengi online walikuwa wamechapia, wakijiuliza ati gani hii speed ya ajabu kwa distance kama hiyo ndani ya muda mfupi? Hii story ilikuwa imewashwa sana mtaa na kwa social media.

**The Evidence**
Sasa, Inspecta wetu wamefichua mambo, na si mwingine ila gospel musician Daddy Owen ndio amefungua machozi yetu. Alifafanua ati hii journey haikuwa tu ya physical endurance, bali ilikuwa campaign ya kuchanga dooh symbolic. “Nimeona watu wakiuliza Eric Omondi alifikaje Mombasa kwa four days. Hii walk ilikuwa fundraiser - ukituma 10k taslimu it covered for 1km, meaning he didn’t have to physically walk that 486kms,” Daddy Owen alibonga wazi. Hii inamaanisha waliset up model poa sana ambapo well-wishers walikuwa wanachangia dooh financially, na kila 10k ilikuwa inakover 1km. Hapo ndio siri ilikuwa, msee, ikigeuza journey kuwa collective humanitarian effort badala ya strictly physical marathon. Hii si mara ya kwanza mtaa inashangaa na mambo za mabuda wetu. Sasa hapa kwa Eric, swali ilikuwa ni speed na vile alifinya hio distance!

**The Verdict**
Baada ya ku-complete trip, Eric Omondi alishukuru sana Wakenya kwa support, akisema response yao ilifinyanga expectations zote. "Tumefinya expectations kwa kuchanga dooh during this walk. Nashukuru Wakenya kwa support wameonyesha from Nairobi to Mombasa,” alisema. Hii charity journey yake ilivutia watu wengi sana kwa major highways, na supporters walikuwa wakishangilia, wakichanga, na hata kumsindikiza part ya safari. Initiative yake pia ilifungua conversations kwa social media kuhusu creative ways public figures wanaweza ku-mobilize resources kusaidia vulnerable communities. Journey ya Omondi sasa imesimama kama moja ya philanthropic campaigns zake noma sana, ikichanganya activism, public participation, na fundraising kufichua needs za struggling families hapa nchini.
