**The Scoop**

Mambo ni matatu, madem na maboyz! Hii story ya Harambee Stars na AFCON 2027 imeshika moto, na inspecta wetu wametoboa dondoo deadly. Kumbe, boss wa Harambee Stars, Benni McCarthy, na chair wa LOC ya AFCON 2027, Nicholas Musonye, wako na targets tofauti kabisa za Harambee Stars kufika next year kwa tournament. Hii si mchezo, manze! Mmoja anataka Round of 16, mwingine anasema semi-finals!

**The Evidence**

Tumenusa hii story kinyama na tumepata McCarthy amesema target yake ni Harambee Stars kufika *Round of 16*. Eti anapray tusiingie kwa "Group of Death" na magoli kama Morocco, Ivory Coast, ama Senegal, kwanza ndio tusidedi mapema. Anataka "modest group" ndio tuwe na chance ya kupigana na kudelve kama vile tulifanya kwa CHAN 2024. Anasema Round of 16 itakua "good starting point" na success kwetu.

Lakini sasa, Musonye wa LOC yeye amepiga luku ingine kabisa! Yeye anataka Harambee Stars wafike *semi-finals*! Hii si kiki, sources wanasema Musonye anadrive pressure ya mapreps za mapema na proper, na anataka serikali iingie mifukoni ku-support team. Anasema tukifanya kama CHAN 2024, pale President Ruto alibonga story ya ku-reward wachezaji na thao millioni kwa win na thao mia tano kwa draw, itainua morale na tutafika mbali. Anasema kwa CHAN tulifika quarter-finals, sasa kwa AFCON inabidi tufanye "better". Hii yote ndio kuepuka story ya empty stands wakati host nation imetoka mapema.

Kama vile tuliona kwa ile stori ya [MCCARTHY NA MUSONYE: AFCON 2027 TARGET NI KIKI AMA REALITY?](/posts/mccarthy-musonye-afcon-2027-target-kiki-ama-reality), hii mambo ya targets ni tricky. AFCON 2027 inakuja next year, June na July, hapa Kenya, Uganda na Tanzania. McCarthy aliingia kama coach March 2025, so amekua kwa ground.

**The Verdict**

Sasa swali ni, nani yuko realistic hapa? Benni anataka kucheza safe, Musonye anataka ku-dream big. Manze, Harambee Stars haijawahi kutoka group stages kwa AFCON zao sita zilizopita. Kufika Round of 16 kama McCarthy anataka, hio itakua noma sana. Lakini semi-finals, kama Musonye anataka, hio ni kama kupanda mlima Everest bila kamba! Mtaani wanasema, kama mapreps hazikanyagi gas na wachezaji hawapati support kama ya CHAN, hizi targets zote zitabaki tu story za mtaa! Itabidi tushike rada kuona kama hawa maboss wata-align ama Harambee Stars itadidimia kwa gap ya expectation.
