Sasa basi, Milan Cortina Olympics zimekuwa moto ajabu! Team USA imechukua gold kwa figure skating team event, na ni Ilia Malinin ndiye alibamba sana, akiwa na miaka 21 tu. Hii event ilikuwa inafanyika kuanzia Februari 6 mpaka 8, 2026, na Team USA ilishinda Japan kwa pointi moja tu, 69-68, huku Italy wakichukua bronze.

Malinin alipiga free skate ya kichwa mbaya, akatua ma-quad jumps tano na akapata 200.03 points, akihakikisha gold inabaki kwa Team USA. Na kama haitoshi, huyu msee wa kihistoria alifanya backflip ya kwanza legal kwa Olympics after 50 years! Hii backflip ilikuwa imepigwa marufuku kitambo, lakini ikahalalishwa 2024. Madini zao walizipata immediately, tofauti na vile ilikuwa Beijing 2022.

Leo, Februari 10, 2026, Ilia Malinin anaanza safari yake ya kuwinda gold ya individual kwa men's singles short program. Huyu "Quad God" ni two-time reigning world champion na four-time U.S. champion, so anakuja na kifua sana. Tukae chonjo tuone kama atapeleka ingine nyumbani!
