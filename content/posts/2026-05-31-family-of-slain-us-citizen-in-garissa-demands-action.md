Despite the family providing investigators with specific details identifying the alleged assailant, local police commanders in Garissa County have yet to effect an arrest or initiate a formal pursuit.

This delay has created an escalating diplomatic friction point, as the family continues to coordinate with the United States Embassy in Nairobi regarding the status of the investigation. Diplomatic protocols dictate that when a foreign citizen is the victim of a violent crime on Kenyan soil, the host government is expected to provide prompt updates and transparency to the relevant consulate.

However, the family asserts that despite their cooperation and the submission of leads, the investigation remains in a state of administrative paralysis. They claim that repeated visits to the local station have been met with vague assurances that the matter is still under review.
