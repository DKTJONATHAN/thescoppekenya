## Scoop Ni Gani?
Kumbe kuna stori ilikuwa inasambaa ati "Wuthering Heights," ile movie kali ya mad love yenye Margot Robbie na Jacob Elordi wame-star, imepata matrouble kwa box office. Wasema walidhani Valentine's Day ingewaseti poa na mkwanja mrefu, lakini rumors zikaanza kusambaa ati haifiki expectations na critics wanaijua daily. Lakini budesko, nimechimba na nimepata form ingine tofauti kabisa! Kumbe hii movie ina-lead box office chart na inafanya poa sana, ikionyesha ma-haters wako wrong kabisa!

## Evidence Ni?
Chini ya radar, mambo ni tofauti na vile watu walidhani. "Wuthering Heights" ilikamata $11 million Friday, siku ya opening, na sasa inafaa ikule mkwanja karibu $40 million kwa ile weekend ndefu ya Presidents' Day/Valentine's Day. Hata kuna ma-projections zinasema inaweza fika hadi $48 million au zaidi kwa hio four-day run! Hii ni stingo ingine kabisa compared na zile whispers za $33-$35 million tu. Globally, inafaa ikule $80 million by end of weekend, kumaanisha inaweza recoup budget yake ya $80 million haraka sana.

Ni kweli, ma-critics hawakubambika sana, na Rotten Tomatoes imewapa 63 percent tu. Pia, audience waliipa B CinemaScore. Lakini, wadhii wa audience kwa Rotten Tomatoes wameipa 84 percent hadi 85 percent, ikionyesha wanabambika nayo sana.
Meanwhile, "GOAT" ya Stephen Curry inaendelea kutesa, iki-exceed expectations na inafaa ichukue $20-$25 million kwa hiyo four-day weekend. Hii ni baada ya kupata A CinemaScore kutoka kwa ma-kids na wazazi.
Lakini "Crime 101" ya Chris Hemsworth na Halle Berry, inakaa imepata kibarua ngumu. Inafaa ichukue around $13.8 million tu, na inaitwa one of Chris Hemsworth's worst debuts. Hii inashangaza sana, una-kumbuka ile siku [Catherine O'Hara alikuwa kwa rada ya rada na ile story ya blood clot, ikishika headline zetu kitambo](/posts/catherine-ohara-genja-blood-clot-rada)? Celeb world huwa na surprises!

## Verdict Yetu Ni
From vile nimeona, "Wuthering Heights" imeshika form mbaya sana, hata kama critics walikuwa wanaijua. Audience wamejitokeza, na inakaa Warner Bros. wametoa rabbit ingine kutoka kwa hat. Hii inaonyesha ati si lazima critics wakupee green light ndio movie ikule mkwanja. Sometimes, audience ndio huamua. Hii ni ushindi mkubwa kwa Emerald Fennell na ma-stars wake, Robbie na Elordi, na ni message kwa ma-studios kuwa, Gen Z na ma-Milli wako na sauti yao kwa box office! Tutaendelea kuwachimba kuona kama momentum itaendelea.
