## Scoop Ni Gani?
Eish, mtaa inawaka! Nimechunguza na nimegundua Roddy Mote, kocha mkuu wa Harding Football, amedropi kitu babkubwa! Ametoa listi kamili ya makocha wa 2026, na hii si story za kitambo, ni form mpya kabisa. Anasema ni staff imejaa "The Brotherhood" vibes, na chenye kimefanya nianze kunoana ni ati kati ya ma-kocha kumi full-time, wote kumi wamesoma Harding University. Hii ni kumaanisha ni talent ya homegrown inapewa kipaumbele, kitu safi sana. Anasema staff ina mix ya mabingwa na wazee wamekuwa hapo kitambo, pamoja na alumni wamerudi after kufanya poa place zingine.

## Evidence Ni?
Sasa hapa ndio mzinga unazidi! Paul Simmons, yule msee mwenye rekodi ya kushinda mingi kuliko wote (92-16) kwa hiyo program, haendi popote. Amepewa position mpya, General Manager wa Harding Football. Huyu jamaa ni kichwa kubwa, amewahi kuwa National Coach of the Year mara mbili, sasa atakuwa ana-manage program, fundraising na roster.

Kwa defensive side, Mote amepandisha ma-kocha wake wawili wa kitambo. Luke Tribble, amepewa Defensive Coordinator na ataendelea kuwa Defensive Backs Coach, na Tre'von Biglow amekuwa Co-Defensive Coordinator na ataendelea kushika Defensive Line. Hii defense ilikuwa namba moja kwa NCAA Division II scoring defense 2025, so hapo walichafua! Neil Evans naye amerudi kama Linebackers Coach baada ya kuchapa kazi poa kwa Harding Academy na kushinda state championships nne.

Offensive side ndio inafanya mtu asmile sana! Bryce Bray, yule jamaa alikuwa anachapa mambo ya Rimington Award, amerudi kama Offensive Coordinator na Fullbacks & Tight Ends Coach. Huyu alikuwa OC Rogers High na akashinda 7A West Offensive Coordinator of the Year. Eric Kelly naye amerudi kama Recruiting Coordinator na Slotbacks Coach, na huyu alikuwa FootballScoop NAIA Coordinator of the Year 2025 aki-engineer highest-scoring offense. Will Francis anaendelea kama Quarterbacks and Fullbacks Coach, na alisaidia Harlon Hill finalist Andrew Miller kufanya ile kazi ya kuweka NCAA records za rushing yards na touchdowns. Gavin de los Santos, All-American lineman wa kitambo, ni Offensive Line Coach. Cameron Tucker ndio anafunga listi mpya kama Special Teams Coordinator na Wide Receivers Coach. Pia kuna ma-graduate assistants kibao, ma-legend wa Bisons kama Kendale Allen na Jhalen Spicer wana-assist. Hii ni kumaanisha Mote anajua venye anapanga mambo yake, si tu kuleta watu ovyo ovyo.
