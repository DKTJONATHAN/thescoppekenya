## vivo Y31d Launches in Kenya: Unpacking the 7200mAh Battery Powerhouse

The vivo Y31d has officially arrived in Kenya, targeting users who prioritize exceptional battery life above all else. This new smartphone, available from KES 26,999, stands out with its groundbreaking 7200mAh battery, marking the largest capacity vivo has integrated into a smartphone to date. The device is a strategic offering for the Kenyan market, emphasizing endurance and resilience for daily use.

### The 7200mAh Battery Advantage and Charging Innovations

The core appeal of the vivo Y31d lies in its massive 7200mAh battery, meticulously engineered to support extensive usage without the need for frequent recharging. Performance data indicates that a single charge can deliver up to 45 hours of video playback, 14.5 hours of navigation, or nearly 12 hours of continuous gaming, providing significant relief from battery anxiety. When power eventually dwindles, the device supports 44W fast charging, enabling a rapid return to full capacity. Vivo has also addressed long-term reliability by designing the battery to retain at least 80% of its original capacity even after six years of regular use, equivalent to approximately 1,600 charge cycles. Furthermore, the charging system actively monitors temperature over 40,000 times daily to prevent overheating, ensuring safe and efficient power management.

### Durability and Display: Built for the Kenyan Environment

Beyond its substantial 7200mAh battery, the vivo Y31d is built with a strong focus on durability, making it well-suited for diverse Kenyan conditions. It boasts IP68 and IP69 ratings, signifying robust resistance to dust and the ability to withstand submersion in water or high-pressure water jets. This level of protection positions the Y31d as a highly resilient device in its segment. The phone also incorporates "Wet-Hand Touch" technology, ensuring the screen remains responsive even when wet, a practical feature for users caught in the rain. Physical durability is enhanced with reinforced glass and structural protection, designed to absorb accidental drops and daily impacts.

The visual experience is delivered through a 120Hz refresh rate display, offering smooth scrolling and vibrant visuals. This display achieves a peak brightness of 1250 nits, ensuring excellent readability even under bright outdoor sunlight.

### Performance and Software: Snapdragon 6s Gen 2 and OriginOS 6.0

Underpinning the vivo Y31d's performance is the Qualcomm Snapdragon 6s Gen 2 processor, a chipset launched in December 2025 and designed for efficient performance in 2026 entry-level smartphones. This processor ensures reliable daily multitasking and smooth operation. The device runs on OriginOS 6.0, Vivo's latest Android-based operating system, which replaces Funtouch OS globally. OriginOS 6.0 introduces a refined notification interface known as Origin Island and integrates several AI-driven tools aimed at enhancing daily tasks and productivity. The shift to OriginOS 6.0 signifies Vivo's commitment to a more premium and fluid user experience across its devices, as detailed in recent company announcements. ### vivo Y31d Pricing and Availability in Kenya

The vivo Y31d is readily available across retail stores in Kenya and through official online channels. It comes in two color options: Feather Gold and Starlight Grey. For pricing, the model with 6GB of RAM and 128GB of internal storage is set at KES 26,999, while the 256GB storage variant is priced at KES 29,999. This aggressive pricing strategy, combined with its robust features, positions the vivo Y31d as a compelling option in Kenya's competitive smartphone market.
