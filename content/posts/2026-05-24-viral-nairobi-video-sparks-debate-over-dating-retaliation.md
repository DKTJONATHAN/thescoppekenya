### Decoding the Struggle Love Narrative

The obsession with who owes whom in a relationship is deeply tied to the modern Nairobi economic climate. The idea of struggle love-the notion that a partner must endure hardship alongside a man to deserve his eventual success-has been twisted into a rigid transactional framework. When life is expensive and opportunities feel scarce, the temptation to view human relationships through a ledger of debts and repayments is high.

However, translating this economic frustration into directives to harass or degrade others is a dangerous pivot. It replaces introspection with aggression. While creators might claim they are simply reflecting the reality of the dating market, they are actually narrowing the scope of human connection. By selling the fantasy of retribution, they distract from the structural issues that make wealth accumulation so difficult for the average Kenyan in the first place.
