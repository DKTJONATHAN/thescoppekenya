Gideon Sitelu Konchellah, who commanded the Kilgoris parliamentary seat for two decades, died today, June 13, following a reported cardiac arrest. Current MP Julius Sunkuli confirmed the passing of his long-time political rival and colleague, noting the immense weight Konchellah held in regional politics. His career, spanning from 2003 to 2022, saw him survive multiple political tides, including a brief but influential stint as Minister for Immigration and Registration of Persons under President Mwai Kibaki between 2006 and 2007. Others offer a more critical perspective, suggesting that his twenty-year tenure defined the very voter behavior that locals are now trying to move away from. A university student from the area remarked that while his departure is a significant event, it highlights a generational shift in Narok.
