Award-winning Kenyan singer Nadia Mukami has ignited a nationwide conversation this week, appearing to hint at a split from celebrity husband Arrow Bwoy. A viral teaser for her upcoming single, "Ex-Harudiwi," shows the "Radio Love" hitmaker dramatically removing her engagement ring, fueling speculation across social media platforms.

Nadia Mukami's Symbolic Gesture

"Ex-Harudiwi" Teaser Sends Shockwaves The speculation surrounding the popular couple stems from a widely shared teaser for Mukami's upcoming single, ironically titled “Ex-Harudiwi” (An ex never returns), slated for release on April 3, 2026. In the captivating video shared on her Instagram account, the “Radio Love” hitmaker is seen washing her luxury vehicle outside her opulent home. While the scene initially appeared domestic, her subsequent actions were far from it. The moment that truly captivated viewers was when Nadia pointedly removed her engagement ring, dramatically tossing it onto her manicured lawn. This symbolic gesture, combined with the song’s evocative title, has led fans to question if the “African Popstar” is publicly addressing a real-life heartbreak or executing a shrewd marketing strategy.

A Message on Self-Worth Beyond the dramatic ring toss, Nadia used the clip to deliver a powerful message to her female followers, urging them to prioritize their self-worth and walk away from relationships where they feel unappreciated. "Listen, guys, if somebody doesn’t know your worth... you tell them, ‘Please get away from me because they fumbled the best thing that they ever had,’" she asserted. She went on to caution against giving second chances to those who failed to value them initially, reinforcing the song’s core message that an ex should remain in the past, even if they later attempt to reconcile. Just as Charisma urges Kenyan youth to register as voters to make their voices heard, Nadia's message emphasizes the importance of making decisive personal choices [https://zandani.co.ke/article/charisma-urges-kenyan-youth-to-register-as-voters].

Public Reaction and Speculation Nadia Mukami and Arrow Bwoy have long been celebrated as one of Kenya’s most stable "power couples," having navigated the entertainment industry together for years. The dramatic video has naturally caused genuine concern among their dedicated supporters. However, some netizens remain skeptical, suggesting the move is a clever "clout-chasing" tactic designed to ensure “Ex-Harudiwi” dominates the charts upon its debut this Friday. As of Tuesday morning, March 31, 2026, Arrow Bwoy has remained silent on the video and the status of their relationship. Regardless of whether this is a candid declaration of a breakup or a high-budget music video concept, Nadia has successfully captivated public attention ahead of her highly anticipated April 3 launch.
