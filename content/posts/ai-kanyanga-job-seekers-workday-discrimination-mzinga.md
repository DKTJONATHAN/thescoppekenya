## Scoop Ni Gani?
Manze, dunia imechange na AI imechukua form kila mahali, hata kwa jobs. Kumbe, hizi systems za AI za ku-hire watu zimekuwa zikifinya wengi vibaya, na sasa kuna mzinga kubwa imeibuka. Sasa hivi, story inasema Workday, kampuni kubwa inapeana tools za HR, wamefinywa kortini na jamaa anaitwa Derek Mobley. Jamaa anasema hizi AI tools zao zimemkanyanga mara kibao kwa jobs, si yeye pekee bali class nzima ya job applicants.

Imagine, una-apply job, alafu AI inaku-reject within minutes ama hours. Hiyo si fair, ama? Mobley anasema, kama African-American na mzee wa 40+, hizi tools zinamkanyanga based on age, race, na hata disability. Hii si tu mchezo, ni story serious ya discrimination, na sasa court imeruhusu notice itumwe kwa wengine wote waliofinywa hivyo, wa-join case kabla ya March 7, 2026. Iko mzinga kweli!

## Evidence Ni?
Hii si tu shori ya Mobley pekee yake, manze. Hii ni trend. World Economic Forum iliripoti 2025, ati 88% ya makampuni tayari wanatumia AI kwa screening ya candidates. Hii inamaanisha, chances ni kubwa kuwa wengi wetu tumepitia hii noma ya AI kutuchuja. Mobley alifile kesi California mwaka wa 2023, akisema hizi AI tools za Workday zinadiscriminate kinyume cha sheria.

Workday wao wanajibamba ati wao hupeana tu tools, alafu decision ya mwisho ni ya employer. Lakini Mobley ana-insist, ati quick rejections zake zinaonyesha ni AI ndio ilikuwa inatoa verdict. Court imekubali class action, kumaanisha Mobley hatuko peke yake kwa hii vita. Watu walio-apply jobs kupitia Workday platform tangu September 24, 2020, na walikuwa 40+ at that time, wanaweza ku-opt-in kwa kesi. Hii inamaanisha ukipigwa chenga na AI, unaweza kuwa na form ya kudai haki yako. Kumbuka, deadline ni March 7, 2026! Hii shori ya AI kudiscriminate si freshi, na imeanza kuleta lawsuits zingine kibao tangu 2022. Hata kuna case ingine dhidi ya Eightfold AI, ikileta maswali kama hizi tools zinapaswa ku-comply na FCRA. Ni kama tu vile watu hu-get caught up kwa digital drama, unakumbuka ile mzinga ya [Instagram Wars: Davido Amemadaa Diamond Platnumz Tena, Ama Ni Kiki?](/posts/instagram-wars-davido-amemadaa-diamond-platnumz-tena-ama-ni-kiki)? Hii ya AI ni mzinga ya ajira, na ni real sana.

## Verdict Yetu Ni
Wasee, hii shori inatukumbusha kitu muhimu: employers hawawezi kujificha nyuma ya AI wakifanya discrimination. Hata kama AI haikusudii kudiscriminate, matokeo yanaweza kuwa mabaya kwa watu wengi. Hii kesi ya Workday ni warning kali kwa makampuni yote yanatumia AI kwa hiring. Lazima wa-cheki their systems vizuri, wa-make sure hazifinyi mtu yeyote based on age, race, gender, ama disability.

Sisi kama Gen Z, ambao tunategemea sana tech kutafuta job, lazima tuwe macho. Tusikubali AI itukanyange bila sababu. Hii kesi ita-set precedence muhimu sana kwa future ya AI kwa jobs. Ni kama tunasema, tech ni freshi, but haki za binadamu ni muhimu zaidi. Employers, chungeni sana! Job seekers, jua haki zako na usikubali kufinywa. Mambo bado, na tuko hapa kukujuza kila kitu.
