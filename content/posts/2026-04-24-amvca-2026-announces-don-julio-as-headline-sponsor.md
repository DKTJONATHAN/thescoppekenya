The 12th Africa Magic Viewers' Choice Awards (AMVCA) has announced a major partnership for its 2026 edition, with luxury spirits brand Don Julio Tequila named as the headline sponsor. MultiChoice Nigeria unveiled comprehensive plans for the prestigious Pan-African event in Lagos, setting the main ceremony for May 9, 2026.

## AMVCA 2026 Unveils Don Julio Partnership and Event Details

### Luxury Upgrade and Strategic Alliances
The AMVCA is set for a significant upgrade in 2026, marked by Don Julio Tequila's inaugural role as headline sponsor. This collaboration signals a new era of luxury for the awards, with Johnnie Walker and The Singleton also joining as supporting brands. Segun Ogunleye, Head of Marketing, Africa Partner Markets at Diageo, stated that this partnership highlights the strength and diversity of their brands and their commitment to celebrating moments of recognition and cultural pride with consumers.

### A Focus on Craftsmanship and Experienced Leadership
Veteran actress Joke Silva has been appointed as the Head Judge for the 12th AMVCA, bringing decades of industry expertise to the role. The 2026 edition's theme emphasizes craftsmanship, directing a stronger spotlight on the pivotal creatives working behind the scenes, including writers, directors, and technical experts. This focus aims to acknowledge the detailed work that brings African stories to life, building on the rich narrative traditions seen across the continent, including insights into [Ghana's Movie Industry: The REAL Nollywood Lessons They're Not Telling You About!](https://zandani.co.ke/article/ghana-movie-industry-nollywood-untold-lessons).

### Categories and Anticipated Nominee Reveal
The 2026 AMVCA will feature 18 jury-decided categories celebrating technical excellence, alongside 11 audience-voted categories allowing fans to champion their favorites. Additionally, three special awards, including the Trailblazer and Lifetime Achievement honors, will be presented. Enthusiasts can anticipate the official nominee announcement on March 29, 2026, which will be hosted by Chiemezie Imo and broadcast across all Africa Magic channels. The 12th edition also introduces two new categories: Best Indigenous Language (North Africa) and Best Indigenous Language (Central Africa), signaling a broader Pan-African direction for the awards.

### Revitalized Fan Experiences
Beyond the main awards night, the AMVCA 2026 will bring back popular fan-favourite events, including Icons Night and Cultural Day. These events are dedicated to celebrating industry heavyweights and showcasing African fashion and heritage, respectively. Atinuke Babatunde, Executive Head of Content and Channels at MultiChoice, shared that this edition's vision is centered on reimagining the celebration of African talent, promising an unforgettable experience that is both stylish and impactful.
