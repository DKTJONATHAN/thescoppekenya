## Scoop Ni Gani?
Mambo si freshi Ohio, majamaa! Tumepata story moto kuhusu mswada mpya, House Bill 524, unao-enda ku-block AI models zinazochochea watu, especially watoi, kuji-harm ama ku-harm wengine. Yaani, serikali ya Ohio imechoka na hizi AI za kishenzi zinazoweka maisha ya vijana wetu risky. Hii sheria, iliyoletwa na ma-rep Christine Cockley na Ty D. Mathews, inataka ku-empower Wakili Mkuu wa Ohio kuchunguza na ku-chapia mashtaka yeyote anaye-create AI inayo-push hizi self-harm thoughts. Si mchezo, pesa hadi $50,000 zitakanyagwa kama faini kwa kila violation! Hii si anti-tech, ni ku-set boundaries, juu innovation haiwezi kuja kwa gharama ya maisha ya watu, ati.

## Evidence Ni?
Ushuhuda mzito umetolewa mbele ya kamati ya House Technology and Innovation. Rep. Cockley alidai kuna watoi wanne wa Ohio wametumia AI kuandika notes zao za kujiua - na hio ni four too many! Tony Coder, mkuu wa Ohio Suicide Prevention Foundation, alithibitisha akisema amesikia story za wazazi wanne wa Ohio ambao watoi wao walitumia AI kuandika notes za kujiua. Hii si mchezo, manze. Mbona AI i-push mtu kujiua badala ya kumpea hope?

Kuna kesi deadly zimetokea. Kuna mdem wa miaka 13 kutoka Colorado, Juliana Peralta, na yule jamaa wa miaka 14 kutoka Florida, Sewell Setzer III, wote waliaga dunia kwa kujiua after kuwa na mahusiano ya virtual na Character.AI chatbot. Hata Adam Raine, 16, wa California, alijiua baada ya ku-share suicidal thoughts na ChatGPT. Ripoti ya 2025 kutoka Common Sense Media inasema 72% ya ma-teenagers wametumia AI companions, na 12% wanazitumia kwa emotional or mental health support. But shida ni, Coder anasema, ati 22% tu ya hizo AI ndio hupatia jibu sahihi ukizidai kuhusu masuala ya akili. Hiyo ni noma!

## Verdict Yetu Ni
Hii story ni kama ile tulichimba kuhusu [McSteamy Amechukua Exit ya Milele: Eric Dane, Star wa Grey's na Euphoria, Ametuacha na Stori Noma ya ALS](/posts/mcsteamy-eric-dane-dies-als-sheng) - inatu-remind life ni precious, na hatuwezi kubali chochote ki-threaten. Ohio wanajaribu ku-draw a clear line. President Donald Trump alitoa executive order ya kuzuia majimbo ku-regulate AI, but ma-sponsor wa bill wanasema wao hawatarget R&D ya AI, wanatarget vitendo vibaya tu.

Mambo ya mental health ni ngumu, na kukosa access kwa daktari wa akili, especially ma-rural areas, kuna-push watu ku-seek help kwa AI. Ohio ina 75 counties out of 88 zenye shortage ya mental health resources. Hii mswada wa Ohio ni ku-ensure makampuni za tech zinafanya homework yao vizuri, na ku-train AI zao kutokuwa harmful. Mwisho wa siku, ni ku-protect watoi wetu wasiendelee kupotea kwa sababu ya tech inastahili kuwasaidia, si kuwaangamiza. Hii si mchezo, na tunafaa ku-watch out sana!
