## The Digital Litmus Test: Why the Frank Edoho Controversy Triggers Unverified Virality

The controversy began when Edoho took to X to state he would no longer enter into romantic relationships. This declaration immediately drew scrutiny, leading critics to resurface an archived video clip where the presenter detailed his use of silence as a disciplinary tool during his marriage. Within 72 hours, the discourse shifted from a critique of his personal philosophy to a full-scale digital crisis. A gossip account known as Cutie Juls released an unverified audio recording purportedly featuring Sandra Edoho and singer Chike, forcing the subjects into the center of a public trial.
