## Dr Aggrey High School Fire in Taita Taveta Raises Concerns

A fire erupted at Dr Aggrey High School in Taita Taveta County on the afternoon of Thursday, April 30, 2026, causing panic among students and teachers. The Kenya Red Cross confirmed the incident, stating the cause of the Dr Aggrey High School fire remains unknown as officials work to contain the blaze on the ground.

### Incident Details and Response

Photos from the scene show a substantial fire engulfing what appears to be a classroom building, with thick smoke rising into the sky. Students and teachers were observed seeking safety and stranded as the situation unfolded. Kenya Red Cross officials, alongside community members and their Taita-Taveta Branch team, promptly arrived and are actively engaged in controlling the inferno. As of this publication, no casualties have been reported, and authorities are anticipated to visit the site for further assessment.

### History of Fire Incidents

This is not the first instance of a fire at the institution. In 2025, Dr Aggrey High School was indefinitely closed after two dormitories were destroyed by fire in February, leading to the indefinite sending home of all students.
