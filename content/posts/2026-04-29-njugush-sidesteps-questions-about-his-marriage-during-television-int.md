Comedian Timothy Kimani, widely known as Njugush, skillfully dodged inquiries about his marriage to Celestine Ndinda during a live KTN interview on April 28, 2026. The exchange, which momentarily diverted from discussions on Kenya's creative economy, saw Njugush deploying his characteristic humour to sidestep personal speculation, entertaining viewers and panelists alike.

## Njugush's Clever Comedy Redirects Marital Questions

During his recent KTN appearance alongside fellow creatives Phil Director and Mammito Eunice, Njugush demonstrated his quick wit when host Leah Ngari brought up trending online speculation regarding his relationship with his wife, Wakavinye. Rather than directly addressing the rumours, the popular entertainer chose to employ his comedic prowess, prompting laughter from the studio audience and effectively redirecting the conversation.

### Humour as a Shield in the Spotlight

The interaction unfolded as Ngari directly questioned Njugush, "Njugush, you have been trending online and we just want to hear your side of the story… what’s happening between you and Wakavinye?" The comedian, known for his impeccable timing, responded with a playful smile, steering the discussion back to the initially intended topic of Kenya’s burgeoning creative economy. This move highlighted his ability to control narratives, even when facing personal questions in a public forum. African creatives are increasingly taking charge of their public personas, much like Davido announcing his focus on African sounds for his next album, showcasing a broader trend of artists defining their own paths.

### Maintaining Control Through Laughter

Njugush's decision to use humour as a shield against personal questions underscores his sharp comedic instincts and his mastery of live television. By refusing to confirm or deny the speculation and instead opting for a lighthearted diversion, he not only entertained his audience but also firmly maintained control over the interview's direction. This moment served as a testament to his unique ability to engage and amuse while expertly managing the boundaries of his public and private life.
