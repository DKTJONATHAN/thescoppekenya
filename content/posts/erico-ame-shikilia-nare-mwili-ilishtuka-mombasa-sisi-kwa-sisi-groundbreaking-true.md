**The Scoop**

Mavijana na wazee, acheni tuwaambie, ile stori ya Eric Omondi ku-walk kutoka Naija hadi Mombasani noma sana! Huyu msee wa comedy na activism alipeana mwili yake challenge ya maana kwa ile safari ya 486km, akitaka ku-raise doo ya Sisi Kwa Sisi Help and Rescue Centre. Sasa, tuna-confirm, ile walk ilimpea mwili toll noma, lakini the mission, inakaa, ili-succeed!

**The Evidence**

Sources zetu zime-confirm Eric Omondi alikonda hadi aka-change form. Yeye mwenyewe aligive out kwa Instagram, akisema, "Nimekonda wasee. Nikianza hii walk nilikuwa nimeungaunga, nilikuwa niko gym, but I lost weight in four and a half days." Pia, hiyo jua kali na terrain ngumu zilimpea tan ya kinyama, eti hapa ni Mrussia na hapa ni Mkenya!

Detectives wetu wamechimba zaidi na ku-confirm ati Erico alipata injuries za ligaments kwa mguu yake ya kushoto na hip. Hata hivyo, alikomaa tu, akisema hata akicrawl, lazima afike Mombasa. Alianza safari February 10, 2026, na alitaka kufika by Valentine's Day, February 14, kule Mama Ngina Waterfront Park.

Kwanza, Eric aliclear the air ati hakucover yote 486km on foot. Kunasema alitembea around 370km, akibypass areas zingine hazardous kama zile za wanyama, like Man Eaters, juu ya safety concerns. Hata hivyo, hiyo haikuzuia the spirit ya Sisi Kwa Sisi. Ma-celebs wengi walijiunga naye kwa stretch tofauti, wakiwemo Khaligraph Jones, Churchill, Amber Ray, Daddy Owen, Carol Radull, Jeff Koinange, Eko Dyda, Butita, Iyanii na Murugi Munyi. Watu wa Team Sisi Kwa Sisi pia walikomaa naye.

Kama vile tuliona kwa ile stori ya [Ugandan Mzito Julius Kyazze Afinya Kitu Deadly, Live54+ Yakimbiza Africa Yote!](/posts/ugandan-mzito-julius-kyazze-afinya-kitu-deadly-live54-yakimbiza-africa-yote), hapa pia Eric Omondi ameonyesha determination ya kinyama kwa project yake. Ile Sisi Kwa Sisi Help and Rescue Centre inafaa kuwa na warehouse, ambulances mbili, clinic, na secondary school ya bure.

**The Verdict**

Licha ya kuchoka na kukonda, Erico hana majuto. Anasema hii mission ni kubwa kuliko discomfort yake. The good news ni ati msee ameshika nare! Sunday, February 16, 2026, Eric Omondi aliofficiate groundbreaking ya Sisi Kwa Sisi Help and Rescue Centre. Hii ni proof ati ile ndoto ya kuwa na rescue centre kamili imekaribia.

Eric ame-urge ma-Kenyans waendelee ku-contribute kwa hii project kupitia paybill yao. The dream ni kufanya ile centre iwe operational, na inakaa itakuwa poa sana. Project imeanza, na tunaona Sisi Kwa Sisi inachukua form. Wacheni tuendelee ku-support hii fomu!
