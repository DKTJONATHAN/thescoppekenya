**The Scoop: Bajaber Bado Ni Mtu wa Simba, Coach Barker Amempea Kibali Full!**
Wasee wa soka, shikeni rada! Kumekuwa na mix-up mob kuhusu future ya Bajaber Mohammed pale Simba SC, lakini sasa mambo ziko clear kama maji ya bomba. Coach mkuu, Steve Barker, amechana mambo yote, akisema huyu mzee bado ni key player kwa plan zake za ku-win. Ati, Bajaber haendi popote, atabaki Msimbazi Street giants! Hii statement imekuja despite Bajaber kukamuliwa na majeraha tu kitambo.

**The Evidence: Majeraha Zilikuwa Zinamfinya, Lakini Talent Yake Iko Rada!**
Huyu mchezaji wetu wa Harambee Stars amekuwa na journey ngumu kiasi hivi na Simba SC. Majeraha zimekuwa zikimsumbua, zikimfinya na kumfanya akose consistency. Remember vile alifunga goli poa kwa Mapinduzi Cup Januari, alafu tena akashika breki na majeraha ingine? Hiyo ilikuwa ngori. Lakini sasa, Barker amem-back kinoma, akisema akishakuwa fit kabisa, Bajaber ni asset muhimu sana kwa ndoto za Simba za ku-dominating hapa East Africa na hata kwa continental football.
Kuna venye ma-rumours zilikuwa zinasema Simba wanafikiria kum-loan back kwa KPL, na ati ma-club kama Kenya Police FC na Nairobi United walikuwa wanamchora. Lakini ma-inspecta wetu wameconfirm, hiyo plan haikupita kabla ya transfer window ku-close. So, Bajaber bado ni Simba official!
Coach Barker, yule alichukua kiti Januari, amesema Bajaber ni "high-quality player" na players wazuri lazima wawe na place kwa team. "Ako na technical ability ya ku-change game, na hatuwezi kutupa talent kama hiyo juu tu ya 'difficult patch' na majeraha," Barker alipiga kiboko. **The Verdict: Bajaber Amekuja Full Dozi, Analenga Ku-Restart Career!**
Na hapa ndio mambo ina-get fiti! Barker amesema Bajaber ako poa sasa. Amekuwa full training for two to three weeks after his most recent setback, meaning ako ready ku-make comeback. Hii ni news njema sana, especially after Simba kutoka kwa CAF Champions League group stage. Sasa wameconcentrate kwa league na Bajaber anaeza kuwa muhimu.
"Amekuwa frustrated sana, but anashow great character kwa training pitch," Barker aliongeza. "Tunammange carefully kuhakikisha akirudi kwa matchday squad, anabaki hapo."
Kuhusu ile story ya ku-loan, Barker alirudia club inam-support. "Priority yetu ilikuwa kumuona akisucceed hapa Simba. Tunaamini ako na potential ya kurelaunch career yake kwa Premier League, na tunataka afanye hivo akiwa na jezi ya Simba, sio elsewhere."
Bajaber alijoin Simba last August after ku-flex skills zake na Kenya Police FC, akisaidia 'Law Enforcers' kushinda ligi yao ya kwanza. Hizo performances ndizo zilimfanya aitwe Harambee Stars. Sasa, tunaona Bajaber akihustle kupita majeraha na ku-dunga goals kwa Simba. Shikeni rada, hii movie bado inaendelea!
