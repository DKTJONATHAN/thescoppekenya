Rwandan director Imanishimwe Viateur, known as Director-i.v Crypto, has officially established his cinematic music video production services in Nairobi this year. The 23-year-old artist, originally from Kigali, promises a fresh, intentional storytelling approach to the city's dynamic and fast-evolving music landscape in 2026.

Director-i.v Crypto's Nairobi Takeover

From Kigali to Kenya's Capital Imanishimwe Viateur, a 23-year-old talent, began his professional journey in 2022, immediately after completing film school. He honed his craft on the vibrant streets of Kigali, Rwanda, starting with freelance work and minimal resources beyond his camera and vision. During this period, he focused intently on mastering the art of directing, not just filming, through a process of small shoots and continuous learning. By 2026, his growing reputation in the Rwandan industry paved the way for his strategic relocation to Nairobi, a move designed to expand his creative horizons in East Africa's bustling entertainment hub.

A Cinematic Signature Director-i.v brings a distinct storytelling methodology to music visuals, prioritizing mood, narrative, and raw emotion. His work often mirrors the depth of a short film, employing controlled pacing, emotionally resonant framing, and a story-led directorial approach. This signature style has already seen some of his music videos accumulate over 400,000 views, a significant achievement that underscores his visibility in the region. His unique perspective offers a refreshing alternative for artists seeking more deliberate and impactful visual narratives.

Notable Collaborations and Future Visions Among his impressive portfolio, Director-i.v has notably collaborated with gospel star Jeanne d’Arc, creating and directing video concepts for her phenomenal hits, "Mujawayezu" and "Igihe." These projects perfectly showcased his distinctive stylistic elements. While the Kenyan music industry is recognized for its faster pace and heightened competition, Viateur views this energetic environment as the ideal backdrop to further develop his creative visual identity within cinematic video production. The dynamic nature of Kenya's entertainment scene, home to captivating figures like Eric Omondi and Lynne, constantly pushes artists to innovate and deliver compelling content. Artists looking for a sophisticated, cinematic video approach for their upcoming projects are encouraged to connect with him on his Instagram page, @director-i.v, to explore collaborative possibilities.
