The Ministry of Education has launched a comprehensive inquiry into the death of a three-year-old learner at Gilgil Hills Academy. The investigation follows a tragic incident on Friday, March 27, 2026, in Nakuru County, aiming to establish the circumstances surrounding the minor's drowning and ensure appropriate action is taken.

## Education Ministry Initiates Probe into Learner's Death

The Ministry of Education confirmed its intervention in a statement issued on Sunday, March 29, 2026, stating that investigations have commenced into the incident at Gilgil Hills Academy. The Ministry is collaborating with relevant investigative agencies to uncover the events that led to the learner's untimely death. Concurrently, the Ministry has reiterated its directive to all public and private educational institutions to rigorously implement existing school safety protocols to safeguard learners within school premises.

### Tragic Drowning Incident Unfolds

The three-year-old learner tragically drowned in a fishing pond located within the Gilgil Hills Academy compound in Gilgil Sub-County. This incident, which occurred while the minor was under the school's supervision, has prompted significant questions regarding institutional responsibility and the adherence to safety standards. The learner's death has sparked considerable concern, leading to a confrontation between the aggrieved family and school faculty demanding immediate answers.

### Family Rejects Autopsy Report, Demands Clarity

According to preliminary autopsy reports, the pupil's death was attributed to a lack of oxygen, consistent with drowning. However, the family of the deceased has publicly rejected these findings, citing discrepancies with initial accounts provided by the school. A family friend reported receiving a call from the class teacher, who initially stated the child was ill with a fever and had been rushed to the hospital. The family expressed profound distress over the conflicting narratives, with one relative questioning, "How did the child end up in the fishpond? I asked him, if you were the one who rescued him in this water, or principal, from which side you rescued him from? he never gave me answers." While the minor has since been buried, the family continues to press Gilgil Hills Academy management for a full explanation of the events preceding their child's death.
