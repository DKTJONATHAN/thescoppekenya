## Eric Omondi and Lynne: A Post-Separation Sweetness Comedian Eric Omondi and his ex-partner Lynne Njihia delighted fans with a tender online exchange. Despite their separation in April 2025, Omondi publicly celebrated Lynne and their daughter Kyla on International Women's Day, receiving a warm response that highlighted their enduring bond.

A Day for Divas (and Daughters) International Women's Day saw many public figures celebrating the women in their lives, and Eric Omondi was no exception. The self-proclaimed "President of Comedy Africa" took to social media, stepping away from his impactful "Sisi kwa Sisi" philanthropic efforts, to honour two very important women. He specifically tagged Lynne (@l.y.nn.e), a content creator and model who supported his transition into social activism, and their young daughter, Kyla (@kyla.omondi), who arrived in August 2023. Omondi's post included a playful promise: "Sending Tam Tam and Yam Yam shortly," accompanied by a string of heart and smiling emojis.

Keeping It Sweet Lynne was quick to acknowledge the public shout-out, responding with an equally warm message. "Awwww. Thank youuuuu Baba Toto. We’re waiting eagerly," she wrote. The use of "Baba Toto," a beloved Swahili term meaning "father of the child," clearly underscores the respect and affection that persists between them, even after their announcement of separation about a year ago. It's a reminder that not all public partings are acrimonious, and mutual admiration can thrive beyond romantic ties.
