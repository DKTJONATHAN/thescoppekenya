In a groundbreaking development for the East African tech ecosystem, PayFlow Kenya has announced the successful completion of its Series B funding round, raising an impressive $50 million.

## The Investment

The funding round was led by:

1. **Global Ventures** - Leading the round with $25M
2. **Africa Tech Fund** - Contributing $15M
3. **Various angel investors** - Making up the remaining $10M

## Future Plans

The company plans to use the funds to:

- Expand operations across 10 African countries
- Hire 500 new employees
- Develop new financial products for SMEs

This milestone puts Kenya firmly on the map as Africa's Silicon Savannah.
