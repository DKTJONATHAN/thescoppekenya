## Scoop Ni Gani?
Tumenusa hii story moto kutoka Edo State, Nigeria, ati Nollywood star Mercy Johnson-Okojie amepigwa kiti kubwa ya ushauri na Governor mpya. Ni kama mambo ya celeb na siasa zimekuwa ikitrend sana sasa hivi. Governor Senator Monday Okpebholo, ambaye alichukua ofisi November 12, 2024, amemchagua Mercy Johnson kama Special Adviser wake wa Public Engagement and Advocacy. Hii appointment ilitoka Thursday, February 19, 2026, na Mercy Johnson mwenyewe aliconfirm Ijumaa, February 20, 2026.

## Evidence Ni?
Receipts ziko wazi kinyama. Statement kutoka ofisi ya Governor ilisema Mercy Johnson-Okojie amepewa hiyo nafasi. Hii kazi yake ni ku-boost communication kati ya Edo State Government na wananchi, especially kueneza awareness na advocacy kuhusu policy initiatives mpya.

Inspecta wetu wameconfirm kuwa Mercy Johnson si mgeni kwa kazi ya kusaidia jamii. Kupitia Mercy Johnson Okojie Foundation yake, amekuwa akisupport initiatives za women empowerment, child welfare, education support, na healthcare outreach kote. Sasa, anatarajiwa kutumia jina lake kubwa na appeal yake mtaani ku-connect na watu. Hii ni kama kusema atakuwa anapeana 'rada safi' kwa ma-youth. Na tukiangalia vile [Budget Foni Zime-Omoka! Gen Z, Hii Rada Ni Yenu!](https://zandani.co.ke/posts/budget-foni-omoka-gen-z-rada) imewasaidia wengi kupata info na ku-connect na tech bila shida, ni wazi Gen Z ni audience muhimu sana kwa hizi initiatives za Public Engagement. Hii ni kumaanisha Governor Okpebholo anajua mambo ya ku-engage na vijana ni crucial kwa serikali yake.

## Verdict Yetu Ni
Hii move ya Governor Okpebholo kumchagua Mercy Johnson ni rada safi kinyama kama anataka kufika grassroots. Celebs wanakuanga na influence noma, na Mercy Johnson amejenga jina kama mtu anayejali jamii. So, for Public Engagement, huyu ndiye picha. Lakini pia, tusisahau siasa ni mchezo mrefu na sometimes appointments kama hizi huleta maswali ya "ni patronage ama ni service kweli?". Inspecta wetu wataendelea kuchimba undani kujua kama hii ni kazi ya kujenga Edo ama ni ku-flex misuli ya power. Time itajibu!
