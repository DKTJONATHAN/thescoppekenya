## Evidence Ni?
Haya sasa, receipts ziko hapa, na zimeongea! Kulingana na data zetu, hawa wazito walianza journey ya Instagram tofauti. Diamond aliingia July 2012, Davido akamfuata March 2012. Ilifika December 2015, Diamond akamadaa Davido, akamfunika na 1.5 million followers, huku Davido akiwa na 1.4 million. Hapo Diamond alikuwa amepiga luku, akawa artist wa Africa mwenye followers wengi zaidi Instagram, akawacha ma-artist wengine kama Wizkid, Don Jazzy na Vanessa Mdee nyuma.

Lakini, kama vile tujuavyo kwa hizi streets, kiti huwa inatembea. By September 2016, Davido alikuwa ameshareclaim kiti chake na 2.9 million followers, Diamond akashuka position ya pili na 2.5 million. Hapo sasa kulikuwa na wengine kama Don Jazzy, Tiwa Savage, Alikiba na Yemi Alade wakipush pia. Hii story ya numbers ni kama ile ya [Fugu Kiki: Ghanaians Wamechafua Mtandao, Tuko Na Receipts!](/posts/fugu-kiki-ghana-zambia-receipts), wazito wote wanataka kuonyesha nani mkuu.

Diamond Platnumz yeye ako na 18.9 million, akishika nafasi ya tatu, nyuma ya Tiwa Savage wa Naija. Globally, the queen wa Instagram ni Selena Gomez kutoka US, ako na 415 million followers!
