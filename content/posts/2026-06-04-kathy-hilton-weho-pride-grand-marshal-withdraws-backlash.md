## WeHo Pride Drama: Kathy Hilton Steps Down, Community Speaks Out

The dust has barely settled in West Hollywood, where celebrity socialite Kathy Hilton has withdrawn as the Grand Marshal Icon for the 2026 WeHo Pride Parade. This decision, announced yesterday, Wednesday, comes after a torrent of community backlash, proving yet again that public figures are under immense scrutiny, especially when aligning with significant cultural events.

### The Backlash Against Kathy Hilton WeHo Pride Role

The digital streets of West Hollywood were ablaze the moment Kathy Hilton was named the 2026 Grand Marshal Icon. In a move that underscored the severity of the situation, the City of West Hollywood and WeHo Pride confirmed that there would be no Grand Marshal Icon designated for the 2026 event. This marks a significant shift, signaling an immediate course correction in response to public sentiment.
