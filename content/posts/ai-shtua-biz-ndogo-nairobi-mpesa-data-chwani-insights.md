**The Scoop**

Madem na majamaa wa Nairobi, Inspecta wetu wamegonga jackpot ya shori moto! Tumenusa hii fom mpya inatrend kwa streets, na inaweza change game kabisa kwa ma-biz ndogo ndogo zetu. Kwani, AI imeingia box na inawafanya ma-entrepreneurs wa Nairobi waache kubahatisha, waanze kukata maji na data kibao!

**The Evidence**

Sources wanasema, hii mzinga yote inatokana na ka-app kameitwa Auni, kale Fastagger, startup ya hapa Nairobi, wamechonga. Hii Auni sasa imeingia ndani ya M-Pesa Business Super App na inafanya kazi kama kichwa kubwa. Unajua vile ma-biz hupigana na spreadsheets na guesswork? Hiyo story imeisha!

Njoki Njoroge, dame wa Mandevu Beard Care, amepiga luku poa sana. Anasema, “Saa hii najua wateja wangu wa repeat, masaa ya peak ya sales, na wapi demand iko juu. Huwezi bahatisha tena, una-know!” Peter Chege, wa Master Stylists Hair Salon, naye amepiga tafu, akisema hii tool inamsaidia ku-track customer frequency na spending patterns. Hii imemfanya apige discounts smart na hata a-staff poa. Hii visibility imemfanya aongeze revenue na hata afungue branch ingine!

Kariuki Mutembi, CEO wa Fastagger, amebonga ukweli akisema Auni inachukua M-Pesa PDF statements na kuziturn into structured dashboards, akitumia optical character recognition na lightweight AI models. Hizi AI models zimechongwa kukimbia kwa simu za bei poa na hata mahali connectivity ni doro. So, hata kama huna IT skills, unaweza jua sales patterns, cash flow trends, na customer behavior bila stress. Johnnie Walker Wamemwaga Bundles!](/posts/nyashinski-njerae-mko-2026-johnnie-walker-bundles), hizi biz ndogo ndogo pia zimepata 'bundles' zao - bundles za insights na profits!

Hii Auni ime-onboard *more than 3,500 MSMEs* ndani ya miezi tatu tu. Hii ni proof kwamba AI si ya mzinga corporations pekee, sasa imeshuka mpaka street level. Na si story za jaba, hii Auni inarun na Microsoft Azure, so unaona tu ni technology ya maana hapa.

**The Verdict**

Na vile MSMEs zinachukua 90% ya biashara za Africa na 70% ya employment, hii AI inaweza piga tafu resilience, kulinda jobs, na kuongeza profit kibao. Mutembi Kariuki amesema hata five percent increase kwa customer retention inaweza translate to 25% profit boost.

Fastagger hawajaishia hapo. Wanasema watapeleka Auni kwa healthcare, manufacturing, na agriculture. Na pia wanataka kuleta generative AI, ndio ma-biz owners waweze kuongea na app na kupata majibu on the spot. Hii ni kama kupata advisor wa biashara kwa mfuko wako, anytime, anywhere. So, kwa ma-biz ndogo za Nairobi, AI si tu buzzword tena - ni partner wa ukweli, akiturn transactions za kila siku kuwa decisions za kichwa kubwa na growth opportunities. Hii ni kipindupindu!
