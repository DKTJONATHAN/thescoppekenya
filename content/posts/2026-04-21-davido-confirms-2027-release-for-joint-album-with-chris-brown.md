Nigerian Afrobeats sensation Davido has revealed a potential late 2027 release for his highly anticipated joint album with American hitmaker Chris Brown. The announcement came during an interview held on the sidelines of his Coachella 2026 Day 2 performance, confirming years of fan speculation about the project.

## Davido and Chris Brown's Anticipated Collaboration

The confirmed timeline for the joint album ends nearly two years of anticipation, with the project now slated for late 2027. Fans should note that this significant collaboration will be preceded by solo album releases from both artists. Davido's interview marks the first time a potential release date has been publicly discussed, amplifying excitement across Pan-Africa and beyond.

### A Long-Awaited Project

Speaking during a recent interview, Davido expressed his enthusiasm for the upcoming work, stating that he and Chris Brown have enough songs together for multiple albums. He has consistently lauded Chris Brown's talent, describing him as one of the greatest performers alive. This revelation of a 2027 release date suggests that the long-teased project is firmly on track, promising a landmark moment for Afrobeats and global music.

### A History of Hit Records

The creative partnership between Davido and Chris Brown dates back to 2019 when they joined forces on the smash hit 'Blow My Mind,' a lead-up single for Davido's album 'A Good Time.' Chris Brown reciprocated the collaboration in the same year, featuring Davido on 'Lower Body' from his album 'Indigo.' Their collaborations continued to flourish; in 2020, Davido featured Chris Brown and American rapper Young Thug on 'Shopping Spree,' a single from his third album 'A Better Time.' Two years later, the duo reunited for 'Nobody Needs To Know' on Chris Brown's 2022 album 'Breezy.' One of their biggest successes arrived in 2023 with 'Sensational,' featuring Nigerian star Lojay, which earned both Davido and Lojay their first entry on the Billboard Hot 100 and a nomination for the Best African Song Performance Award at the 2024 Grammys. Their most recent collaboration was in 2024 on 'Hmm,' an Afropop party starter featured on Chris Brown's album '11:11.' This extensive list of successful tracks underscores their profound synergy, effectively translating into hit records spanning Afrobeats, R&B, and Pop genres.

### Anticipation Builds for Solo Releases and Beyond

As the joint project awaits its 2027 release, both artists are actively engaged in individual endeavors. Davido is currently navigating the European leg of his highly successful 5ive Alive Tour. Meanwhile, Chris Brown recently announced a joint tour with American R&B icon Usher. Before their collaborative album, Chris Brown's 12th studio album, titled 'BROWN,' is scheduled for release on May 10, 2026. Davido has also shared plans for his next solo album, indicating a return to his Nigerian and African roots. The prospect of a joint tour between the two global sensations is also an exciting possibility for fans, eager to witness their combined stage presence.
