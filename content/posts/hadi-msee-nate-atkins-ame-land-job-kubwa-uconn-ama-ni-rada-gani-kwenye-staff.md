## Scoop Ni Gani?
Wasee wangu wa mtaa, leo tuko na story moto sana kutoka states, juu ya msee Nate Atkins. Jamaa alijoin UConn kama assistant defensive line coach kuanzia 2025. Kweli, ilikuwa move noma sana, ku-level up hivi kutoka Ball State. Lakini sasa vile tuko 2026, rada ni ati kwa staff ya UConn 2026, jamaa ameandikwa kama "Defensive Analyst". Hii si mchezo bana, meaning jamaa bado ako strong kwa system, labda tu amebadili gear.
