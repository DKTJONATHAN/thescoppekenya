**The Scoop**

Wasee! Fom ni hivi: Charles Okwemba, yule legend wetu wa Harambee Stars, ameamua kuweka mambo wazi. Tumenusa hii story mapema leo, February 17, 2026, na Okwemba amezusha ngori mzito, akichapa FKF (Football Kenya Federation) makofi kwa standards duni za futa nchini. Anasema hii kitu ndio inafanya Kenya ikose kudunda kimataifa. Hii si mara ya kwanza mzee anatema cheche; amekuwa akipiga makelele kuhusu shida kwa system ya FKF kitambo.

**The Evidence**

Inspecta wetu wameconfirm kuwa si ndrama tu, evidence iko. Angalia tu venye Nairobi United, licha ya kudunda hadi group stages za CAF Confederations Cup, walimaliza chini kabisa ya Group B na zero points! Walipigwa teke February 15-16, 2026, na sasa wako out. Walikuwa wameingia hapo baada ya kushinda FKF Cup last year, lakini bado wakapigwa mangumi.

Na si wao pekee. Kenya Police FC, wale mabingwa wetu wa FKF Premier League wa 2024/2025, walitolewa mapema kwa CAF Champions League. Walikung'utwa na Al Hilal Omdurman mwezi wa Kumi, 2025, kwa round ya pili ya preliminary. Hii ilimaanisha walikosa mamita kibao, kama KSh 90.3 milioni, yenye ingeweza kuwasaidia sana. Kweli, shida iko deep na si mchezo. Okwemba amekuwa akisisitiza sana kuhusu youth structures na kulea talent mapema, kama vile tuliona kwa ile stori ya [Nairobi's PR Governor? Sakaja's](/posts/sakaja-omanyala-bahati-diana-nairobi-drama), ati tusipige makelele tu bila actions za maana.

**The Verdict**

Kwa kifupi, Okwemba anasema FKF na stakeholders wote wanahitaji kuingia box. Clubs, coaches, players, na ata fans, wote tuko kwa hii meli. Anataka turejeshe zile "glory days" za futa yetu. Harambee Stars pia wako na rada zao, wataingia uwanjani kwa FIFA Series mwezi March, 2026, wakijiandaa kwa AFCON 2027. Lakini kama standards za ligi na clubs zetu haziko sawa, tutaendelea kupigwa matuta na kulia kila mara. Ni wakati wa kuacha kupiga tu stories, na tufanye mambo. Futa yetu inahitaji resuscitation, na haraka!
