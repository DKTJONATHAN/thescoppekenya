**THE SCOOP**

Wadau, tumenusa hii story na imetuliza kabisa! Weekend ilipita, ma-star wetu wa Harambee Stars walikuwa waki-flex muscle zao kwa ligi za kigeni. Na si ku-flex tu, wengine wamefunga magoli kibao, wakishika neti kama wajanja. Hii ni stori ya wazito wawili, Kenneth Muguna na Zech Obiero, ambao wamefanya Wakenya waji-feel proud sana.

**THE EVIDENCE**

Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, Kenneth Muguna, yule msee wa midfield wa Harambee Stars, alikuwa *on fire* kwa club yake mpya ya Botswana, Sua Flamingos. Alipiga goli ya ushindi, dakika ya 90, kwa game yao ya Botswana Premier League waliposhinda Gaborone United 3-2 Saturday, 14 February. Unaona hiyo presha? Sua Flamingos walikuwa wameshikilia 2-1 halftime, Gaborone wakalevel 55th minute, alafu Muguna akaingia na 'ile kitu' dakika za mwisho. Alijiunga na Flamingos January, so hiyo ni kama *welcome party* yake! Na kwa hiyo win, Flamingos wameshikilia number four na 35 points, huku Gaborone United wakishuka number two na 44 points.

Alafu tukihepa huko England kwa League Two, Zech Obiero alishika neti pia. Huyu msee wa Tranmere Rovers alifungua 'scoring' kwa game yao waliposhinda Crawley Town 2-0. Obiero alikuwa *on point* dakika ya 12, akapiga goal yake ya kwanza kwa home debut yake, halafu Joe Ironside akaongeza ingine ya penalty dakika ya 45. Hiyo win imesaidia Tranmere kukwea mpaka 17th position na 35 points, huku Crawley wakishikilia 22nd na 26 points. Kusema ukweli, hawa maboys wametuletea heshima!

Lakini si wote walikuwa na weekend safi. Kwa CAF Champions League, Moses Shummah alistart kwa Power Dynamos, akacheza 64 minutes, lakini timu yake ya Zambia ilichapwa 3-1 na Pyramids FC kutoka Egypt. Power Dynamos wakaishia group A wakiwa number three na seven points, Pyramids na RS Berkane wakaenda quarters. Vile tuliona kwa ile stori ya [THE VILLAGE CIRCUS: Ruto and Gachagua’s Fake Hugs, Raila’s AUC Ghosting, and Eric Omondi Giving MPs Blood Pressure!](/posts/ruto-gachagua-drama-eric-omondi-political-tea), si kila drama inaisha na furaha. Wengine huishia na machungu!

Na kwa CAF Confederation Cup, Levis Opiyo, yule keeper wa zamani wa AFC Leopards, alikuwa goal kwa ZESCO United. Walichapwa 2-0 na Al Masry SC. Zesco wakaishia kampeni yao na three points tu, Al Masry SC na Zamalek SC wakaendelea.

Huko South Africa, Harambee Stars keeper Ian Otieno alicheza game yote, lakini Richards Bay walichapwa 3-0 na Chippa United. Brian Bwire yeye alikuwa tu benchi kwa Polokwane City walipotoa draw ya 0-0 na Siwelele FC. Polokwane wako 7th na 25 points, Richards Bay wako 10th na 19 points.

**THE VERDICT**

Ni wazi, Muguna na Obiero wameweka bendera ya Kenya juu sana weekend hii. Hao ndio ma-vibes tunataka kuona kutoka kwa ma-star wetu wa Harambee Stars. Wengine pia wamejitahidi, hata kama matokeo haikuwa poa sana. Hii inashow tu Wakenya tuna-represent kwa madaraja tofauti! Hii si mchezo, ni dedication!
