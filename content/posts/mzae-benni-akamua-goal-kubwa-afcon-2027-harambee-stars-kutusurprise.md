**The Scoop:**
Hii hapa mambo imechemka kwa mtaa! Mzae Benni McCarthy, yule mzito wetu wa Harambee Stars, ameweka target ya maana sana kwa mbogi yetu pale AFCON 2027. Tumenusa hii story Pan-Africa Football wamesketch, na inaonekana mambo si mchezo. Wasee wetu wanataka kufanya make nyumbani!

**The Evidence:**
Sasa, inspecta wetu wamechambua kauli za Mzae Benni. Anasema Harambee Stars lazima watoke group stage. Si mchezo! Hii AFCON inafanyika hapa kwetu, tuki-host na Uganda na Tanzania, starting June 19 hadi July 18, 2027. Hii ni mara ya kwanza nchi tatu zina-host, na ya kwanza kwa CECAFA region after Ethiopia ilifanya make 1976.

Mzae Benni anasema Kenya ni "sleeping giant" na kuna talents kibao ameshuhudia. "Wasee wetu wana mentality poa, work ethic noma, na running power ndefu," McCarthy aliongea na CMTV ya South Africa, kama Pulse Sports walimnukuu. Anaona progress ni poa, na kwa sababu tuko home, Round of 16 ni minimum.
Anasema anapray wasipate "Group of Death" na timu kama Morocco, Ivory Coast, ama Senegal, juu quality bado hatufikii hapo. Lakini kama group ni modest, basi mambo ni set kufanya make kama vile walifanya CHAN, walipofika Round of 16 kabla wapigwe na Madagascar kwa penalti.

McCarthy anataka football hapa Kenya iwe on par na athletics, juu talents ziko.

So, wasee, mambo imeanza kunukia moto! Mzae Benni ameweka benchmark ya maana, na inaonyesha anabuy into the vision ya Kenya kuwa power house ya football.
