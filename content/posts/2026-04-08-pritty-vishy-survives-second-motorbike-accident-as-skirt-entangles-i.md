Content creator Pritty Vishy narrowly escaped serious injury on Wednesday, April 8, 2026, after her long skirt entangled in a motorbike's rear wheel on the Thika Superhighway, causing a crash. This marks her second road incident in two months, reigniting her urgent appeal for a personal car to avoid future "bodaboda" mishaps.

Pritty Vishy's Harrowing Highway Ordeal

Skirt Entangles, Chaos Ensues The incident unfolded shortly after Pritty Vishy had concluded a session with her makeup artist. While en route to her next commitment, her lengthy red skirt unfortunately became entangled in the motorbike's rear tire. The sudden entanglement caused the vehicle to lose stability and crash on the busy Thika Superhighway. The force of the wheel completely shredded her skirt, leaving the content creator exposed. Fortunately, bystanders quickly came to her aid, with one individual offering a hoodie and cap for cover.

A Humorous Take Amidst the Tumble Despite the alarming fall, Pritty Vishy maintained her characteristic humor, light-heartedly suggesting the accident seemed to target her physique. "My BBL was the main target of the accident," she quipped while displaying the tattered remnants of her outfit. Her resilience shone through as she swiftly found new clothes and insisted on continuing her errands, much to the amazement of her motorbike rider, who had offered medicine and water out of concern.
