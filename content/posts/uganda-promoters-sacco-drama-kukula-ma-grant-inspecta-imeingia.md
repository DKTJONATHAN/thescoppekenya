## Scoop Ni Gani?
Ma-jamaa wa mziki industry Uganda wako kwa ngori njoro! Tumenusa hii story kinyama, ati the Uganda Music Promoters Savings and Credit Cooperative Society (SACCO) imepigwa inspection after whistleblowers kudai kulikuwa na illegal takeover, governance breaches, na mismanagement ya ma-board na management yao. Hii si mchezo, maana inaguza directly ile role muhimu ma-promoters hucheza kwa entertainment scene ya Uganda - from kuorganize concerts hadi kubook acts za kimataifa na kusupport local talent.

## Evidence Ni?
Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, in a letter ya February 18, 2026, Ministry of Trade, Industry and Cooperatives waliannounce hii inspection baada ya kupokea hizo complains za mismanagement. Registrar wa Cooperative Societies, Robert Bariyo Barigye, ndiye alidunga hiyo directive. Kwanza walikuwa wamepanga hii inspection ya February 9 but ikadeferred coz officials walikuwa wameitwa State House Anti-Corruption Unit (SHACU) hiyo same date. Sasa, inspection imereschedulewa leo, Friday, February 20, 2026, saa nne asubuhi pale kwa offices zao Kitebi, Rubaga Division. Hawajamaliza hapo, ati saa nane mchana kutakuwa na harmonization meeting kati ya Board of Directors na wale lead petitioners.

Hii si mara ya kwanza mabeans ya promoters inaleta sakatta. Inaripotiwa, mwezi uliopita tu, kulikuwa na drama ya sh750m ilikuliwa, funds zimetoka kwa presidential grant ilikuwa meant kusupport promoters. Events promoter Nobert Twizire (Nobert Events) alidunga petition January 20, 2026, akidai Shs750b ilitolewa kutoka kwa kila account ya ma-SACCO mbili kwa personal gain badala ya kuwa revolving fund. President Yoweri Kaguta Museveni alikuwa ameamrisha Shs10b ipewe ma-SACCO za promoters, na kwa round ya kwanza serikali ilidunga sh1.5b kwa kila SACCO.

Kuna time hadi 12 leaders wa ma-SACCO waliaffirm kurudisha hiyo sh750m within six months. Kila member alikubali kulipa sh62.5m, walikuwa wamechukua kutoka kwa SACCO accounts. Hii ni mambo ilipangwa kwa meeting iliyoitwa na Minister wa State for Youth and Children Affairs, Balaam Barugahara Ateenyi. Government ilidunga ati funds zitatoka kwa phases na stronger financial controls na Annual General Meeting itaitwa kuappoint new bank signatories.

Uganda Music Promoters SACCO inawachwa na Abbey Musinguzi, anajulikana kama Abtex. Board members wengine ni Paul Katongole, Godfrey Mayanja, Yasin Kaweesi na Zaidi Kafeero. Hawa majamaa wako kwa rada kinyama. Na si hao pekee, unaona hata kule kwetu, mambo ya deals za wasanii na mapesa inaweza kuchemka mbaya. Sasa ona hii ya Uganda inazidi kuwa noma.

## Verdict Yetu Ni
Hii inspection inaweza kucarry weight kubwa sana kwa future ya hii SACCO na inaweza kurudisha confidence kwa members. Lakini kwa vile tumenusa hii sakatta ya mabeans ya government ikikuliwa na 'few individuals', inatoa picha mbaya sana. Kama The Nairobi Street Insider, tunaona hii ni lesson to all creative industry players; accountability ni kitu muhimu sana, si mchezo. Maboss wa SACCO wanahitaji kusafisha majina zao haraka, ama hii story itaendelea kuwa noma sana kwa mziki scene ya Uganda. Receipts ziko, na ukweli utabaki wazi, hakuna mahali itafichwa.
