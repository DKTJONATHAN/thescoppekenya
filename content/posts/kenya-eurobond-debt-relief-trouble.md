**The Scoop**
Okay, so the government imerudi kwa soko ya pesa ya dunia. Wamesema wamepata $2.25 billion, hiyo ni kama KSh 290 billion jo. The whole point ni ati itatusaidia kulipa madeni na ku-finance budget yetu.

**The Evidence**
Walai, hii loan ni ya aina mbili. Tranche ya kwanza ni $900 million, inafaa kulipwa 2034 na interest rate ni 7.8% - hiyo ni figure kubwa sana, manze. Alafu ile ingine kubwa, $1.35 billion, inafika 2039 na interest yake ni 8.7%. Imagine! Hii pesa yote, ati mostly ni ya kulipa madeni za zamani, especially zile Eurobonds zetu za karibu ku-mature. Wanasema kama hatungefanya hivi, tungeweka nchi kwa shida kubwa ya default. Lakini sasa, na hizi interest rates, experts wanasema hii ni kama kununua muda ghali sana. KRA imewekwa presha. **The Verdict**
End of the day, financial gurus wanasema hii ni kama 'tactical victory' for Treasury technocrats, but for us, the common mwananchi, it's just a 'stay of execution'. The real hustle ya ku-fix uchumi ndio inaanza sasa, manze. Lazima government ipunguze matumizi, i-privatize zile makampuni za serikali hazifanyi poa, na tu-boost export ili tupate dollar za kutosha kulipa hii deni.
