## Scoop Ni Gani?
Wasee wa U2, wale macomrade wa Ireland, wame-shake dunia na kutoa EP yao mpya inaitwa "Days of Ash" juzi tu Wednesday. Hii si EP ya kawaida, manze. Ngoma ya kwanza, "American Obituary," imemwagwa kama tribute kwa Renee Good, mzae wa watoto watatu aliyepigwa risasi na kufa huko Minneapolis. Renee, dem wa miaka 37, alimalizwa na ma-agent wa federal immigration, yaani ICE, mnamo January 7, 2026.

U2 wamefuata nyayo za wasanii wengine kama Bruce Springsteen na Billie Eilish wakifanya statement kali. Bono, the frontman, ameshika mic na kuimba lyrics kali, akisema, "Renee Good, born to die free. American mother of three. Seventh day, January. A bullet for each child, you see." Hii EP pia ina ngoma zingine zinazoongelea mambo ya Gaza, Iran, na Ukraine, ikionyesha vile U2 hawajawahi piga corner kwa mambo ya human rights. Kuna pia collabo na Ed Sheeran na Taras Topolia wa Ukraine kwa track "Yours Eternally," na kuna docu itatoka February 24 ku-mark miaka nne ya invasion ya Russia kwa Ukraine.

## Evidence Ni?
Kama vile tumenoki, kifo ya Renee Good haikuwa mchezo. Huyu alikuwa citizen wa US, mshairi na mwandishi, na alikuwa akijitolea ku-alert majirani wake kuhusu operation za ICE. Video za incident zilionyesha ICE agent Jonathan Ross akimpiga risasi tatu Renee Good akiwa kwa gari, akijaribu kuondoka. Hii kitu ilichoma sana, na ma-protest yalinuka nchi nzima.

Wazito wa serikali na President Trump walijaribu ku-defend agent, wakisema Good alijaribu kumgonga na gari. Lakini, mashuhuda na videos zimelalia hiyo story, zikionyesha ukweli ni mwingine. Hata mama na partner wake Renee Good, Becca, wamesema Renee alikuwa mtu wa upendo na angefurahia U2 kuongea ukweli kwa ngoma. Sad story ingine ni ati memorial yake Renee Good huko Minneapolis ilichomwa moto juzi tu, February 17, 2026. Hii inatuonyesha vile tension bado iko juu.

## Verdict Yetu Ni
Hawa U2 wameconfirm tena vile wao ni legends kwa activism. Kutoa hii EP haraka hivi, wamesema wenyewe, "These EP tracks couldn't wait." Bono amesema hii ngoma si tu hasira, ni huzuni, "grief for the death of an America that at the very least would have had an inquiry into her killing." Hii inatupea signal kali vile wasanii wanaweza tumia platform zao kuongelea mambo mazito, si tu entertainment.

Hawa U2 wamekuwa kwa rada ya activism kitambo, si mchezo. Daima kuna pressure ya kusimama na ukweli. U2 wanatuonyesha kuwa hata kama tuko busy na maisha, lazima tufunue macho kwa chenye kinaendelea. Wamesema, "These are songs of defiance and dismay, of lamentation." Hii story ni moto, na inatukumbusha umuhimu wa kusimama na watu, hasa wale wanyonge.
