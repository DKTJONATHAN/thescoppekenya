## Dr. Ofweneke Reacts After Daughter Faith Calls Him Bro

### A Comedic Culture Shock
The celebrated entertainer, known for his quick wit and stage presence, found himself at a loss for words when his daughter bridged the generational gap with a single word. Faith, who is currently navigating her teenage years, seems to have adopted the popular Gen Z lingo that treats every relationship with a level of informal camaraderie. This interaction highlights the evolving dynamics between Kenyan parents and their children in 2026, where traditional boundaries often meet modern slang. ### The Price of Premium Education
Ofweneke jokingly lamented the irony of paying high tuition fees only to be addressed as a peer. The comedian pointed out that while he provides the best for his children, the cultural influence of social media and school environments remains dominant. He noted that the term "Bro" has become a universal identifier for Gen Z, regardless of the recipient's status or relation. This lighthearted family moment reflects a broader trend among urban Kenyan families who find themselves balancing traditional respect with contemporary global trends. While the comedian continues his busy schedule, including tracking infrastructure updates like when [President Ruto announces construction of Ksh400 million Suneka Airport](https://zandani.co.ke/article/president-ruto-announces-construction-of-ksh400-million-suneka-airpo), his primary focus remains the entertaining evolution of his own household.

Ofweneke's ability to turn a personal parenting moment into a relatable comedy bit has once again cemented his status as a favorite in the entertainment industry.
