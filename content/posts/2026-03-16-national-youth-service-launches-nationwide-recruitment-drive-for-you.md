The National Youth Service (NYS) has initiated a comprehensive recruitment drive across all sub-counties, commencing Monday, March 16, and concluding Saturday, March 21, 2026. This exercise aims to integrate young people into national service, offering valuable skills and training opportunities within various vocational fields.

NYS Recruitment Details

Current Recruitment Exercise The nationwide recruitment officially began today, March 16, 2026, as previously announced. Eligible young individuals are urged to present themselves at their designated sub-county recruitment centers. This program provides an opportunity to contribute to the nation while acquiring essential skills, discipline, and professional training. The National Youth Service functions as a state corporation dedicated to youth empowerment through paramilitary, national service, and vocational education.

Skills and Opportunities Through its structured programs, the NYS equips recruits with vital technical and vocational skills. These include expertise in construction, agriculture, mechanical works, and community development. The agency strategically deploys its trained personnel in significant national development projects, crucial disaster response efforts, environmental conservation initiatives, and public infrastructure works. This commitment to national service aligns with broader governmental preparedness, as seen in efforts to navigate challenges like heavy rainfall and thunderstorms across Nairobi and Western. Earlier notices from the NYS detailed a recruitment drive for over 700 vacancies, comprising both cadet and private officer roles. A total of 250 cadet officer positions and 500 private posts were announced on Tuesday, March 3, 2026, offering significant avenues for employment and skill acquisition.

Application Process The advertised private posts encompass diverse fields such as drivers, plant operators, electricians, carpenters, plumbers, band members, agricultural workers, secretaries, vehicle mechanics, welders, and general duty personnel. Prospective applicants must download and complete the official Application for Employment Form, available on the Public Service Commission website. Submissions can be made via email or delivered by post to the NYS Commandant General at the National Youth Service Headquarters in Nairobi. The final deadline for application submission is March 18, 2026.
