## George Clooney's Lake Como Villa Grabs a "Prada" Cameo

It seems even celebrity real estate can achieve stardom without its owner's knowledge! George Clooney was charmingly surprised to discover his breathtaking Villa Oleandra on Lake Como makes a prominent appearance in the upcoming *The Devil Wears Prada 2*. The iconic Italian estate features in the film's trailer, much to the delight of fans and apparently, Clooney himself.

### A Star is Born: The Villa Edition

Clooney, ever the good sport, reacted with humor to the news on Monday at the 51st annual Chaplin Award Gala in New York City. "Is it really? Oh man, I've made it," he quipped to *Access Hollywood*. The *Jay Kelly* star expressed his deep affection for the property, noting, "The house holds a special place in my heart, because I love going there." The film, set to premiere on May 1, 2026, has already generated buzz thanks to its early trailer release, which prominently features the picturesque villa as Anne Hathaway and Emily Blunt speedboat across Lake Como.

### A Dear Friend's Connection

The surprise cameo is even sweeter for Clooney due to his close friendship with Emily Blunt, one of the sequel's stars. "The whole cast! Has it come out? It's going to be a big hit," Clooney enthusiastically shared. Blunt herself has a significant history with Villa Oleandra, revealing on *The Howard Stern Show* in 2023 that she and husband John Krasinski tied the knot there in July 2010. The wedding even saw a partial *Devil Wears Prada* reunion, with none other than Meryl Streep in attendance.

### The Million Dollar View

Clooney's connection to Villa Oleandra dates back to 2002 when he purchased the property. Now valued at an estimated $100 million, the sprawling 100,000 sq. ft. estate in Laglio boasts an 18th-century history, complete with its own pier, swimming pool, and tennis court. It was also on the scenic banks of Lake Como that Clooney famously met his wife, human rights lawyer Amal Alamuddin, who became Mrs. Amal Clooney in 2014.
