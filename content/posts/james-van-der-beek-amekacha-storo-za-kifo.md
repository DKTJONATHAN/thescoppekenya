**THE SCOOP**
Tumenusa hii story, na ni ukweli mtupu: James Van Der Beek, yule msee alikua anashikilia screen kama Dawson Leery kwa "Dawson's Creek", ameacha dunia akiwa na miaka 48. Hii imepiga mtaa kama radi!

**THE RECEIPTS**
Inspecta wetu wameconfirm: hii ripoti ya kifo imetoka Texas. Dem wake, Kimberly, ndiye amekuja public na hii news kwa social media, akisema mzae wao James David Van Der Beek amepumzika salama. Amesema alikufa peacefully na alikua na courage, faith, na grace hadi mwisho.

Receipts ziko: walisema alikua na shida za afya, na sahii tumegundua, alikua anapambana na stage 3 colorectal cancer tangu 2023. Alitangaza hii mambo public late 2024 tu!

Huyu msee alikua celeb wa ukweli from 1998 mpaka 2003, akishikilia show ya The WB kama icon wa "Dawson's Creek". Pia alifanya movies kama *Varsity Blues* na *The Rules of Attraction*.

Ata kama watu walimjua sana na ile 'crying meme' yake - yenye yeye mwenyewe alikua anajokes nayo - alikua ameshikilia heshima kwa acting na sense ya humour. Mwaka wake wa mwisho, aligeuka champion wa cancer awareness. Ata kama alishindwa kuattend reunion ya "Dawson's Creek" September 2025, alituma video message akishukuru ma-fan wake.

Ameacha bibi yake Kimberly na mabarn wake sita. Ma-fan kwa social media wanatuma tribute kibao, wakisema 'Dawson alitufunza ku-dream big.' Hii ni hasara kubwa kwa Gen X na Millennials wenye walikulia na yeye.
