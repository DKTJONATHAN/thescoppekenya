Airtel Kenya is incentivising its customers to refer new users to the MyAirtel App, offering Ksh100 in cash for each successful referral. This "Refer and Earn" programme provides a straightforward opportunity for mobile users aged 18 and above to accumulate earnings by expanding the app's user base.

## MyAirtel App Referral Programme Details

The "Refer and Earn" programme distributes Ksh20 to the referrer's Airtel Money wallet monthly over five months, totaling Ksh100 per referral. For these monthly payments to be processed, the referred individual must complete transactions worth at least Ksh100 via Airtel Money each month, specifically by purchasing airtime or data bundles directly through the application. Consistency is key; should 50 referred friends consistently meet this condition, a referrer can accumulate Ksh5,000.

The programme requires customers to share a unique referral link or code, which can be disseminated through various communication platforms, including WhatsApp. Earnings are credited each time a friend successfully downloads and registers on the app using the shared link or code.

### Incentives and Exclusive Offers

To further encourage participation, Airtel Kenya is providing new users with 1GB of free data upon downloading and registering the MyAirtel App. This dual incentive structure aims to benefit both the referrer and the new user, providing a tangible reason for engagement.

Beyond the referral scheme, the MyAirtel App hosts a range of exclusive offers not accessible through other channels. These include a 2GB data bundle for Ksh99, valid for three days, and a Midnight Bundle offering 2GB for Ksh50, valid until midnight, catering to late-night internet usage. A unique 4GB data bundle for Ksh99 also provides significant value. Users can purchase these bundles and airtime using Airtel Money, debit/credit cards, or other channels, with Airtel Money purchases earning an additional 25 per cent extra airtime. The additional income generated through referrals can also support users' digital lifestyle, whether it's for purchasing more data to stream content or engaging with popular cultural phenomena like the renewed interest in [The Mummy 4: How the Reunion of Brendan Fraser and Rachel Weisz is Igniting a Millennial Fan Frenzy](https://zandani.co.ke/article/the-mummy-4-reunited-brendan-fraser-and-rachel-weisz).

### Enhanced App Functionality

The MyAirtel App positions itself as a comprehensive tool for managing an Airtel line. It has consolidated Airtel Money services into a single, organised section, and features Global Scan & Pay prominently on the home screen for expedited access. Users also have the option to display or hide their Airtel Money balance on the main screen for enhanced privacy.

Through the application, customers can execute a wide array of transactions, including purchasing airtime and data bundles, sending money across all networks, making payments to millions of merchants countrywide, and settling bills. It also supports real-time management of Home Broadband accounts via the Selfcare App. Postpaid bill payments are facilitated for both personal numbers and other postpaid accounts, alongside bill settlement with registered merchants using Lipa na Airtel Money. Furthermore, the app enables users to deposit funds into their Airtel Money wallet through various methods, including Bank to Wallet transfers and Airtel Money Agents.

To participate, users need to download MyAirtel App, navigate to the "Refer and Earn" section, and share their unique referral link or code with friends and family.
