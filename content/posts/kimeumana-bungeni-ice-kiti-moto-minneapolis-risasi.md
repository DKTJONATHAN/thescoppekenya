Si walikuwa wameenda tu kutoa greetings, hapana! Walikuwa wameitwa kuwekwa kiti moto, kuchapwa maswali za kizito kuhusu vile wamekuwa wakifinyilia sheria za ma-mgeni, hasa baada ya hizo ma-risasi za Minneapolis zimeshikanisha!

**The Evidence: Madeni ya Kinyama na Kujitetea kwa Nguvu!**

Sources zetu za ndani zinasema, Acting ICE Director Todd Lyons, Commissioner wa CBP Rodney Scott, na Director wa USCIS Joseph Edlow ndio walikuwa wamefunguka. Lakini hekaya ilianza kuwa mbaya after ma-federal agents walishoot na kuua ma-U.S. citizens wawili, Renee Good na Alex Pretti, huko Minneapolis mwezi uliopita. Ma-Democrats walikuwa wamekasirika vibaya sana, wakishika ma-officials maswali ngumu kuhusu hizo tactics za enforcement na vile wanatekeleza sheria.

Hii sasa ndio rada yetu imeleta:

1.  **Kukataa Kuomba Msamaha**: Inspecta wetu wameconfirm kwamba Acting ICE Director Todd Lyons alikataa kabisa kuomba msamaha kwa familia za Good na Pretti. Alisema tu mambo ya "ongoing investigations" na "nitawasalimia kwa private." Hii ilikasirisha ma-mhesh Democrats vibaya sana.
2.  **Ma-Deportation za Nguvu**: Lyons alijitetea, akisema ma-officers wake wako fiti na hawataogopa kuendelea na "mass deportation plans" za President Trump. Tena akajisifia ati kwa mwaka moja pekee, wameshatoa watu zaidi ya 475,000 nje ya nchi! Hii ni mzinga, maze!
3.  **Border Security na Ma-Protest**: Commissioner Scott wa CBP naye alijigamba ati wameshikilia border, na illegal entries zimekuwa record-low. Lakini pia alicomplain eti ma-protest yamekuwa yakishikanisha kazi yao vibaya sana, "unprecedented in scale and intensity."
4.  **Madeni ya Secretari wa DHS**: Hata Secretary wa Homeland Security, Kristi Noem, alikuwa ameshikwa deni. Alikuwa amesema uongo kuhusu Pretti kupigwa risasi na hata kumuita Renee Good "domestic terrorist." Kumbe, stori za uongo zilianza kwa ma-top!
5.  **Pesa Zimekwama**: Hizi hearing zimefanyika wakati ambapo funding ya DHS imeshika mate. Ma-Democrats wanasema hawatafund DHS bila reforms za kutosha kwa immigration enforcement tactics, kama vile kuwakataza ma-agents kuvaa masks na kuwafukuza huko kwa ma-schools, churches, na medical facilities.
