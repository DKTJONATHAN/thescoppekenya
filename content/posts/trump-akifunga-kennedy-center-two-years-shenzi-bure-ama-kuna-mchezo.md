Wasee, mambo imechangamka Washington D.C.! President Donald Trump, mzee wetu wa America, ameweka kamba kwa Kennedy Center, ati ati kwa miaka miwili mzima kuanzia July 4, 2026. Wacha nicheshe! Anasema ni "Construction, Revitalization, and Complete Rebuilding" ya "tired, broken, and dilapidated Center". Ati itakuwa "World Class Bastion of Arts, Music, and Entertainment". Ai!

Lakini wajanja wa mtaani wanasema hii ni mchezo tu ya ego, buda. Kumbuka huyu mzee alichukua Kennedy Center February 2025, akabadilisha board yote na akajiweka chairman. Halafu December 2025, board yake mpya ikapiga kura kubadilisha jina, ikaitwa "Trump-Kennedy Center." Eh, unaskia hiyo?

Sasa, Michael Wolff, yule mwandishi wa vitabu za Trump, ametoa siri nzito! Kwa podcast yake "Inside Trump's Head," Wolff amesema ati original plan ya Trump ilikuwa kuitwa tu "Trump Center." Na kwanini? Trump alidai eti kwa sababu alinusurika "assassination attempts" 2024, anastahili heshima zaidi ya Kennedy! Yaani, "I was almost assassinated. Therefore, it should be me," eti. Hapa ni mambo ya kiburi tu, wajameni!

Arts community imekasirika vibaya. Tangu Trump aanze kuchafua mambo na kubadilisha jina, wasanii wengi wakubwa wamekataa kutumbuiza huko, na ticket sales ziko chini kabisa. Imagine, staff wa Kennedy Center walisikia habari ya kufungwa kwa Center kupitia Truth Social ya Trump, hakuna communication rasmi kabla ya hapo! Lakini National Symphony Orchestra wameahidiwa funding yao itaendelea na watafutiwa sehemu nyingine ya kuperform.

Na kama haitoshi, Trump anatishia kumsue Michael Wolff na Epstein estate, akidai walikuwa "conspiring... to hurt me, politically or otherwise." Huyu mzee anataka kuchoma kila mtu anayemkosoa! Wasee, hii drama ya Kennedy Center ni kama movie. Tutawaambia chenye kitaendelea!
