Popular Gen Z influencer, Biko, found his April 9, 2026, TikTok live stream abruptly interrupted as Kenya Power scheduled widespread outages across Nairobi. Fans were left wondering how the digital creator would navigate a day without his usual electric glow, especially in his Parklands studio, for nearly nine hours.

Nairobi's Power Puzzle Puts Stars to the Test

Unplugged and Unscripted The planned eight-to-nine-hour power interruption across Nairobi's Parklands, Umoja 2, and areas in Kitengela on April 9, 2026, sent a ripple through the city's vibrant celebrity circles. While Biko scrambled for a power bank to continue his online presence, other well-known personalities across the nation faced their own challenges. A prominent fashion designer in Nyali, Mombasa County, for instance, had to reschedule client fittings, relying on natural light. Similarly, a popular restauranteur in Keroka Town, Kisii County, known for their vibrant evening atmosphere, swiftly shifted to a gas-powered kitchen and candlelit dining, promising a "rustic chic" experience for patrons during the 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. outage. Areas like Thangathi in Nyeri and Kibichoi in Kiambu also saw their daily routines disrupted.

The Digital Divide and Diversification For many of Kenya's entertainment figures, a significant portion of their brand and income streams are deeply intertwined with their digital presence. From managing online merchandise stores to engaging with global fan bases, consistent connectivity is paramount. The current outages, which also impacted areas like Migwani in Kitui County and Masalani in Tana River County, highlight the vulnerability of digital ventures to infrastructural challenges. Some stars, already forward-thinking, have been exploring diversification, not just in income sources but also in how they manage their assets. The ongoing discussions around regulating digital finance, such as when Kenya Treasury proposes draft regulations for virtual asset service providers, become particularly relevant when traditional systems face disruptions. This pushes celebrities to consider resilient financial strategies beyond the typical.
