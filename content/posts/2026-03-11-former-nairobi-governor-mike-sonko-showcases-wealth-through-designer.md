## Mike Sonko's Luxe Life: Designer Drip and Cutting-Edge Tech Former Nairobi Governor Mike Sonko continues to command attention with his extravagant lifestyle. From head-to-toe designer ensembles by Versace and Gucci to pioneering luxury technology like the new Samsung Galaxy Z TriFold, Sonko's opulent choices solidify his reputation as a connoisseur of high-end living and an early adopter of elite gadgets.

The Tri-Fold Revolution Sonko consistently pushes the boundaries of luxury, now venturing into the realm of elite technology. On March 10, 2026, he reportedly became the first individual in East and Central Africa to acquire the groundbreaking new Samsung Galaxy Z TriFold. This rare device features a unique tri-folding display, confirming his status as an early adopter of cutting-edge tech. His enthusiasm for advanced gadgets extends beyond phones; one can only imagine his interest in innovations like the [Samsung Galaxy Buds4 Pro With AI Call Boost: A New Era for Wireless Audio](https://zandani.co.ke/article/samsung-galaxy-buds4-pro-ai-call-boost), showcasing a new era for wireless audio.

Wardrobe Wonders and Designer Declarations The former governor's "matching" philosophy is legendary. He often commits to a single designer brand, adorning himself from hat to socks in labels like Versace and Gucci. In 2023, he offered a glimpse into his expansive walk-in wardrobe, describing it as a "one-stop shop" for coveted brands including Louis Vuitton and Supreme. This meticulous approach to fashion often makes headlines, even during serious occasions. In 2019, his appearance before the Kenya Revenue Authority in a full Versace ensemble, complete with a silk shirt costing an estimated Ksh 144,000, captured public imagination. Even during his December 2019 arrest, he was observed wearing a Versace t-shirt valued at a minimum of Sh53,000.

Timepieces and Triumphs of Taste Sonko's penchant for extravagance extends to his accessories. In 2024, he was seen sporting a Jacob & Co. Bugatti Chiron Tourbillon Titanium watch. This limited-edition timepiece is a true marvel, carrying a staggering value of over Ksh 52 million. His generosity is equally grand; during a 2019 visit to the United Arab Emirates, he procured a Versace necklace for his wife, Primrose, at a price of Ksh 4.3 million.

Wheels of Fortune and Pockets of Plenty His mode of transport is as flamboyant as his attire. Sonko has been seen in a gold-trimmed Mercedes G-Wagon, often as part of a luxurious convoy. By March 2026, reports indicated a substantial car collection linked to him, featuring an array of Range Rovers and LX 570s. Beyond vehicles, he frequently shares videos showcasing considerable sums of money. In May 2023, he displayed suitcases brimming with Ksh 2 million in cash, which he casually referred to as mere pocket change.

A Legacy of Lavishness This flamboyant persona is not a recent phenomenon. Sonko first burst onto the national style scene in 2011. As the newly elected MP for Makadara, he was famously ejected from Parliament for wearing sunglasses and earrings. That moment cemented his public image for years to follow. Today, that image remains vibrant, characterized by gleaming gold, coveted designer labels, and the world’s most exclusive gadgets.
