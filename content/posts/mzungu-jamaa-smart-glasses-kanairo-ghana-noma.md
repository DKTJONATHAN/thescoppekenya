## Scoop Ni Gani?
Tumenusa hii story ikigonga vichwa maonline na kwa mtaa, na fom ni noma mbaya sana! Kumbe kuna huyu mzungu jamaa, jina lake Yaytseslav Trahov, amekuwa akipiga picha na videos za madem wa Kanairo na Ghana, tena bila hata wao kujua. Story imeanza kuviral after videos zake zikajaa mtandaoni, zikionyesha vile anachukua madem kwa public places kama malls na streets, anawapigia story, alafu anawashawishi waende kwa keja yake ya short-stay. Eti anawaconvince wakudondjo na yeye within hours tu ya kukutana. Hii kitu imewasha moto Kenya na Ghana, na wazee wa serikali wameingia box!

## Evidence Ni?
Inspecta wetu wameconfirm huyu jamaa anatumia miwani za akili, maybe Ray-Ban Meta glasses, kurecord all these encounters bila madem kuwa na rada. Receipts ziko, zikionyesha vile huyu mzee anafilmia madem huku Kanairo na hata majuu, hadi kwa coastal towns kama Mtwapa, Nyali na Diani. Hiyo video ya Quickmart Roysambu na yule mama wa watatu huko TRM, hizo zimetuliza ball sana. Hizi clips fupi amekuwa akizidrop TikTok na YouTube, but full-length videos ziko kwa Telegram channel yake ya kulipia, eti subscribers wanalipa $5 (Ksh650) per month kucheki "African Series" yake. Hii ni kama yule IShowSpeed alikuwa anapiga kiki huku kwetu, but huyu amechoma picha mbaya sana. Yake imetrend vibaya kwa reason mbaya. Madem wanatumiwa kama content bila consent, na hii ni kucall out! Wakenya wamekataa hii ujinga, na DCI imeambiwa ichukue action haraka. Ghana pia, Gender Ministry yao imesema hii ni serious criminal offence na ni violation ya privacy na human dignity, na wameamuru investigations zikick off mara moja.

## Verdict Yetu Ni
Kufilmia mtu bila consent ni kubant mbaya sana, na Kenya iko na sheria za kulinda privacy ya watu. Computer Misuse na Cybercrimes Act, na pia Data Protection Act, ziko hapa kukulinda wewe na mimi dhidi ya vitu kama hizi. Huyu jamaa Yaytseslav Trahov lazima ashikwe ajibu maswali. Hakuna mtu anafaa kutumiwa kama content online bila kujua, na hata kama sex ilikuwa consensual, kurecord na kudistribute sio. Hii ni digital sexual exploitation, na kama Nairobi Gen Z Reporter, nasema hapana! Madem wetu sio trophies za clicks, na dignity ni muhimu. Tukae rada na hizi smart gadgets, na tukumbuke, consent ni key kila wakati. Hii kiki yake imemchoma na haitawai sahulika.
