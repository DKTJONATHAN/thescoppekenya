## Scoop Ni Gani?
Leo hii, the Nairobi Street Insider tumeshika form mob inatisha. Ni kama kuna deal inakwenda mbaya sana, inahusu ma-Kenyans wetu wamepigwa nare kwenda kudunda vita huko Russia na Ukraine! Imagine, ripoti mpya ya Intelligence Service ya Kenya imesema zaidi ya ma-Kenyans elfu moja (1,000+) wametumwa huko front lines, wakitrickiwa na mabrokers "rogue" na ma-official wa Gava. Hii si mchezo, wasee!

## Evidence Ni?
Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, hii story si ya uongo. Ripoti inasema idadi ya ma-Kenyans huko vita imepanda kutoka 200 Novemba mpaka 1,000+ sahii. Wengine ati waliahidiwa ma-job za ufundi (electricians, plumbers) huko Russia, but kufika huko, wanapewa bunduki na kurushwa vitani na training kidogo ama hakuna kabisa!

Kimani Ichung'wah, yule Majority Leader wa Bunge, amepiga lipua akisema kuna network ya ma-official fisadi wa serikali wameshikana na ma-human traffickers kupiga shugli ya kupeleka maboys wetu huko. Hata staff wa Russian Embassy hapa Nairobi na Kenyan Embassy Moscow wamedaiwa kushikana na hii form. But wazito wa Russian Embassy wamekataa allegations, wakisema hawajawahi pewa visa za kwenda vita, ila tu, foreign citizens wanaweza jiunga na jeshi yao voluntarily.

Hii form ni ya kichapo! Ripoti inasema 89 wako front lines, 39 wako hospitalini, 28 wamepotea (missing in action), na mmoja amekufa! Wengine wamerudi home na majeraha ama mental trauma. Eti ma-Kenyans, mostly ex-police na ex-military, wenye umri wa 20s mpaka 50s, wanadesa mshahara wa around $2,700 (roughly KSh 350,000) per month, plus housing na bonus kibao, na hata citizenship ya Russia.

Na si ma-Kenyans pekee! Foreign Minister wa Ukraine, Andrii Sybiha, alikuwa amesema Novemba ati zaidi ya 1,400 Africans kutoka nchi 36 wanadunda huko vita for Russia. Wengi wao wameshika anga kama PoWs. Hata ma-families za recruits ziko inje zikilia Gava irudishe wao home. Hii inatisha, wasee. Ma-South Africans wengine walilurwiwa hivyo wameanza kurudi kwao.
