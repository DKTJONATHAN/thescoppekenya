A Kenya Power meter reader was seriously injured following an attack while on duty in Isiolo County today, Thursday, April 9, 2026. The employee sustained severe injuries and has since been airlifted to Nairobi for specialized medical attention, the company confirmed.

Attack on Kenya Power Employee A Kenya Power employee, working as a meter reader, suffered serious injuries after being assaulted while on duty in Isiolo County earlier today. The utility firm confirmed the incident and stated that the officer was airlifted to Nairobi this afternoon to receive specialized medical treatment for the sustained injuries.

Company Condemns Assault Kenya Power has issued a strong condemnation of the attack, describing the criminal act as unacceptable and detrimental to service delivery. The company emphasized that such assaults undermine their efforts to provide consistent electricity services to customers across the country.

The company noted that many officers and contractors are frequently targeted during disconnections for illegal connections or efforts to collect unpaid bills. The matter has been reported to the relevant authorities, and investigations have commenced. Kenya Power has appealed to the public for support to its workforce.
