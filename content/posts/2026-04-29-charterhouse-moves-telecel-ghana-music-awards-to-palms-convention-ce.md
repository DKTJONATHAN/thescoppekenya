Charterhouse Ghana has relocated the 2026 Telecel Ghana Music Awards from the Grand Arena to the Palms Convention Center. The shift comes as organisers faced unresolved arrangements at the Grand Arena, necessitating a swift change of plans for the anticipated annual ceremony. The new venue presents both solutions and challenges for the prestigious event.

## Telecel Ghana Music Awards Venue Change Confirmed

Robert Klah, Public Relations and Communications Officer at Charterhouse Ghana, confirmed that the 2026 Telecel Ghana Music Awards (TGMA) will be held at the Palms Convention Center following unforeseen difficulties with the Grand Arena, which has hosted the event for the past five years. Initial communications from the Grand Arena management indicated that key arrangements remained unfinalised, creating uncertainty for the organisers despite earlier optimism. This prompted Charterhouse to activate a backup plan and explore alternative venues, rigorously evaluating options based on high production standards, including location, parking, crowd movement, and the red carpet experience, often a highlight reflecting current beauty and health trends in entertainment.

### The Shift to Palms Convention Center

The Palms Convention Center, situated within the La Palm Royal Beach Hotel in Accra, emerged as the primary alternative venue for the 27th TGMA, scheduled for May 9, 2026. While offering a viable solution, Klah noted its limitations, particularly a smaller capacity of approximately 2,500 seats, compared to the Grand Arena's larger capacity. Recreating the elaborate stage designs typically seen at the Grand Arena will require careful planning to adapt to the Palms Convention Center's constraints. The Grand Arena, a modern venue within the Accra International Conference Centre, is known for its versatility and capacity for major events.

### Behind the Grand Arena's Unavailability

Klah maintained confidentiality regarding the specific details of the Grand Arena's unavailability, indicating that the venue's owners had their own plans for the space. He underscored that having a contingency plan is standard practice in major event organisation, ensuring that unforeseen circumstances do not derail the execution of high-profile ceremonies like the Telecel Ghana Music Awards. The move highlights the dynamic challenges faced by event organisers across the continent in securing appropriate venues for large-scale productions.
