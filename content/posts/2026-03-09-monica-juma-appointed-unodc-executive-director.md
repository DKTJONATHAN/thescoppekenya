A Power Play for the Continent This isn't just any appointment; it's strategic, especially coming as the Commission on Narcotic Drugs forum gathers global leaders in Vienna to tackle the ever-evolving challenges of illicit drugs and substance abuse. NACADA's CEO, Dr. Anthony Omerikwa, hit the nail on the head, affirming that Dr. Juma's recognition spotlights Kenya's growing influence in addressing complex global issues. Her extensive experience in governance, security policy, and diplomacy uniquely positions her to steer international efforts, from prevention to enforcement and rehabilitation. It's truly a proud moment for Kenya and the African continent.

So, while Dr. Juma is busy making power moves on the global stage, she's essentially working to make the world, and by extension, our communities, safer. NACADA has expressed full confidence that under her leadership, UNODC will champion evidence-based approaches, strengthening collaboration to protect societies from the harms associated with illicit drugs and crime. Kenya is officially a major player, and Dr. Juma is leading the charge!
