Former Cabinet Secretary Raphael Tuju resurfaced on Monday, March 23, 2026, after a missing person report sparked national concern. A shaken Tuju recounted being pursued by an unmarked vehicle in Karen on Saturday evening, forcing him to abandon his car and seek refuge with a local family before publicly addressing the press today.

## Raphael Tuju's Escape from Unidentified Pursuers

### Close Call on Karen Roads

The incident unfolded on Saturday evening as Tuju drove himself to a scheduled interview at Ramogi FM. His journey took an alarming turn when he noticed he was being followed by a vehicle conspicuously lacking license plates. This detail instantly raised his suspicions, recalling prior police encounters at his home involving unmarked Land Rovers. The pursuit intensified as Tuju approached the Karen Roundabout, where the suspicious vehicle menacingly positioned itself "back-to-back" with his car. Leveraging his intimate familiarity with the Karen suburbs, Tuju executed a swift turn onto Nandi Road, a maneuver that proved critical. The road's layout presented his pursuers with an immediate dilemma: confront oncoming traffic or undertake a lengthy detour, granting Tuju the precious seconds he needed to evade them.

### A Weekend in Hiding

After successfully shaking off his tail, Tuju took the drastic step of abandoning his vehicle by the roadside. He immediately sought shelter with a local family who graciously provided him a safe haven throughout Sunday. During his press briefing, Tuju expressed profound relief, stating his gratitude to be alive, particularly in light of recent reported deaths across the country. The former CS's harrowing experience highlights broader concerns, as even prominent figures can find themselves in precarious situations, a stark reminder of public safety issues.

### Prior Concerns and Official Report

This recent incident was not an isolated concern for the former Jubilee Secretary General. Tuju revealed that he had already filed a formal police report regarding previous instances of being followed in the days leading up to Saturday's dramatic pursuit. He even shared the specific report number with the assembled press, underscoring the premeditated nature of the surveillance he experienced. Tuju remained with the Good Samaritan family until Sunday night, only returning to his residence before making his public appearance on Monday morning.
