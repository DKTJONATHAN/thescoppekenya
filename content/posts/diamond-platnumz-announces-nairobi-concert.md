Diamond Platnumz, the Tanzanian music sensation, has officially announced his highly anticipated concert in Nairobi. The event, scheduled for March 2024, will take place at the iconic Kasarani Stadium.

## What to Expect

The concert promises to be a spectacular affair featuring:

- **Live performances** of all his greatest hits
- **Special guest appearances** from top Kenyan artists
- **State-of-the-art production** with world-class sound and lighting

## Ticket Information

Tickets will go on sale starting February 1st, with prices ranging from KES 2,000 for regular tickets to KES 50,000 for VIP packages.

> "Kenya has always shown me love, and I can't wait to give back with an unforgettable show," Diamond said in a press statement.

The announcement has already sent shockwaves through social media, with fans expressing their excitement using the hashtag #DiamondInNairobi.
