## Gor Mahia Extend Lead with Crucial FKFPL Victory

Nairobi, Kenya - K'Ogalo reigns supreme! Gor Mahia have once again demonstrated their undeniable prowess in the Football Kenya Federation Premier League (FKFPL), successfully extending their lead at the top of the table after a hard-fought 2-1 victory over a spirited Mathare United. The crucial encounter, played on Saturday, February 28, 2026, at Nyayo Stadium, saw the record Kenyan champions dig deep to secure maximum points and solidify their position as formidable title contenders.

The highly anticipated FKFPL clash lived up to its billing, with both sides showcasing determination. Gor Mahia, known for their attacking flair, faced a resilient Mathare United side that proved to be a tricky opponent. Despite the challenge, Gor Mahia's tactical discipline and individual brilliance shone through, ultimately securing a vital win.

### The Battle for the FKFPL Summit

The win pushes Gor Mahia to 49 points from 22 games, establishing a significant nine-point buffer over their closest rivals, AFC Leopards, who currently sit on 40 points. This impressive run underscores Gor Mahia's consistent form and their relentless pursuit of another league title. Mathare United, currently positioned 11th in the league with 26 points from 23 matches, put up a commendable fight, but it was not enough to halt K'Ogalo's charge. The match saw Ghanaian duo Ebenezer Assifuah and Enock Morrison deliver the crucial goals for Gor Mahia, with Morrison converting a late penalty to seal the victory after Dennis Okoth had equalized for Mathare United.
