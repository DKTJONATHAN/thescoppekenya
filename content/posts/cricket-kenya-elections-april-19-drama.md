**The Scoop**

Cheki, mtaa imesikia rada na ni true! April 19, 2026, ndiyo tarehe Cricket Kenya (CK) watafanya national elections. Hii si mchezo, wasee, ni kimeumana kabisa kwa ile chombo ya kriketi. Ronald Bukusi, CEO wa CK, ametuma notice kutoka Ruaraka Sports Club, Nairobi, akiconfirm hii rada. Hii ni fomu mpya ya ku-reclaim ile glory Kenya ilipoteza kwa kriketi, after kufika semis za 2003 World Cup. Lakini, hii journey imekuwa ndefu na imejaa ngori kibao, hadi unasahau ni nini ilikuwa form ya kwanza.

**The Evidence**

Inspecta wetu wame-dig deep, na story hapa si mchezo. Wasee wanajua CK imekuwa na leadership wrangles na mismanagement from way back. Sasa, inatimia,Chairman Manoj Patel na acting Treasurer Kennedy Obuya walishikwa rada mbaya na Sports Disputes Tribunal (SDT) mwezi wa October 2025. Tribunal iliwakatia off kabisa, ikisema hawawezi fanya biashara yoyote ya CK, kuingia ofisi, ama ku-represent feds. Juu ya nini? Kuna zile allegations za financial mismanagement, rada ya 26.7 million shillings ati ilikuwa imepotea, na DCI ilikuwa inawinda!

Kuna story ya October last year, kulikuwa na Special General Meeting (SGM) ya kuchagua vice chairperson mpya. Lakini, Sports Registrar Rose Wasike alikata mawe, akisema ma-county associations yote yalikuwa illegally in office kwa sababu hawakuwa wamefanya elections zao. Hiyo ndio ilifanya hiyo meeting ibume. Hapo ndio sasa Bukusi akasema hawawezi piga kura za national kabla counties hazijamaliza zao. County elections zilikata deadline ya end of January, na more than 30 counties zimefanya zao. Hii ni ile form ya ku-streamline things, vile ICC ilikuwa inataka, ju waliliftt ban ya Kenya June 2023 but rada ya funding ilibaki controlled ju ya hizi ngori za leadership.

Hata hapa kwa kriketi, kiti ya vice chairperson imekuwa vacant tangu May 2025 vile Maina Kamau aliresign. Treasurer Kalpesh Solanki naye alikuwa suspended August 2024 juu ya financial malpractices. Hizo ni vitu zimefanya stakeholders wakose matumaini. Bukusi na wengine kama Pearlyne Omamo, Director Women’s Cricket, wamekuwa kwa ile side ya kutaka mambo iende sawa.

**The Verdict**

Sasa basi, kwa April 19, kuna positions kama chairperson, vice chairperson, treasurer, Director Women’s Cricket na Director County Associations zitakuwa up for grabs. Manoj Patel, yule alikuwa chairman, hajasema kama ataji-elect tena, na Nation Sport hawakumpata comments. Lakini na Tribunal ilishamkata off, kuna wasiwasi kama hata anaweza simama. Hii si elections tu, ni kama rematch ya ngori zote za miaka nne zimekuwa ziki-haunt Cricket Kenya. Ni kuwatch na macho nne kuona kama patapotea ama tutapata viongozi fresh wata-bring back ile 'lost glory'.
