Haki bana, Twitter streets zinawaka moto leo after Jastas Madoya decided to drop some wisdom about golf. Kwanza the way wasee wame-react ni kama Madoya ame-spill the secret sauce ya maisha, not just golf!

**The Scoop**

Manze, Jastas Madoya, huyu msee wa Great Rift Valley Lodge and Golf Resort, is back at the Magical Kenya Open (MKO) 2026, na anafurahia kurudi after missing last year. The 57th edition ya MKO inaanza kutoka February 19 hadi 22 huko Karen Country Club, yenye ni par 70. Huyu ni mmoja wa 144 players from 30 countries wako hapa ku-fight for that Sh62 million prize money yenye inashinda Sunday. Jastas anasema anajiskia *alive* tena ku-showcase skills zake kwa hii global contest.

Kama mmoja wa wale wame-represent Kenya kwa MKO for almost 15 years, Madoya anasema passion yake inatokana na love ya sport, discipline, support from sponsors, na benefits za kucheza golf - like fitness, kujuana na watu wapya, na interaction zenye zime-change life yake for the better. Walae, jana (Thursday), Madoya alimaliza round one na 1-over 71, akitry kujiweka sawa for the cut.

**The Evidence**

Secret yake ya ku-punch tickets to this annual golf showpiece? Simple: great preparations na discipline. Anapractise six days a week, 7-8 hours a day. Hii ndio imemsee through the championship since 2013, minus last year when alimiss out juu preparations zake hazikuwa on point.

But si tu skills ndio anazo, jo. Madoya, anaye-double kama pastor, attributes longevity yake kwa spiritual journey. Kabla ya game, anasoma bible, anapray, anafast na kusikiza gospel songs. Anasema hii routine inam-calm nerves na inamfanya a-stay focused, kwanza in tight contests. Anatumia golf yake ku-impart Christian values kwa wenzake.

Advice yake kwa upcoming players ni ku-cultivate discipline. Anawaambia wa-avoid retrogressive behaviours like drug abuse na crime, coz hizo zitakata short journey yao ya kuwa future stars.

Challenge kubwa kwake ni finances, bana. Golf ni expensive at elite levels, so anawaita sponsors waingie box. Anafurahi ati gaming firm Betika ili-come forward ku-cushion yeye na wachezaji wengine wanne. Na si Betika pekee yao wanashika wasee radani. **The Verdict**

Madoya alikuwa na hope ya ku-survive the axe jana (Ijumaa) na ku-aim for a top 20 finish at least. Wish yake ingine ni ku-net at least seven birdies everyday, kwanza Sunday. Anapea Kenya Golf Open Limited advice, ati wanapaswa ku-create more opportunities for local pros na elite amateurs kucheza international tournaments abroad. Anasema hii itainspire wasee wengi ku-embrace sport na zeal, waki-aim ku-accumulate world ranking points na ku-get exposure. Manze, huyu msee ako serious na game na maisha pia!
