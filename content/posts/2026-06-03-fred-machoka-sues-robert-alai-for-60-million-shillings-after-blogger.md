This stance has drawn significant scrutiny, as the account in question has been used for over a decade to shape public opinion and target high-profile figures. Observers have noted the sudden irony of a man who has built an entire career as a digital provocateur attempting to vanish from his own platform the moment he faces substantial financial liability.

### A Contradictory Defense
The defense strategy relies on the claim that Alai has no connection to the account, despite its long history of activity and influence. To suggest that a profile used for years to influence public discourse belongs to an anonymous stranger is an insult to the intelligence of the public.
