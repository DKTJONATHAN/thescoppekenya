**THE SCOOP**

Mtaa yote imeshika moto na maneno ya Super Bowl LX 2026 Halftime Show ratings. Inspecta wetu wa mtaani wameconfirm, na ukweli umeseti, Bad Bunny, yule msanii mdem kutoka Puerto Rico, alikill show vibaya sana. Ratings zake zimepiga zile za Mzae Kid Rock, aliyekuwa anajaribu kupiga counter-show na Turning Point USA. Hii ni kumaanisha, Benito alikamua!

**THE EVIDENCE**

Namba zimekuja, na hazidanganyi. Bad Bunny alipiga show yake ya Apple Music Super Bowl Halftime Show tarehe nane February, 2026, pale Levi's Stadium, California. Sources zinasema performance yake ilichota wastani wa 128.2 million viewers between 8:15 na 8:30 ET kwa Super Bowl Sunday! Hii namba inamfanya awe na show ya nne kuangaliwa sana kwa historia ya Super Bowl.

Na si hapo tu! Kwa YouTube pekee, Bad Bunny alikuwa na zaidi ya 61 million views, siku mbili baada ya show. Hata kwa social media, show yake iliset record ya 4 billion views ndani ya siku moja! Imagine! Na akiwa hapo, alikuwa na wageni wakali kama Lady Gaga na Ricky Martin, na alichapa ngoma zake mostly kwa Spanish, akionyesha mzinga ya culture yake. Hadi Telemundo, ile channel ya Spanish, ilichota 4.8 million viewers during halftime, ikiset record ya Super Bowl kwa lugha ya Spanish. Baada ya show, streams zake zilipanda 470% kwa US na 210% globally. Hii ni noma!

Kwa upande mwingine, Turning Point USA walijaribu kupiga yao, ile "All-American Halftime Show," na Kid Rock, Brantley Gilbert, Lee Brice, na Gabby Barrett kama main acts. Hii show yao ilikuwa live kwa social media channels kama YouTube na X, na pia kwa conservative networks fulani. Namba za hapa zimeonyesha Kid Rock na team yake walifikisha roughly 5-6.1 million concurrent viewers kwa YouTube. Though TPUSA walijigamba walikuwa na zaidi ya 20 million views, hizo hazikuwa official Nielsen ratings. Wengine hata walimake fun ya Kid Rock wakisema alikuwa analip-sync vibaya. Rais Trump mwenyewe alichoma Bad Bunny akisema show yake ilikuwa "one of the worst, EVER!" lakini hakusema kitu kuhusu ya Kid Rock.

**THE VERDICT**

Ni clear kama maji ya kunywa, Bad Bunny alikuwa mshindi mkubwa wa ratings kwa Super Bowl 2026 Halftime Show. Kid Rock na show yao ya alternative walijitahidi, lakini hawakuweza kushika Bad Bunny, aliyekuwa amewaka moto na performance yake kali na namba za viewers zikiwa juu sana. Bad Bunny alikua ameshikilia mtaa, na Kid Rock alijipata amebaki nyuma. Hii ni proof kwamba Gen Z wako na taste, na hawadanganywi na makelele. Bad Bunny alikill!
