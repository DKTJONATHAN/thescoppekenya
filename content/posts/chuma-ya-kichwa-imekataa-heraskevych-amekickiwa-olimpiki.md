**The Scoop**

Form imechemka huko Milan Cortina Games, na si poa kabisa! Tumenusa hii story sasa hivi, na ni confirmed, mwanaspoti wa Ukraine, Vladyslav Heraskevych, amefungiwa mbaya huko Winter Olympics. Sababu ni gani? Ame-refuse kabisa kuondoa kofia yake ya skeleton, yenye ameweka kama tribute kwa ma-comrade wake wa Ukraine wenye walikufa kwa vita. Hii ni rada imesumbua sana, na ma-bigwig wa IOC wameingia box!

**The Evidence**

Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, Heraskevych alifika huko Olympics na kofia special yenye imewekwa sura za wanaspoti na makocha zaidi ya 20 wa Ukraine, wenye walipoteza maisha yao kwa ile vita na Russia. Msee anaita hii kofia "helmet of memory" ama "helmet of remembrance," na msanii Iryna Protts ndiye alichora.

Sasa, ma-bigwig wa International Olympic Committee (IOC) hawakuwa rada na hiyo style. Sources wanasema walimwambia hii kofia inavunja Rule 50.2 ya Olympic Charter, yenye inakataza "demonstration ama political, religious or racial propaganda" kwa uwanja wa michezo. Walisema wanataka mchezo ibaki neutral, bila mambo ya siasa, na kulinda wanaspoti wasiwekewe pressure.

Ma-IOC walijaribu kutoa offer, eti Heraskevych anaweza vaa hiyo kofia kwa training, lakini si kwa race. Pia walimpea option ya kuvaa black armband ama kuonyesha hiyo kofia after race, lakini yeye akakataa katakata. Ni kama hakuna form ingine alitaka.

IOC President Kirsty Coventry alijaribu kumnasihi huko track kabla ya race, wakashika private talk. Lakini, Heraskevych alisimama ngumu. Coventry alitoka hapo machozi zikimtiririka, akisema ingawa hakuwa na noma na message yake, shida ilikuwa ni mahali alitaka kuonyesha hiyo message. Aisee, si ni noma!

**The Verdict**

Heraskevych ametoa msimamo wake wazi, akisema, "Hii ndio price ya dignity yetu." Yeye anaamini kuwa hii ni kama discrimination, ju wengine wamewahi kutoa msimamo wao huko nyuma bila shida kama hii.

Hata President wa Ukraine, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, ameingilia kati, akipiga vita ma-IOC, akisema sport haifai kuwa na amnesia na inafaa kusaidia kuzuia vita, si kucheza chini ya maji na ma-aggressor. Wanamuona Heraskevych kama champion, hata bila kudunda race. Sasa huyo msee amesema atapeleka case yake huko Court of Arbitration for Sport. Game bado haijaisha!
