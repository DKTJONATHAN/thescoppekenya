**The Scoop**

Wasee wetu wa Harambee Stars, Mohammed Bajaber, amekuwa topic moto sana kule Tanzania kwa mabingwa Simba SC. Rumours zilikuwa zimeshika rada vibaya, zikisema Bajaber amefinywa na injuries hadi Simba wanataka kumtema. Lakini sasa, inspecta wetu wameconfirm Coach Steve Barker ameingia box na kutoa ukweli wote.

**The Evidence**

Bajaber alijoin Simba last August na mzinga wa pesa, akitarajiwa kufanya vitu kubwa. Lakini, story imekuwa different. Amecheza mechi mbili tu tangu aingie. Debut yake ilikuwa December 4, akashona goli na touch yake ya kwanza. Then January 18, akapewa start lakini akapiga busu after 12 minutes tu, muscle injury ikamtoa nje. Hii imewavunja moyo sana mafans wa Simba, na kuseti agenda ya fitness yake kuwa mzinga.

Kulikuwa na reports za kusema Simba walikuwa wanaplan kuregister January signing Inno Loemba mahali pake, huku Kenyan clubs zikiwa zimeweka macho zikiseti kama atarudi. Hii Ndio Ukweli!](/posts/toxic-lyrikali-zaituni-spotify-rada-ukweli), si kila uvumi ni ukweli, na sasa Coach Barker ametoa mwangaza.

"Mohammed ni player mpoa, na ma-player wazuri watakuwa kwa plan zetu kila saa," Barker alisema. "Ako fine. Anapiga tizi na amekuwa full training after kuwa out for two to three weeks." Huyu South African coach, ambaye alichukua usukani January, amejua kutosha wasiwasi.

**The Verdict**

Hii inamaanisha Bajaber bado ako na chance kubwa kutesa kule Simba. Lakini, pressure iko juu yake kurudi full form na adundishe ball bila shida. Mabingwa Simba wanatarajia mengi, hasa baada ya kutoka Champions League, so focus yote iko kwa league. Macho yataelekezwa kwa mechi zao zijazo dhidi ya Tanzania Prisons na Pamba Jiji kuona kama Bajaber ataingia uwanjani. Pia, Harambee Stars coach Benni McCarthy anajua Bajaber anaweza kuwa crucial kwa March international window. Ni time ya Bajaber kujiset na kuthibitisha kuwa bado yuko na form.
