The Tulsa Tempest Zach Bryan's Friday concert in Tulsa, Oklahoma, at H.A. Chapman Stadium was abruptly canceled due to severe weather conditions. The country singer-songwriter expressed his frustration on Instagram, stating he was "forced" to call off the show. He revealed this was his first-ever canceled headlining show in five years of touring, noting he had "pissed off plenty people this morning trying to ‘play anyways'".
