## Christina Applegate's Secret Teenage Infatuation Uncovered Christina Applegate's new memoir, *You With the Sad Eyes*, reveals a long-held secret: she was head-over-heels for Johnny Depp during her teen years. The *Married... With Children* star documented her intense feelings for the actor, even as she became part of his friend group and visited him on set.

Journal Entries Reveal Deep Crush Applegate cracked open her teen journals, providing a candid look at her feelings for Johnny Depp, starting from age 15. "One thing is very clear from the journals: I was in love with Johnny Depp for years," Applegate writes in her book. She recounts visiting him often on the Vancouver set of his cop series, *21 Jump Street*, after guest starring on the show. The actress quickly became a part of Depp's inner circle.

Unrequited Feelings and On-Set Visits Despite an eight-year age difference, Applegate remembers Depp's impeccable conduct toward her, even though her journals plainly expressed her intense affection. Her entries capture a whirl of emotions, shifting from adoration to a sense of cold reality in a matter of weeks. One journal entry specifically details "really strange dreams about Johnny," where only his presence, not the content, was memorable upon waking. The young Applegate wished she could "get through to him," lamenting that she couldn't change who he was.

Depp's High-Profile Romance During this period of Applegate's infatuation, Johnny Depp's dating life was making headlines. His timeline suggests he was romantically involved with his *Edward Scissorhands* co-star, Winona Ryder. While Applegate's heart was set on Depp, his focus was elsewhere in Hollywood's spotlight. Applegate eventually found lasting love, marrying musician Martyn LeNoble in 2013, with whom she shares a daughter, following her divorce from actor Johnathon Schaech in 2007.
