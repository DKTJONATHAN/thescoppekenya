Bongo Flava icon Harmonize has openly addressed swirling rumors, confirming his recent eviction from his luxurious Dar es Salaam mansion due to unpaid rent. The Konde Gang president is now relocating to a new residence in Goba, approximately 30 kilometers from the city, embracing life's challenges publicly.

Harmonize Confirms Eviction and Goba Move Harmonize utilized Instagram to confirm his rent arrears and subsequent relocation to Goba. He openly acknowledged owing house rent, stating, "Yess wadau kama mlivyosikia kwamba nadaiwa kodi ya nyumba… imebidi nifungashe," (Yes stakeholders, as you heard, I owe house rent… I had to pack up). He encouraged fans by adding, "Usirudi nyuma, usijali hata Konde Boy anadaiwa." (Don't hold back, don't worry, even Konde Boy has debts.) This transparent approach resonated with many followers, sparking wide discussion online and feeding into the ongoing narrative of public perception in the entertainment industry.

A Deep Dive into Harmonize's Financial Journey The Bongo Flava star spoke candidly about a massive financial overhaul he has undertaken over the past three years, noting the successful clearance of five major debts totaling over 1.4 billion TZS. These obligations included 600 million TZS for his former label and a 100 million TZS debt to Dr. Jembe, which left him with "barely anything." Harmonize disclosed that his heavy investment in his own label exceeded 1 billion TZS, resulting in a reported "zero" net liquid cash over this period, as he commented, "In 3 years, I have got nothing!! Yani Zero."

Assets and Future Aspirations Despite the recent rent challenges, Harmonize emphasized that his overall net worth remains substantial. He proudly highlighted a luxury car collection valued at over 2 billion TZS, property holdings worth 4 billion TZS, and a music catalog estimated at upwards of $5 million. The artist asserted that selling his assets would render him entirely debt-free, but his current focus is on enhancing his financial literacy and making strategic long-term decisions. Harmonize's net worth in 2026 is estimated at $1.2 million, placing him among the wealthiest East African artists.

Love and Collaborations on the Horizon Harmonize publicly expressed gratitude to his partner, Frida Kajala, for her unwavering support during this transition and for agreeing to move with him to Goba. In a move sparking considerable fan excitement, Harmonize also shared a video clip of a spontaneous encounter with fellow artist Kusah at a local shop during a downpour. This sighting, coupled with Harmonize's praise for Kusah's wife, actress Aunty Ezekiel, and their evident camaraderie, fuels speculation about a potential musical collaboration marking this fresh chapter in Harmonize's career. The timing of their meeting, especially amidst recent warnings from the [Kenya Meteorological Department about heavy rains in Nairobi and Central Highlands](https://zandani.co.ke/article/kenya-meteorological-department-warns-of-heavy-rains-in-nairobi-and-), adds an intriguing layer to this unexpected interaction.
