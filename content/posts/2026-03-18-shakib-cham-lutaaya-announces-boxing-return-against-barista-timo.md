Shakib Cham Lutaaya, husband to Ugandan socialite Zari Hassan, announced his highly anticipated return to the boxing ring against Barista Timo on April 11, 2026. This clash follows his dramatic knockout defeat last year at the MTN Arena Lugogo in Kampala, sparking immense excitement among fans eager for a comeback.

Shakib Cham Lutaaya Set for Ring Comeback

A Redemption Story Unfolds Entrepreneur Shakib Cham Lutaaya confirmed on Instagram that he will face Barista Timo, marking his first fight since a dramatic knockout loss in August 2025. "The battle has been declared! No excuses. No backing down. Shakib Cham vs Barista Timo. April 11, 2026," he declared, signaling his resolute determination. This upcoming bout offers Shakib a crucial opportunity to redefine his boxing narrative after his previous setback.

The Shocking Defeat of 2025 The memory of Shakib's last fight, part of the widely publicized The Kampala Rumble, remains fresh in many minds. On August 30, 2025, at the MTN Arena Lugogo in Kampala, he squared off against Ugandan musician Rickman Manrick. The event, preceded by weeks of intense trash talk and bold predictions, culminated in a stunning second-round knockout when Rickman landed a powerful counterpunch, sending Shakib to the canvas. Clips of the unexpected defeat quickly went viral, igniting widespread discussions online. While many lauded Rickman's surprising boxing prowess, others found humor in Shakib's confident pre-fight declarations turning into a swift loss.

Zari Hassan's Ringside Reaction The knockout was a visibly emotional moment for Zari Hassan, who watched from ringside. Witnesses observed the socialite appearing shaken as medical personnel attended to her husband. She was seen holding her head in disbelief as the fight's dramatic conclusion unfolded before a packed arena. Though Shakib later recovered after receiving medical attention, the incident sparked debates on his future in celebrity boxing.

The Rise of Celebrity Boxing in Uganda Celebrity boxing spectacles, such as The Kampala Rumble, continue to gain immense popularity in Uganda, blending competitive sport with entertainment. These events feature musicians, influencers, and public figures, drawing large audiences eager for the unique blend of athleticism and showbiz. Just as fans keenly follow these exciting matches, the public is also captivated by other significant events, like the [FKF Premier League's thrilling title race](https://zandani.co.ke/article/fkf-premier-league-match-week-21-six-lessons) which offers its own share of dramatic contests.

What Lies Ahead for Shakib? With the April 11 clash fast approaching, Shakib is evidently determined to demonstrate resilience and prove that his prior defeat does not define his journey. The boxing world and his fans will be watching closely to see if he can stage a triumphant comeback against Barista Timo, or if the ring will once again deliver an unexpected drama.
