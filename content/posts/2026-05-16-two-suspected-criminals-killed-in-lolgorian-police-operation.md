Security forces in Trans Mara South Sub-county shot and killed two armed suspects while pursuing a third individual who escaped with gunshot wounds during an intelligence-led operation in Lolgorian on Saturday morning, May 16.

## Lolgorian security crackdown

Police units launched the tactical operation following credible intelligence provided by local residents regarding suspicious activities in the region. Officers intercepted three individuals traveling on a motorcycle, leading to a high-speed chase and a subsequent exchange of gunfire. Two of the suspects succumbed to their injuries at the scene, while the third suspect managed to flee into the surrounding wilderness despite sustaining wounds during the confrontation.

Trans Mara South security teams have established containment measures across the area to prevent the fugitive from escaping the region. Command of the ongoing search operation rests with Eric Telewa, who has directed officers to conduct door-to-door checks and intensify patrols along the main transit routes in Narok. The heavy security presence aims to restore stability in a region that has faced intermittent threats of armed robbery and banditry.

Authorities describe the remaining suspect as a male in his late twenties, likely exhibiting visible bleeding or favoring his limbs due to potential gunshot trauma.
