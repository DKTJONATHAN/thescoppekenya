**The Scoop**
Wasee, tumekuwa chonjo tukinusa madini safi kuhusu preparations za AFCON 2027. Kumbe ma-boss wa CAF wamekuwa Kanairo wakipiga round ku-check venue zetu. Hii ni story serious, juu tushajua Kenya, Uganda na Tanzania ndio wata-host hii continental showpiece. Kwanza kulikuwa na maswali kama tournament itapushwa, lakini CAF president Patrice Motsepe amesema mambo iko shavaa, timetable bado iko set. So, ni kama tuko mboka ya kuharakisha infrastructure upgrades, juu clock inatick, na CAF wako rada na inspections zao.

**The Evidence**
Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, CAF officials walitwanga kazi kwa siku mbili, wakikagua five venues key. Hussein Mohammed, mkuu wa Football Kenya Federation, alithibitisha hii. Delegation ya CAF ilikuwa inalediwa na security chief Dr. Christian Emeruwa na infrastructure mkuu Ms. Stephanie Totokra. Walikuwa wako chonjo waki-evaluate safety, security standards na facility preparedness ya hizi grounds: Talanta Sports City, Nyayo Stadium, Ulinzi Complex, Kasarani Stadium na Police Sacco Stadium.

Sasa, story ni hivi: Talanta Sports City, ile stadium President William Ruto alisema itaitwa Raila Odinga International Stadium, inamaliziwa. Iko over 85% complete na interior installations ndio zimebaki. Kasarani Stadium imefungwa kabisa ku-start Phase II upgrades, ikiwa ni pamoja na ku-overhaul playing surface yote ndio ifike international standards. Pia Nyayo National Stadium inafungwa soon, after World Rugby Sevens Division II tournament kumalizika this weekend, ndio waekee spectator canopy mpya na media centre. Na sio stadium pekee ziko rada. Coach wa Harambee Stars, Benni McCarthy, amesha-share target yake ya Harambee Stars kwa AFCON 2027. So, team pia iko motivated kufanya noma.

**The Verdict**
Hussein Mohammed alifafanua ati hii inspection inaonyesha jinsi FKF na serikali wamejitolea ku-upgrade na ku-modernise football facilities. Hii si tu kwa ajili ya ku-meet continental requirements za tournament, but pia kuacha legacy noma ya development ya sport Kenya hata baada ya 2027.
