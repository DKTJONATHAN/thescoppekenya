Kitutu Central Sub-County Police Commander Kazungu Charo has died following a prolonged illness. Charo succumbed on Friday, April 17, 2026, after being admitted to Mediforte Hospital, marking a significant loss for the National Police Service.

Commander Charo's Final Hours Kazungu Charo reportedly fell ill during the night of Thursday, April 16, 2026. He was subsequently rushed to Mediforte Hospital around 5 AM for urgent treatment, but his condition deteriorated, and he later passed away. This tragic event occurred just weeks after his discharge from Jaramogi Oginga Odinga Referral Hospital in Kisumu, where he had been receiving specialized medical care.

Health Complications and Treatment Sources indicate that Commander Charo had been battling an undisclosed health complication for the past six months. Throughout this period, he frequently received hospital care, undergoing blood transfusions and treatment for severe pelvic and back pain. His regular hospitalizations underscore the intensity of his health struggle leading up to his death.

Postmortem and Funeral Arrangements Following his demise, Charo's body was transferred to the Kisii Teaching and Referral Hospital (KTRH) mortuary in Kisii County for preservation, awaiting a postmortem examination. Plans are now in motion to transport his remains to his home area in Kilifi County, where his family will conduct funeral arrangements. The National Police Service has confirmed that Charo's family has been notified, and preparations are underway to ensure a dignified send-off.

Impact on the National Police Service The death of Commander Charo represents a profound loss for the National Police Service. His passing adds to the number of senior police officers who have died since the beginning of 2026. Just three weeks prior, an OCS at Kibugat Police Station in Kericho County also died under unclear circumstances after being found unresponsive inside a vehicle at Ikonge Market. Such losses highlight the challenges faced by the service and occur amidst a broader context of changes within governmental leadership.
