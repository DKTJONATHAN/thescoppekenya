### Lessons for the Kenyan creator economy

The Kenyan digital landscape is currently defined by hyper-competition. By forming non-competitive alliances, creators can aggregate their fan bases to secure larger deals, share production costs, and maintain a presence even when their individual platforms are experiencing downtime.
