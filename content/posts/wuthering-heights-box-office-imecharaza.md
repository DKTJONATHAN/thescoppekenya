## Scoop Ni Gani?
Ile rada imefika na hatuwezi jifanya hatujaskia! Filamu mpya, "Wuthering Heights" ya director Emerald Fennell, imetishia vibaya kwa box office ya dunia mzima, na kuweka kamba na $83 millioni. Hii ni ile drama ya ma-toxic lovers Catherine Earnshaw na Heathcliff, na Margot Robbie na Jacob Elordi wamei-represent poa sana.

Hii figure imepita zile makadirio ya mwanzo, especially international. Wasafi wa Warner Bros. walikuwa wameeka estimate ya $82 millioni, lakini filamu imetoboa. Hata ka North America haikufikisha ile target yao kabisa, mauzo ya ng'ambo ndio yamefanya kazi.

## Evidence Ni?
Si tuchunguze hizi figures kiasi. Filamu imepiga $38 millioni North America, lakini wenyewe walikuwa wanataka $40 millioni. But sasa, ile pesa imetoka international, $45 millioni, ndio imeshangaza wengi na kusaidia kufikisha global tally ya $83 millioni. Hii R-rated film inaweza ikawa ina-depend sana na ma-fans wa ng'ambo kukaa kwa soko.

Warner Bros. walipiga budget ya $80 millioni ku-produce hii movie, bila kuhesabu mamilioni ya promotion. Na, cha kushangaza, madem ndio walijaza sinema, wakiwa zaidi ya 75% ya crowd over Valentine’s Day weekend.

Hii inamark namba tisa ya Warner Bros. ku-debut namba moja, baada ya ma-winner wa 2025 kama "A Minecraft Movie," "Sinners," "Final Destination Bloodlines" na "Weapons." Kumbuka, Netflix walikuwa wametoa $150 millioni kwa Fennell atengeneze hii filamu kwa streaming, lakini director na Robbie walikataa, wakachagua Warner Bros. na theatrical release na full-scale marketing. Washindani wengine kwenye soko walikuwa Sony’s "GOAT" na $35 millioni domestic na $50.6 millioni global, na "Crime 101" ya Chris Hemsworth na Mark Ruffalo na $16.3 millioni domestic na $28.3 millioni global. "GOAT" imefanya poa na kupata "A" grade kwa CinemaScore, ikionyesha audience walipenda. Meanwhile, "Crime 101" haikufikia expectations na kupata "B" CinemaScore, na budget yake ya $90 millioni, inahitaji ku-perform sana ndio i-justify hiyo price tag.

Kwa ujumla, box office iko 8% mbele ya last year, lakini bado haijafikia ile level ya 2025 Presidents’ Day weekend wakati Marvel’s "Captain America: Brave New World" ilifungua na $100 millioni. Next ni "Scream 7" ya Paramount, Feb 27.

## Verdict Yetu Ni
"Wuthering Heights" imethibitisha kuwa kubet na quality na vision ya director inalipa. Hata kama reviews zimekuwa mixed, na wengine wakisema Fennell ame-twist story sana, bado imetoboa. Margot Robbie na Jacob Elordi wame-prove wako na chembe chembe za ku-attract crowd, especially madem. Warner Bros. wako kwa top game yao, na wanatamba.

Soko ya sinema inaendelea kupanda, na tunasubiri kuona kama "GOAT" itaweza kufuata nyayo za "Elemental" na kuwa hit ya polepole. "Crime 101" inahitaji ku-do extra work ndio i-break even. Lakini kwa sasa, mavibes ni safi kwa "Wuthering Heights."
