Tanzanian music sensation Ibraah has finally clarified the circumstances surrounding his departure from Konde Gang, dispelling long-standing rumours about Diamond Platnumz's involvement. Speaking on Saturday, April 11, 2026, Ibraah revealed Diamond's unexpected supportive role in his journey to becoming an independent artist.

Diamond Platnumz's Unsung Role in Ibraah's Independence Ibraah dismissed widespread speculation that Diamond Platnumz influenced his exit to weaken Konde Gang, offering a starkly different account of their interactions. He explained that public perception is often shaped by fan narratives that do not always reflect the truth of artists' experiences. Instead, Ibraah described Diamond as a supportive figure who intervened during a challenging period in his music career.

A Guiding Hand Through Transition During a time when Ibraah was struggling within Konde Gang, Diamond stepped in to provide assistance that proved instrumental in his transition to independence. The support allowed Ibraah to pursue his music freely, earning directly from his work without the constraints of a record label. He highlighted that these earnings, no matter how modest, are now entirely his own, helping him stay true to his musical aspirations. One of the most significant forms of assistance came in securing legal representation. As Ibraah prepared to leave Konde Gang, Diamond helped him find a lawyer, ensuring a smoother and more structured departure. This invaluable guidance from an established industry figure, who understands the complexities of artists' contracts and the importance of proper legal standing in a highly scrutinised industry, was crucial for Ibraah's autonomy, a situation distinct from instances where legal irregularities can lead to severe consequences for businesses.

Business and the Speculation of a Signing Despite the close association and invaluable support, Ibraah addressed persistent speculation about him joining Diamond's WCB Wasafi label. He unequivocally clarified that the two have never discussed a possible signing. Ibraah maintained that while fans often anticipate such a move, decisions of that nature are purely business-driven and would only happen if and when the timing is right. He concluded that the personal assistance he received from Diamond was arguably more impactful than any label signing, providing him with the guidance he needed to take control of his career independently.
