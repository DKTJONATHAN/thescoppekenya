**The Scoop: Shindano Kubwa la Ma-Game ya Shule**

Hii rada ya Term One championships imeshika moto Nairobi yote, na tumenusa venye ma-team zinajifinya vibaya sana kutafuta hizo tickets za kuomoka hadi next level. Hakuna kulala manze, kila mtaa na ground imeshika! Hii ni ile time ma-mbogi zinaonyeshana nani mshindi halali na nani anacheza kama ako na **kichele** kwa mfuko.

**The Evidence: Ma-Drama na Ma-Form Zimefika!**

Kwa upande wa madem wa basket, Parklands Arya wameingia uwanja wakiwa na rieng ya kurejesha heshima yao. Wako semi-finals Westlands Sub-County na Loresho Secondary School. Arya, walikuwa wamepigwa ngeta na Buruburu Girls na Olympic High miaka kadhaa, sasa wanataka kurudisha taji walipoteza 2017. Waliona national games mara ya mwisho 2016 na wamekuwa wakistruggle tangu coach wao, Godfrey Mwaloma, atoke. Wakisurvive hii semi, wanapiga final na winner wa Aga Khan High School na New Dawn.

Sasa, huku kwa maboys wa basket, Hospital Hill wanakashika na Kabete Vet-Lab, halafu Nairobi School wanapiga New Dawn. Kangemi Secondary School wanachapana na Loresho, alafu Aga Khan wanachoma na Farasi Lane kwa quarter-finals. Game zote zinachezwa Hospital Hill na Nairobi School.

Kwa hockey, champions Mwiki Secondary School na Hospital Hill wanasaka ku-maintain streak yao kwa pool matches zao. Mwiki, walishatuma warning to opponents na ushindi wa nguvu day one, kesho wanapiga Starehe Girls Centre na St George’s Girls kwa Group A matches zao za tatu na nne Highway Secondary School. Walishika title defence na 8-0 dhidi ya Precious Blood Riruta, alafu wakathump Moi Girls Nairobi 7-0. Hii ni ile form ya kuonyesha **top shot*a** ni nani!

Lenana School, Pangani Girls walikuwa na slow start, walipata point moja tu kwa six possible kwa Group B matches zao za kwanza. Sasa wanapiga Buruburu Girls na Ngara Girls kutafuta ushindi. Buruburu na Ngara pia wanapiga Mt Larvena, huku Parklands Arya wakipiga State House Girls na Makongeni Secondary School.

Maboys wa hockey, Hospital Hill wanarenew rivalry na Nairobi School, walio-dethrone last year, kabla ya kupiga Mwiki, ambao walianza poa, wakishinda all Group A matches zao. Nairobi School watacheza na Dr. Ribeiro Parkland,s ambao wanapiga Moi Forces Academy (MFA) kwa game yao ya kwanza.

Na kwa handball huko Kimilili Sub-County, champions wetu wa national na East Africa, St Luke’s Kimilili na Moi Girls Kamusinga (MGK), wameingia uwanja leo. Kimilili wanapiga Kamuside Boys na Kibingei Friends Secondary School kwa Group A. Friends School Kamusinga (FSK) wanabattle for semi-finals dhidi ya Chesamisi High School na Friends Kamasielo kwa Group B. Coach wa Kimilili, Fenweck Opicho, ako na confidence, akisema hawaexpect competition ngumu. "Hii level, hatuhisi tumethreatenwa, lakini tunaingia competition tukiwa determined kufanya best yetu na ku-qualify for county games," Opicho alibonga.

MGK wanapiga Kamasielo na rivals wao wa kitambo Matili kwa Group A, huku St Joseph’s, Kibingei, na St Mary’s Sosio wakichapana kwa Group B. FSK, former national boys’ hockey champions, wako favourited kubeba boys’ trophy.
