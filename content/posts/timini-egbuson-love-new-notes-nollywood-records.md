Huyu Timini Egbuson na Kayode Kasum, ati wanasema sisi hatuoni vile wamepanga hii kitu yote, eh? Wacha tuone.

## Scoop Ni Gani?
So, 'Love and New Notes', hiyo romantic drama yenye Timini Egbuson ameact, imefanya mambo kiasi huwezi amini. The film just dropped and in its opening weekend, imeshika N106 million kwa box office. Hii si mchezo! It’s not just the highest-grossing Nollywood film ya 2026 so far, but pia ndio ya kwanza ku-cross N100 million mark outside ya ile December holiday season yenye huwa na competition kali. Manze, hizo numbers zinasema mambo mingi!

## Evidence Ni?
Wacha nikupatie facts. Hii movie ilitoka during Valentine's weekend, which was a calculated move, right? And kweli, ilichukua N48 million kwa single-day gross, hiyo ndio highest for a 2026 release. Ilipremier February 13, 2026, na ikashika watu wengi kwa cinema chains huko Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, na Accra. Si ati ni luck tu, analysts wanasema ilikuwa strategic release timing, smart marketing, na obviously, Timini Egbuson's commercial appeal inazidi kukua. Kumbuka 2025, alitoa 'Reel Love' ikafungua na N95 million na ikagross N356.82 million total, ikakuwa the fifth highest-grossing Nollywood film ya 2025. So huyu msee si mgeni kwa hizi mambo.

Director Kayode Kasum alishukuru fans kwa social media, akisema, "Thank you, everyone, for the Love you showed the New Notes and us. Love and New Notes opens with N106M, breaking multiple records in just one weekend. Love and New Notes did not just open in cinemas; it kicked the door down. This was only possible because of you.” Hata Timini mwenyewe alishukuru fans, akisema, “This is me saying a big thank you to everyone who has contributed to the success of this film. We go harder.” Story ya film ni kuhusu Adrian Cole, music executive, na Ifeoma Daniels, songwriter, wanareconnect. Cast ina big names kama Ayoola Akinyoola, Toluwani George, Sophie Alakija, na Odunlade Adekola. Watu online wanasema story ni relatable, soundtrack ni fire, na chemistry ya actors iko sawa.
