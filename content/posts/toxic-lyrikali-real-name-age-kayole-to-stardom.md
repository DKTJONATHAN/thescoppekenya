### What's Toxic Lyrikali's Real Name and Age?

Well, honestly, it's the question on everyone's perfectly manicured lips! The man known as Toxic Lyrikali to his legions of adoring fans is, in fact, Timothy Omondi. A rather dashing name, don't you think? And as for his age, Timothy Omondi was born on November 23, 1995, which makes our rising star a vibrant 30 years old as of now.
