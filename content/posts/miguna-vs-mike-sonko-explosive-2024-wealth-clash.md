<h2>Miguna vs Sonko: High-Stakes Defamation Battle Escalates Over Wealth Claims</h2> Nairobi's political figures, Miguna Miguna and Mike Sonko, are engaged in a heated legal battle. Miguna threatens a defamation suit over Sonko's explosive claims regarding his past and family, which followed Miguna's public questioning of Sonko's immense wealth source. <h3>Wealth Questions Ignite Fiery Exchange</h3> The latest chapter in the long-standing animosity between the two firebrands began when Miguna Miguna publicly questioned the origins of former Nairobi Governor Mike Sonko's considerable wealth. This inquiry followed a video posted by Sonko, which appeared to showcase a cabinet filled with stacks of cash. Miguna suggested that Sonko's riches were acquired through "dubious means," specifically alleging involvement in drug smuggling. <h3>Sonko's Personal Attacks</h3> Mike Sonko quickly retaliated against Miguna's accusations, shifting the focus to Miguna's personal history and appearance. Sonko alleged that Miguna was a "notorious criminal" during his youth in Nairobi's Jerusalem estate, claiming that the lawyer and his father were involved in housebreakings to steal television sets and refrigerators. Furthermore, Sonko offered a sensational explanation for Miguna's distinctive white cap, asserting it hides a "huge scar" sustained during a past violent encounter. <h3>Defamation Suit Looms</h3> Through his legal representatives, Miguna has vehemently denied Sonko's claims, labeling them as malicious fabrications. His law firm has sent a demand letter to Sonko, highlighting the distress and damage caused to Miguna's family and professional reputation. The letter explicitly states that Sonko "uttered and published as fact that the undersigned is a criminal, a burglar, a robber, and a violent person with a history of criminality... where he and his father were allegedly notorious criminals." The viral nature of Sonko's posts reportedly led to relatives contacting Miguna to verify the allegations. This legal action marks a significant escalation, moving their dispute from social media into the court system.
