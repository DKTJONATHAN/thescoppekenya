Miguna suggested that Sonko's riches were acquired through "dubious means," specifically alleging involvement in drug smuggling. <h3>Sonko's Personal Attacks</h3> Mike Sonko quickly retaliated against Miguna's accusations, shifting the focus to Miguna's personal history and appearance.
