Achieving this target requires a critical review of the government-to-government oil procurement agreement. By proposing revisions to both the VAT Act and the Road Maintenance Levy, Nyoro is challenging his fellow lawmakers to reconsider taxes they previously supported.

This strategy forces members of the parliamentary caucus to choose between party loyalty and public sentiment. While many representatives typically avoid diverging from the party line, Nyoro’s proposal provides an alternative that aligns with voter demands for cost relief.
