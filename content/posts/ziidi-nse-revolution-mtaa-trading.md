**The Scoop: Ziidi Imefungua Mitaa Njia ya Pesa!**

Kuna venye tumekuwa tukisema mtaa inahitaji form mpya ya kuomoka, na sasa Safaricom wamekuja na jibu ya kuruka! Tumenusa hii story fresh kabisa kutoka kwa ground, na ni hivi: Ziidi trading platform imeteremka kwa Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE) na kubadilisha game yote. Hii si mchezo, wamefungulia almost 38 million M-PESA subscribers njia ya kubuy na kusell shares bila stress ya form-filling mingi na minimum investment za kutisha. Ni kama tu unanunua airtime, unaweza nunua hisa!

**The Evidence: Namba Hazidanganyi, Prezzo Alikata Ribbon!**

Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, tangu Ziidi ianze piloting February 5, deals zimepanda mbaya sana. Tarehe 11 February, kulikuwa na trades 25,773 za equities, na February 12, zilikua 24,357. Hizi namba zimevunja rekodi zote kwa NSE! Kabla ya Ziidi, daily trades zilikuwa zinacheza tu around 4,000-7,800. Hii inamaanisha si spike ya kawaida, ni structural lift-mtaa imeamka!

Prezzo wetu, William Ruto, ndiye alizindua hii kitu deadly pale NSE February 10. Sources zinasema alibonga venye hii platform itafungua milango ya capital markets kwa wale wako bottom ya economic pyramid, aki-democratize wealth. Yaani, sasa kila msee anaweza shika hisa zake na kuomoka. NSE Chairman, Kiprono Kittony, pia alikuwa hapo akisema investment si privilege ya wachache, ni haki ya kila Mkenya.

Safaricom wamecheza kama wao na ku-partner na Kestrel Capital kama broker, na mambo yote inaenda via M-PESA KYC credentials. Hakuna haja ya CDS account ya kibinafsi, Kestrel wanashikilia shares zote kwa omnibus account. Na unajua nini kingine ni noma? Fees za Ziidi ziko around 1.5% per trade, chini ya zile za kawaida za broker ambazo hufika 1.8%-2.5%. Hii inamaanisha unatoa kidogo, unalipa kidogo, na unapata return kubwa. Kuna pia story ya real-time cash settlement, unlike ile T+3 ya kitambo.

Hii si mara ya kwanza Safaricom kupiga makelele kwa financial sector. Walidrop Ziidi Money Market Fund (MMF) January 21, 2025, na by September 2025, AUM ilikuwa KES 15.1bn na 1.15m customers! **The Verdict: Kenya Tunapiga Hatua, Tuomoke Pamoja!**

Sasa basi, Ziidi Trader si tu app, ni mabadiliko ya maisha. Imeanza ku-attract wasee 9 million kwa soko la hisa by 2029, kutoka 200,000 active participants wa kitambo. Hii inamaanisha hata wewe, ukiwa na simu yako, unaweza kuwa part ya hii revolution. Hii platform inakupa data ya real-time, portfolio analytics, na updates za corporate actions, kukufanya uwe investor mwerevu. Yaani, unajitambua na soko!

Mtaa ianze kuzoea hii vibe mpya ya ku-invest na kuomoka. Ziidi imekuja kubadilisha vile tunapiga mboka na kuwekeza. Kenya imefunguka!
