The Directorate of Criminal Investigations (DCI) announced today, Friday, April 3, that the reported disappearance of former Cabinet Secretary Raphael Tuju was a staged and self-orchestrated incident. This conclusion follows extensive investigations, with findings from CCTV footage and witness accounts directly contradicting earlier claims of an abduction.

## DCI Concludes Tuju's Disappearance Was Staged

### Detailed Sequence of Events Uncovered

The DCI's inquiry established that the incident commenced on March 21 at 6:46pm when Tuju’s vehicle departed Entim Sidai Hotel. A brief roadside stop followed, during which the driver of Tuju’s car interacted with the driver of a white Toyota Probox, in what detectives believe was a pre-arranged meeting. The two vehicles subsequently proceeded in different directions, with Tuju’s car captured at 6:52pm heading towards his residence, and the Probox seen at 7:03pm joining the main road towards Ngong.

Further CCTV footage indicated Tuju’s vehicle moving towards Karen Shopping Centre at 7:04pm. The two vehicles converged along Nandi Road, where at 7:11pm the Probox was observed closely trailing Tuju’s vehicle. Shortly thereafter, at 7:12pm, both vehicles were captured heading towards Miotoni Road, described by investigators as a coordinated movement. At 7:17pm, Tuju’s vehicle arrived at Miotoni Lane and parked with hazard lights activated, followed a minute later by the Probox. At 7:18pm, three individuals exited Tuju’s vehicle and entered the Probox, which then departed. The Probox was later captured at approximately 7:38pm heading towards Tuju’s residence, concluding what investigators termed a carefully orchestrated operation.

### Evidence Contradicts Abduction Claims

In a press statement issued on April 3, the DCI asserted that findings from CCTV footage, witness accounts, and other evidence definitively contradicted the initial reports of an abduction. “After a thorough and meticulous inquiry, investigators have established beyond doubt that the alleged disappearance and abduction of Hon Raphael Tuju was in fact a staged and self-orchestrated incident,” the DCI statement read. This conclusion underscores the meticulous nature of the evidence collection and analysis conducted by the Directorate.

### Persons of Interest Identified, Investigations Ongoing

Police have identified several persons of interest believed to have played roles in planning and executing the incident. The DCI confirmed that investigations remain ongoing, and those found culpable will face legal action.
