**The Scoop**

Sasa form ni gani? Inspecta wetu wameconfirm rada ya makubwa. President Ruto na ma-CS wake wamechapisha majina kibao kwa gazeti notice ya juzi, Friday, February 13, 2026. Hapa ni kupanga safu mpya, ama kurudisha wale washikadau wa zamani, kusimamia ma-agency za Dola. Kimeumana!

**The Evidence**

Sources zetu zimeconfirm Ugas Mohamed amerudishwa kama chairperson wa Capital Markets Authority (CMA) for three years. Pia Sally Njambi Mahihu ameshikilia kiti chake kwa Kenya Investment Authority (KenInvest) for the same duration. Hizi ni reappointments, you get? Then John Mbadi, yule wa National Treasury, amewarudisha Elena Natalia Pellegrini, Meshack Moses Kiprono, Gibson Kimani Maina na Michael Bowen kama ma-board members wa CMA.

Gender CS Hannar Wendot amempata Rebecca Ghati Maroa kama non-executive chairperson wa National Government Affirmative Action Fund (NGAAF). Alafu Co-operatives na MSMEs Development CS Wycliffe Oparanya amemrudisha ex-governor Jack Ranguma, sasa ni chairperson wa Sacco Societies Regulatory Authority (SASRA). Huyu jamaa haendi mbali na power manze!

Na huko Agriculture, CS Mutahi Kagwe amemshona Makarios Munanga Makwata kama member mpya wa Kenya Dairy Board. Pia amemrudisha Genesio Mugo kama chairperson na wengine kibao kama Abraham Kiplel Rugut, Mercy Karimi Mbogo, Mike Koross, Fatuma Abdi Haji, Josephine Moraa Omanwa, Beth Wangare Mbuthia, na Kenneth Pkemoi Lomaibong kama members wa hiyo board. Hadi Tourism CS Rebecca Miano amemleta Zippie Metto kwa Tourism Fund.
