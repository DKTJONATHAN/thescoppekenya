April 2026 presents an exciting slate for entertainment enthusiasts across Africa, with highly anticipated film returns and gripping series finales. Audiences can anticipate a diverse array of releases, from star-studded dramas and animated spectacles to intense thrillers and a culturally significant biopic. This month offers something for every discerning viewer.

## Upcoming Films and Series in April 2026

### A24's Divisive Drama Unveils Dark Secrets

A24 delivers one of its most anticipated releases early in April. This drama features Zendaya and Robert Pattinson as a seemingly perfect couple whose Boston wedding week unravels. An alcohol-fuelled confession exposes a violent secret from the bride's past, leading to a major twist that is already dividing audiences, with some appalled and others thrilled. The film is designed to spark arguments.

### Animated Adventure Delivers Visuals, Lacks Cohesion

A new animated film follows Princess Peach, Mario, and Luigi on an intergalactic mission to rescue Rosalina from Bowser Jr. Critics praise the stunning animation but criticise the plot, with the film currently holding 42% on Rotten Tomatoes. It is recommended for its visual spectacle, especially for younger audiences, with expectations for the story moderated.

### Gaten Matarazzo's Unhinged Hulu Comedy

Gaten Matarazzo's first significant post-Stranger Things project arrives on Hulu with considerable buzz. It follows a shy college student and his reckless roommate who accidentally consume an experimental drug. Their attempt to retrieve a pizza from their dorm lobby to counteract the effects transforms into a full, surreal experience. This project promises an unusual comedic journey.

### Anticipated Sequel Expands World with Bloodbath and Intrigue

Picking up immediately after its predecessor, a highly-anticipated sequel widens its world to include a "High Council" of cult-like wealthy families. Kathryn Newton joins the cast as a new character named Faith. Described as a full-on bloodbath that remains playful, this film promises more of the elements that made the original a success for fans.

### Explosive Finale Season for Popular Series Commences

The final season of a popular series drops two episodes on April 8, with weekly releases culminating in a May 20 finale. Homelander has seized control of the United States, pushing Billy Butcher's crew into their most impossible fight yet. Creator Eric Kripke has promised a "gory, epic, moist climax," suggesting an extreme conclusion for a show known for its excess.

### Halle Bailey and Regé-Jean Page Lead Joyful Rom-Com

For a lighter option, Halle Bailey and Regé-Jean Page headline a romantic comedy about a woman who impulsively travels to Italy after a personal tragedy. She ends up posing as the fiancée of a villa owner. The film offers beautiful scenery, two extremely watchable leads, and provides the joyful escapism synonymous with April releases.

### Highly-Anticipated Return Jumps Five Years Forward

The most talked-about return of the year features a five-year time jump, moving characters out of high school and into early adulthood. This shift amplifies the consequences of their teenage choices. Creator Sam Levinson has described Cassie and Nate's marriage as "unforgettable" and called this the best season yet, generating both excitement and concern for devoted viewers.

### Animated Spin-off Expands Beloved Sci-Fi Universe

An animated Netflix spin-off, executive produced by the Duffer Brothers, is set between Seasons 2 and 3 of its parent series. Styled in a mature visual language similar to Arcane, it fills in the Hawkins story between the Snow Ball and Season 3, introducing a new character named Nikki. The series promises a horror-focused expansion of the world, offering essential viewing for fans.

### Tense Survival Thriller Set in Australian Wilderness

An expert climber retreats to the Australian wilderness for solitude but becomes the target of a psychological predator hunting her for sport. Directed by Baltasar Kormákur, known for brutal survival films, this offering promises relentless tension and a physically demanding performance from its lead actress.

### Michael Jackson Biopic Features Nephew in Lead Role

The Michael Jackson biopic arrives with Jaafar Jackson, the King of Pop's nephew, in the lead role. His family describes his performance as a genuine embodiment rather than an imitation of his uncle. Covering Jackson's life from his Jackson 5 breakthrough through his solo career and later controversies, this is poised to be the most culturally significant and debated release of the month.
