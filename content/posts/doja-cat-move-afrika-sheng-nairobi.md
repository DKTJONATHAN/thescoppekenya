### THE SCOOP

Manze, tumenusa hii story ya Doja Cat kurudi mtaa yetu Africa, na huyu msupa wa "Paint The Town Red" amechukua headline ya Global Citizen's Move Afrika tour! Kumbuka ile time Hey Neighbour festival ya 2025 iliharibika? Ilifanya Doja Cat asirudi home South Africa kama alivyopanga. Lakini sasa, Global Citizen wameshamuweka rada na hii Move Afrika tour, na si poa tu, ni deal kubwa yenye inamix entertainment na kuleta dooh kwa long run.

### THE RECEIPTS

Inspecta wetu wameconfirm, receipts ziko wazi! Huyu Grammy winner Doja Cat anajiandaa kurudi continent na kitu deadly. Ataperform kwa BK Arena kule Kigali, Rwanda, tarehe 17 March, alafu anashika njia hadi SunBet Arena Pretoria, South Africa, tarehe 20 March.

Hii Move Afrika, si mchezo! Iko delivered in partnership na pgLang na Kendrick Lamar. Walisema wanataka kujenga pan-African music touring circuit, eti kuleta job mob, kukuza creative industry, na kuongeza investment kwa nchi kibao za Africa, kama Nigeria walivyofanya Lagos hapo awali.

Doja Cat mwenyewe amebonga akisema, "From my South African roots straight to the world stage, we’re bringing energy, purpose, and and real change to Move Afrika in Kigali and Pretoria." Akaongeza, "This isn’t just a tour, it’s a movement that creates jobs and opportunities that last." Anajivunia kuwa part ya kitu yenye inacelebrate creativity ya Africa, invests kwa future yake, na kuonyesha dunia ati Africa haiji, ishakuwa hapa.

Kama kawa, tickets zitatoka in phases, kwanza kwa selected cardholders, alafu fan club, ndio general public kwa Ticketmaster. Alafu, Global Citizen wanapanga kutoa info kuhusu tickets chache za bure, wakipeleka ile model yao ya kumix entertainment na kuadvocate for social impact. Na msidhanie hii Move Afrika ni kitu mpya, imeexpand kadri, na wasanii kama John Legend walishaperform hapo mbeleni. Akimaliza kutudundisha hapa Africa, Doja anaendelea na tour yake Europe na North America, hadi December 1 huko New York City.

### THE VERDICT

Sasa basi, The Nairobi Street Insider tunajiuliza: Hii ni comeback tu ya Doja Cat baada ya kudropiwa festival, ama ni kweli anataka kuleta change yenye amebonga? Tukiona Kendrick Lamar na pgLang wako involved, na story ya job growth na investment, inakaa kama kuna mambo serious inakuja. Sio tu entertainment, ni deal ya kukuza uchumi na talent zetu. Doja Cat anakuja kufanya show, but pia anabeba message ya Africa kwenda mbele. Acha tuone kama hii movement itabamba na kuleta zile jobs na opportunities anabonga. Kwa sasa, tunangojea kuona kama tickets za bure zitatuletea furaha ama ni mchezo wa paka na panya!
