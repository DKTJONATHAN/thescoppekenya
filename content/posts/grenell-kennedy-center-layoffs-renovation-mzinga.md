**The Scoop: Grenell Amecheza Staff na Kuwaacha Kwa Mataa**

Enyewe, mtaa wa Washington D.C. umeshika moto! Tumenusa hii story ya moto sana pale The John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, ati President wao, yule Ric Grenell mwenyewe - msee wa Trump - amewatupia staff barua ya kifo. Eti wanatakikana wajipange juu kuna renovation kubwa inakuja, na hiyo inamaanisha layoffs kibao na "skeletal teams." Imagine, hii Center inataka kufungwa miaka mbili nzima, kuanzia July 2026, eti Trump anataka kuifanya "finest Performing Arts Facility of its kind, anywhere in the World"! Lakini sasa, staff wamekasirika vibaya sana, wanasema ni "stupid and cruel" na inaonekana ni njia ya kuwafukuza kazi kiholela.

**The Evidence: Madharau ya Grenell na Trump Kutoka Mtaa**

Inspecta wetu wameconfirm Grenell alipeleka email akisema departments zitafanya kazi na staff wachache sana, zingine zitazimwa kabisa mpaka Center ifunguke 2028. Sources wanasema hii move ya Grenell na Trump imewashangaza hata wao wenyewe; Grenell mwenyewe hakuwa anajua Trump atafunga Center. Wasee wengi wa staff wananyamaza tu lakini wako na hasira kali, wakiogopa Grenell atawafinya zaidi.

Sasa, mtaa unajua, hii yote imetokea tangu Trump alichukua Kennedy Center last February, akatoa wazee wa zamani na kuweka loyalists wake. Na si hapo tu, December 2025, jina yake iliwekwa kwa building! Hii imefanya wasanii wengi wakatae kuperform hapo, na ticket sales zimeenda chini sana. Wengine wanasema hii "renovation" ni kutoa mende ya Grenell na Trump kutokana na leadership mbovu imeharibu jina ya Center na kuangusha sales. Hata kuna msee mmoja wa zamani wa Kennedy Center alikuwa amepanga protest Novemba 2025 juu ya siasa chafu zilizokuwa zikiendelea hapo. Inaonekana pesa pia ni shida, Center inafanya kazi over budget na sales zimeanguka. Grenell anajaribu kusema leaders wa zamani ndio waliharibu mambo ya pesa, lakini wengi wanahisi ni njama tu ya kisiasa.

**The Verdict: Wasee Wa Kennedy Center Wako Rada na Hawachezwi**

Ni wazi, hii mzinga pale Kennedy Center ni noma sana, na staff wamechoka kuchezwa. Hii si tu renovation, ni kama kubomoa kitu kizima na kujenga upya na siasa chafu. Wasee wanahisi Grenell na Trump wanatumia kisingizio cha renovation kuficha ukweli wa financial struggles na pia kuweka siasa zao mbele. Kennedy Center Arts Workers United, chama cha wafanyakazi, wamesema wako rada na hawakubaliani na hii mipango itakayoathiri maisha ya arts workers. Tunaendelea kuwa rada na hii story, na tutawapa update yoyote itatokea. Kwa sasa, Grenell na Trump wako kwa kiburi, lakini mtaa wa sanaa umekasirika na hawatakubali kuchezwa kirahisi!
