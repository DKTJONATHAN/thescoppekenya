Nigerian beer brand 33 Export recently unveiled a boldly refreshed bottle design in Uyo, Nigeria, during an energetic relaunch event in early 2026. The celebration highlighted the brand's "Taste That Unites" philosophy, fostering camaraderie among guests in the bustling city of Uyo.

33 Export Relaunches with Renewed Vision The event marked the official relaunch of one of Nigeria's most iconic lager brands, introducing its new bottle design. The atmosphere in Uyo was vibrant from the outset, filled with laughter, music, and genuine camaraderie. Friends reconnected and new acquaintances were made, truly bringing the brand's spirit of "Taste That Unites" to life in an authentic and spontaneous manner.

A Night of Unity Guests at the Uyo celebration were treated to a lively lineup of games and fun activities that kept the energy high throughout the evening. A highlight was the "33 Connect Friendship Table," a uniquely designed communal table shaped like the number "33." Attendees gathered around this special table for the "33 Connect Table Toast," raising the newly unveiled 33 Export bottle in a collective gesture of friendship and connection. This moment powerfully embodied the campaign's core message, transforming "Taste That Unites" from a tagline into a tangible, shared experience.

Iyanya Electrifies the Crowd Adding to the electrifying atmosphere, Nigerian music star Iyanya took to the stage. He delivered a high-energy performance of his biggest hits, ensuring the crowd remained captivated and on their feet from start to finish. Across the continent, diverse stories continue to shape the entertainment landscape, from such vibrant musical showcases to ongoing discussions regarding celebrity engagements.

Commitment to Nationwide Connection Abayomi Abidakun, Portfolio Manager at Nigerian Breweries Plc., spoke at the event, emphasizing the brand's ethos. He stated, "The 33 Export Beer brand has always been about authentic connections, friendships and bringing people together, and this event was inspired by that spirit. 'Taste That Unites' is an expression that resonates with the Nigerian spirit of unity, and the choice of Uyo as the location to kickstart this relaunch underscores the brand's commitment to connecting with consumers nationwide." The "33 Connect Party" in Uyo is the inaugural event in a series planned across Nigeria for 2026. The celebration is set to move next to Benin City, Edo State, where 33 Export aims to replicate the same energy, spirit of connection, and great taste for a new audience. Consumers are encouraged to follow @33ExportNigeria on Instagram and engage with the conversation using #TasteThatUnites and #33ConnectParty.
