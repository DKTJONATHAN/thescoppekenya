Protest organizers viewed this unprecedented overreach as a direct affront to sporting integrity and fair play.

Security personnel reportedly intercepted approximately 10,000 of these protest cards. This pre-emptive action by stadium security effectively neutralized the planned visual demonstration, preventing what could have been a unified and widespread symbolic rebuke from the stands during the final moments of the tournament.
