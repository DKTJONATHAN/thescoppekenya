## Iyanii's Virginity Claims Ignite Fiery Dispute with Ex-Girlfriend Diana Kemunto

Kenyan hitmaker Iyanii ignited a firestorm last year claiming he was a 27-year-old virgin, publicly stating he and Diana Kemunto were abstaining until marriage. Now, his former flame has publicly dismissed these pronouncements, insisting he wasn't celibate when they met, throwing the entertainment scene into a frenzy of speculation.

### The Bombshell Revelation

Last year, the music scene was abuzz when "Donjo Maber" hitmaker Iyanii revealed he was a 27-year-old virgin, a claim that immediately polarized fans and critics alike. The charismatic singer further asserted that during his much-publicized relationship with Diana Kemunto, they maintained strict celibacy, intending to wait for marriage. Their union was meticulously documented, even featuring a "relationship contract" designed to navigate their public and private boundaries. This declaration positioned Iyanii as either a paragon of self-discipline or a master of image reinvention, given the often-suggestive themes in his music.

### Kemunto's Candid Counter-Narrative

Following their split in early 2026, Diana Kemunto, Iyanii's ex-girlfriend, recently offered a starkly different account. She candidly confirmed that she was not the one to break his virginity, adding another dramatic twist to the ongoing celebrity saga.
