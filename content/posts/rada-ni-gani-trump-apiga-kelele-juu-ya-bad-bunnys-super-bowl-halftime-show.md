Wasee, mambo imechemka vibaya sana! Just two days after Bad Bunny lit up the Super Bowl LX halftime stage kwa Santa Clara, California, Donald Trump amechapa makelele zake, akisema hiyo show ilikuwa "mzigo mkubwa" na "slap in the face to our country."

Apparently, President Trump alikuwa amefura hasira baada ya performance ya yule mzee wa Puerto Rico Sunday, February 8, 2026, na hakuchelewa kuenda kwa Truth Social kumwaga feelings zake zote kama maji ya mtaro. Alisema show ilikuwa "absolutely terrible, one of the worst, EVER!" na eti "doesn't represent our standards of Success, Creativity, or Excellence." Imagine! Anaongea kama yeye ndiye mtaalam wa burudani sasa!

Trump hakumalizia hapo. Aliguna eti "Nobody understands a word this guy is saying," na akaita dancing "disgusting, especially for young children." Yaani, alikuwa anafura tu bure. Huyu mzee wa siasa alifunga virago vyake akisema, "This 'Show' is just a 'slap in the face' to our Country, which is setting new standards and records every single day." Na usisahau, alikuwa anataka NFL ibadilishe rules mpya ya kickoff. Kwani yeye ndiye commissioner wa NFL sasa?

Bad Bunny alipiga show ya kihistoria, akikuwa wa kwanza Latin artist ku-headline peke yake na aka-bring Lady Gaga na Ricky Martin kama surprise guests. Performance yake ilikuwa imejazwa na utamaduni wa Puerto Rico, kutoka sugarcane fields hadi wedding ceremony live on stage. Watu wengi wamependa sana, wengine wakisema Bad Bunny alihighlight definition pana ya "America" na aka-promote unity. But Trump, you know, yeye huona mambo kivyake.

Hii mambo yote imekuwa ikitrend tangu Bad Bunny atangazwe kama headliner last year, na conservative figures walikuwa tayari wameanza kelele zao. Hadi Turning Point USA, the conservative crew, walikuwa na alternative halftime show yao na Kid Rock. Sasa, Trump ameweka chumvi kwa kidonda. Let's see kama Bad Bunny atajibu hizi vijembe, ama atawaachia tu wakizunguka kama kuku bila kichwa.
