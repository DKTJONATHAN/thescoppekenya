As the 2026 rainy season descends upon Kenya, even our most celebrated personalities are keen on safeguarding their assets from nature's unpredictable temperament. Britam Insurance is stepping forward with essential flood safety guidelines and comprehensive motor vehicle coverage, providing a crucial shield for Kenyans navigating urban deluges and protecting their investments.

## Britam's Comprehensive Cover: A Celebrity Must-Have for 2026

When the skies open up and floodwaters threaten, the instinct to protect a beloved vehicle is strong. Britam Insurance advises a pragmatic approach: if a car becomes stuck in floodwater, switch off the engine and prioritize personal safety by moving to higher ground. The vehicle can always be recovered later, a sentiment echoing across Nairobi's discerning car owners. Documenting the scene with footage, once safety is assured, can significantly streamline the insurance claim process. Britam's Comprehensive Motor Insurance offers extensive protection, covering risks such as accidental damage, theft, fire, third-party liability, and crucially, flood damage as an insured peril. This means if a vehicle is submerged due to heavy rainfall or flash flooding, policyholders have grounds for compensation.

### Swift Solutions: WhatsApp Insurance in Under 10 Minutes

In an age where convenience reigns supreme, Britam has revolutionized the insurance application process, making it accessible through WhatsApp. Imagine your favorite influencer securing their motor insurance policy in less than ten minutes, all from their smartphone. By simply saving the number 0705 100 100 and sending a "Hi," customers can navigate an automated menu, select "Invest with Britam," input their national ID, and follow prompts to provide personal and vehicle details. After accepting terms and conditions and verifying identity with an SMS OTP, the preferred coverage option can be selected, and the initial premium paid via M-Pesa using paybill number 4157051. This streamlined digital approach ensures policy activation without queues or endless paperwork, a perfect fit for a fast-paced Kenyan lifestyle.

### Beyond the Road: Home and Property Protection

While protecting your ride is paramount, Britam also extends its protective umbrella to homes and properties. Their Homeowners Insurance covers residential buildings against risks including storms and floods, offering peace of mind to homeowners when heavy rains cause damage. This policy can also be expanded to cover household contents, theft, and third-party liability for incidents within the property. For those in real estate, the Landlord Insurance package provides compensation for lost income if a rental property becomes temporarily uninhabitable following a flood. It is worth noting that unlike the motor insurance, these property-focused packages are not yet available for WhatsApp application, requiring a visit to the Britam website for sign-up.

### The Future of Claims: AI Motor Assessment

For comprehensive motor insurance customers facing minor vehicle damage, Britam introduces its innovative AI Motor Assessment Service. This AI-powered drive-through claims facility, located at an assessment centre in Nairobi, is designed to fast-track claim settlements. Vehicles undergo inspection and photographic documentation, and a digital claim form is sent directly to the customer's phone. Once submitted, claims are reviewed swiftly, and approved settlements are processed efficiently through bank transfers, mobile money, or repair authorizations at approved garages. This technological leap ensures a quicker recovery process, highlighting how preparedness, combined with efficient services, can make all the difference when the rains inevitably come. While the government addresses larger-scale issues like the Ksh2 billion allocated to compensate victims of protests, individual foresight in securing comprehensive insurance remains key for personal financial resilience.
