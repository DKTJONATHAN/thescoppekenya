### The Scoop

Mtaa mzima inachapa story, na Inspecta wetu wameconfirm! Eric Omondi, yule comedian-turned-activist, amesema hapana mchezo tena, ameshika jembe na kuanza kupiga mchanga ya kwanza kwa ile Sisi Kwa Sisi Rescue Centre yake. Hii ni baada ya ku-finish ile *walk* yake ndefu kutoka Nairobi hadi Mombasa, akichanga doh ya kusaidia wale wanateseka. Trust me, hii ni kitu moto imekuwa ikifuatiliwa na wengi.

### The Evidence

Sources zetu zimesema ground ilikuwa ina-buzz na *heavy machinery* na malori. Yellow tipper truck ziki-drop ma-aggregate, ikionyesha kabisa ujenzi umeanza rasmi. Hii project imefanywa possible na ma-contribution ndogo ndogo kutoka kwa maelfu ya Wakenya, kuonyesha Sisi Kwa Sisi movement ni *real*.

Eric alionekana pale, akisema, "What was once a dream is now becoming a reality." Si mchezo, hii base inataka kuwa *multi-purpose complex* ya kusaidia ma-needs kibao. Plans ziko hivi: kutakuwa na rescue centre kabisa, warehouse ya ku-manage supplies, na ile kitu deadly - free secondary school ya *mabingwa* ambao familia zao haziwezi kulipa fees. Plus, Omondi anataka kuset up ma-ambulance mbili za *emergency response*. Hiyo rescue house, anasema, ni *temporary holding area* tu before wajue venye watasaidia ma-victim *long-term*.

Kama vile tuliona kwa ile stori ya [Ruto's Billboard Ya Ngong Road: Nani Anajichekesha Na Hii Fom?](/posts/rutos-billboard-ngong-road-nani-anajichekesha), Eric pia hapendi jokes kwa development. Anapush goals kubwa na anataka kuona Wakenya wakisaidiana bila kusubiri serikali. Kumbuka, alikaza mwendo 486km (though baadaye ikapunguzwa hadi 370km juu ya KWS warnings kwa *wildlife zones*) kutoka Nairobi hadi Mombasa, akianza February 10 na kumaliza Valentine's Day. Wasanii kama Khaligraph Jones, Jeff Koinange, Churchill, na Daddy Owen walikuwa pale kumu-support mwanzoni. Hii Sisi Kwa Sisi movement imechanga zaidi ya KSh 72 million tangu 2024, si mchezo.

### The Verdict

Hii project ya Eric Omondi ni *big deal* mtaa. Inaonyesha power ya *collective effort* na venye tunaweza *kujisukuma* kama Wakenya. Omondi anaendelea kuita Wakenya waendelee kuchangia, hata kama ni shillingi moja, ili hii base i-maintainiwe ikishaisha. Hata kama bado location kamili haijawekwa wazi, the *vibes* ziko high, na tunaona Omondi akiji-push kufanya *dreams* zake ziwe *reality*. Tunaendelea kupima ground na tutawapa *updates* zote kama kawa!
